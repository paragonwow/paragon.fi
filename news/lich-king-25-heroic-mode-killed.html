The Lich King (25) Heroic Mode killed

Submitted by xenophics on Sat, 27/03/2010 - 08:00

 

EDIT: There have been some rumors floating around how we ignored Infest by canceling and rebuffing hp buffs. This, and rumors of other exploits, are simply false, as you'll be able to see from the upcoming video.

 

The Lich King is now dead in 25-man. Getting the kill took us more than 170 tries, and today we were left with 20/35 tries.

 

 ”This is definitely the most challenging boss in WoW history, and I’ve been there among the first to kill Horsemen in Naxx-40 and whatnot. Everything else up to this point pales in comparison. The encounter is extremely ruthless in the sense that anyone making a mistake will almost instantly lead to a raid-wide wipe. The fight itself requires almost 20-minutes of full-on concentration coupled with very high personal skill-level.

 

Quite simply the perfect end-boss to finnish the expansion with, couldn't have hoped for more. My hat is off to Blizzard on this one. I can't wait to see if this encounter can be outdone in Cataclysm."

-Synti  

 

We would like to thank all the members of our guild for giving their all to make this happen. A special thanks to those who had to sit out on the kill.

EDIT: nerdscreams (YouTube)

 

Congratulation to our lucky warlock Tsukky for winning the raid roll and winning the Invincible mount. We will publish an article featuring our progress on the Lich King, as well as a video a bit later on. A fun fact: Glorenzelg, High-Blade of the Silver Hand dropped. Check out the yellow flavor text on the sword.

 

/Paragon
