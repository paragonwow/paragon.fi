A Competition for a Run in Firelands

Submitted by arx on Wed, 02/05/2012 - 18:43

With Mist of Pandaria still quite a while ahead, we wanted to activate both our raiders and our readers/followers/viewers/fans and introduce a little contest. Have you ever wanted to raid with us? Do you still desire something from previous tier? This is your chance to combine the two! 

For every few weeks from now on we offer you a chance to join us to a raid of your dreams through Firelands. Nine Paragon players will boost you for two hours following your wishes; you will get all the loot you need, all the achievements that are obtainable on a single run and every mount that drops. You will also have a chance to join our TS during the raid. All you have to do is to take part in series our contests, the winner of each getting the prize. 

The first contest will be a quiz about Paragon. By answering correctly to the ten questions, you have a chance of winning the run of your dreams. The winner will be chosen randomly amongst the people with the most correct answers. Do you know everything about family bonds inside Paragon? Have you followed the progression of Paragon since the beginning? Find out how much you know (and learn the correct answers on Saturday)! You can find the questions here:

Answering time ends on Friday at 21:00 CET, so be sure you won't miss your chance! THE TIME TO ANSWER THE QUIZ HAS ENDED. THE WINNER WILL BE PICKED AND CONTACTED SOON.

First boost run will take place on Saturday at around 20:00 CET, with the details communicated with the winner, so make sure you are able to attend in case you win. Winners will be contacted via e-mail, so please remember to add that to the answer template.

Requirements to join:

  • Level 85 
  • Horde 
  • Character on EU servers 

We hope you enjoy getting to know our guild better. Stay tuned for the results on Saturday!
