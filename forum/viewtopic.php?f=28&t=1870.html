There is no real "best DPS spec", they are very well balanced.

Survival appears to generally do slightly higher DPS in 10-man raids, while MM dominates (by a little DPS) in 25-man raiding, though I, personally, use MM for 10-man raids, I find the rotation easier to handle, and the DPS higher, but that is just my personal opinion. But if you want a straightforward answer, MM is the best, that is the result of number crunching I won't go through here, but try out Survival and Marksman, learn what suits you.

As for Marksman stat priority:

Agility > Hit (yes, Agility is over Hit, even if you are not hit capped, but still, go for Hit cap ofc) > Crit > Haste (till you get approximately 1300 (this works for me)) > Mastery.

Survival would be..

Hit (till cap)> Agility > Crit > Haste (don't touch haste in terms of reforging, go crit) > Mastery.

I find BM doing higher DPS than Survival (my opinion), might as well just summarize these:

Hit (cap) > Agility > Crit > Mastery > Haste.
