Kanlo wrote:If this 10 and 25 man dropping the same loot , everyone will be the same gear lvl and the game will be very easy and there will be nothing that everyone wants to fight for and then the game is pointless then people start quitting.


I wouldn't necessarily make the assumption at all that the game will be any easier. If I had to guess, I'd say Blizzard will make gap between Normal mode and Heroic wider (by making Heroics harder).

For me, the "something to fight for" part comes from competing against a lot of other players as a team. If the gear level is the same between the 10-man and 25-man modes, I'm all for it. It will just level the playing field for a lot of people. It will also, I bet, create more 'hardcore' raiders due to a handful of different reasons. Some of these will make the jump from 10-man to 25-man, making the competition even tougher. I like that thought.


For the initial raid content though, I wouldn't mind some type of "attunement" or key quest however. Being able to do 10 different bosses at the same time is something I dislike a lot. More linear progress path is what a lot of players have been wanting for a looong long time. It's fine to have an option or two here and there though, just for flavor.
