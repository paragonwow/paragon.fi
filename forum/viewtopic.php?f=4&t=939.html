Assign some individual people on each Val'kyr, use some kind of marking addon for them (BigWigs has one, for example). Paladins have pretty long stuns, for example. Afterwards you can follow up with the AoE stun(s). If one of the Valks gets too close to the edge while already being immune to stuns, a rogue can still save the day with a Cheap Shot - it's not on the same diminish with usual stuns.

Make sure you have slow sticking to them 24/7. It's absolutely crucial that they don't get to sprint at all.
Synti / Synjin / Vorel

The biggest fail with Val'kyrs, after getting the stun rotation going, is doing too much damage to each Val'kyr. Having nameplates with HP % is really helpful when trying to maximize effective dps. No point aoe'ing if 2 Val'kyrs are at 50% and one at 75% for some reason. If they are going as low as 30-40% your dps is doing it wrong.
