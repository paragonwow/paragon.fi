eserendib wrote:I have seen some pages for info, but i don't find anywhere the cap of hit, haste and mastery.


Hit caps at 8%, which is about 964 rating or so (could be off by a few, can't remember the exact number).
Haste caps when your Cobra Shot starts clipping GCD, which happens at about 7000 rating according to my quick math.
There is no cap for mastery for SV I believe. For MM it caps when you have 100% chance of proccing the extra attack. You cannot get enough of the rating to be even near so don't worry about it.

eserendib wrote:I assume that better stast are agility and critical strike then.


Agility is clearly the best stat, the priority of the secondary stats is somewhat unclear and varies depending on how much you already have and such.

eserendib wrote:And one question about rotation, when i have to use arcane shot, because i don't find any time to cast in more than one time between explosives and cobra shot and not all times.


When all of these are true (assuming you're SV):
  1. You are in risk of overflowing focus (more than 80 is reasonably good estimate)
  2. The target is below 80% hp
  3. Explosive Shot is not ready
  4. Black Arrow is not ready
