From research and questioning, it's only better to gem ArP if you're near Hard Cap, take Potlol for example, he's over 1200. I currently gem Str/Haste because we have a high amount of crit already - let alone I have Herkuml War Token (Which gives me hella haste)

Basically, I'm going to make this short. Don't base your gemming off what people tell you, look at your stats, your Crit rating, ArP rating & Haste rating before anything. As I've said, I gem Str and Str/Haste because when KM Procs, FS is going to be a crit, and I get plenty of crits already. Base gemming off your gear, run constant sims. If I had more ArP I would, honestly, probably gem ArP (I want H Fanged Skull before I did that, as well)

EDIT: Before anyone asks "Why?" - If I were to gem straight ArP I would be losing the 7 sockets I have with Str/Haste, which is 70 haste (With Herkuml War Token it adds up to 224) which is quite a chunk. Now, with the Crit/ArP that will be on the gear already, it would make more sense (To me, what ever happened to theory crafting, btw?) to have H Whispering Fanged Skull because of the extra crit, and and it would benefit more because of all the haste I would be losing.

A good sim is on Elitist Jerks, should be the first one in the main post (Mr. Robot or something, don't remember) you can set your own rotation and priorities.
