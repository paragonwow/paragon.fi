mixer wrote:Current prevalent opinion is that disc is inferior to holy at raid healing

Ten-man environment operates differently from that of twenty-five. All one has is a couple of parties, and the trends and compositions of the disproportionately-publicized 25-mans are often of ill fit.

The shape of Holy's output is tyrannized by Prayer of Healing, and said damage patterns are only provided in limited volume by ten-man encounters. Holy's left as a round peg in face of square holes as far as the eye can see (barring Magmaw, Al'Akir and Sinestra, which are octagons), without even the support of Mana Tide to brute-force through.
