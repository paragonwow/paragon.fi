I´m also getting ready for the hard modes and for the first time I´m really in the dark when it comes to theorycrafting.

The big question is when we replace the Moonwell Chalice and stop pet twisting. I was lucky enough to get the trinket from Spine this week. I´ve been simcrafting myself with both the Chalice and the VPLC and Chalice came out on top as second trinket. Maybe when we get Cunning of the Cruel its time to drop pet twisting. Maybe we have to wait until we have 4-set and Cunning of the Cruel before it outperforms the Chalice?

After giving it some thought I´ve ended up with using 2-set T13 and 2-set T12, pass rest of the tokens to better needing classes since I think that guild progress will benefit most from this. Upgrading to 4-set T13 is just a tiny tiny upgrade (if you look away from the fact that T13 gear obv is better than T12, but then again, I still believe other classes benefit more from their upgrades).

Then there is the matter of what different fights require. Your guild will probably benefit more of a strong Meta with Demonsoul:Felguard and Chalice. Even though your overall dps might drop with a couple of hundred dps I think beeing able to do a ton more dps in burnphases and AoE-phases actually is better for the end result. Your final dps is not all that matters, most fights have a phase that make or break the fight and I rather perfom very well on those. But then there is Ultraxion..... No burn phase, no gimmick, just a good ole dps race where we want to maximize our dps.
´
My conclusion is that there is no final answer to whats best for demo. You simply have to look into what the fight require and specc and gear for that. Also,the length of the fights will have a huge impact on your performance. If the fight end after 4,6,8 etc minutes or very close to those number, dropping pet twisting will be better since that will most likely give you one more meta (unless the RNG gods are against you). My guess is that dropping pet twisting on Ultraxion will be a clear winner since that opens up for a fourth Meta.

The key to solid Demoplay is knowing your fight and my playstyle will most likely vary from fight to fight. It´s frustrating not having a "final answer" to whats best but it´s also very fun and it gives us a challange many classes dont have in this dumbed down game =)

But I would also love to hear the thoughts of Paragons locks thoughts regarding Demonsoul, Chalice and pet twisting. But then, the race is on and I doubt they will give up their secrets and thoughts about every encounter which is understandable. But some thoughts and advice would be highly appreciated!

Moonwell chalice is still incredibly strong for stacking cooldowns for burn phases and such, you might find good use for it in many encounters in dragon soul. Petswapping for demon soul (edit:talking about chaliceless play) _GENERALLY_ isn't worth it. The few conditions I'm usually doing it is if there is some downtime like on some transition phases or adds incoming which needs to be AoE'd.

Incinerate filler is definitely a dps gain on Ultraxion, and with increased dps value on corruption it might be better for multidotting aswell. I haven't personally done or seen any theorycrafting about it, I suppose I'll have quite a bit of time with my 7 days of ban LOL! Incinerate filler definitely is easier to play with and if You decide to go without MWC You'll almost completely remove all need for petswapping. Cataclysm demo is currently at its simplest state :(.

Personally I'm still using MWC on Ultraxion even though simulationcraft results state that using 2 dragon soul trinkets would be ~300 dps gain. I'm haven't bothered to learn how to make my own action lines for simulationcraft, but the sims won't use another meta untill the next Demon soul is ready, Which is a problem with incinerate filler when you get heroism, especially if you have power infusions on top of that. You can have 1 free meta during those first 2 minutes without any penalty. But once you get 4 piece of t13 using chalice loses a lot of its value compared to chaliceless play. I managed to do 41.5k dps on this week's Ultraxion normal kill, I don't have any data to compare it with but I find the result quite positive even though my impending doom proc amount was less than average during those first 2 minutes.

RL had work this week so we first got to Ultraxion hc today. I was not really sure how to approach him gearwise but after reading Jubetos post I decided to go with the MWC after all. My strategy varied alot from try to try, depending on how many impending doom proccs I got. We popped BL at start so when the RNG gods was with me I went for a meta without MWC and then stacked MWC with the third meta. If I didnt get many proccs I lined up the second meta with MWC (decided to stack if MWC was of CD not later than 15 sec after meta was of CD) and I used my second pot for the meta where I had MWC of CD.

This seemed to work extremely well for me, my gear is not that good, still having way to much 378 gear and gear without mastery. I ended up reforging so I had 2k haste for that extra Corruption tick (not really sure if thats best at my current gear level). I did not swap pet after the initial Meta, again not sure if thats optimal but I went with my gutfeeling.

Just like Jubeto I dont know how to make my own lines in Simcraft so I am not really sure this was optimal by any means but my results was really positive aswell with the MWC. I didnt have a single try below 40k (although many tries ended early and cant really be counted as valid) and the actual kill ended up with 42k which seems to be top of the line atleast when looking at worldoflogs. Dont think it was "good" RNG either, our best attempt before the kill, a 10% attempt had me at 44k with meta just coming of cd.

Anyways after doing the first 5 bosses my conclusion is that I will stick to MWC until I have 4-set and both my BiS trinkets.
