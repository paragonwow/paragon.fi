@ttemplario
You have a lot of 10 strength 10 crit gems to accomodate your socket bonuses, most of which are 6 strength. You only need one of these orange gems to make your mea active. I would suggest taking out the others and losing the socket bonus and gemming flat out armor pen in them. The problem you have it, you're currently at 75% (approx) passive armor pen, and you're using NES trinket. This is basically a waste of a trinket, as you really only need about 55% passive armor pen for the NES to make you hard capped at arP. I would suggest changing out the 10 stength 10 crit gems to armor pen gems, putting a sunder or two on the boss, and changing your NES trinket to death's verdict, WFS or if you don't have these, Herkuml war token. This should up your dps quite a lot, as at the moment, NES is costing you a lot of dps.
