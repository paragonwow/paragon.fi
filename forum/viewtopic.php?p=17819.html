It is certainly easier to handle the rotation as a destruction during the tendon phase, but as long as you get meta for each opening, demo should perform better. There are a few things I'd like to point out:

1. Do you have Moonwell Chalice? If you haven't even started the dailies in Molten Front it might be a bit too time consuming to obtain at this point but if you are able to get it in reasonable time, you should certainly get it.

2. Your reforges are a bit off. If you are playing affliction, crit should be better stat than mastery and if you are playing demo, you haven't prioritized mastery enough.

3. You didn't mention pet swapping on your rotation, but that's also something you should do. Preferably you start with felstorm and switch to fel hunter when it's over but if that feels too hard, it is an increase to make the pet swap combined with demon soul just before tendon pops as well.

4. I have also seen a few macros around which basically include the whole tendon rotation, I suggest you to do some research if you feel that such macro could be an answer.

I hope those pointers helped but if there is anything else you would like to ask, feel free to contact me here, in-game or at IRC.

edit: Oh, and to the other poster, hellfire doesn't reduce the cooldown on meta, incinerate/shadowbolt/HoG/Soulfire spamming does.
