I'm the only shadow priest of the guild and I feel it's hard for me to keep up with the rest of the raid on single target fights. Also adds that die fast are a pain in the ass for a shadow priest. The optimum is 2-3 targets you can roll dots on.

One of the problems with checking who does what dps is that apparently recount/skada doesn't show shadowy apparitions right, as spriest already have one pet. Only the spriest herself has the meters right, so you should use those when comparing dps.

I can't say any range of dps what you should have on single target fights but with this gear I'm not even near 19-20k (where rest of the dps seems to be) with lust and all the potions if the fight lasts even a bit longer. I realize I might have a lot to improve but I still have a feeling spriest single target/aoe dps isn't quite where it should be. Feel free to disagree, feedback is welcome.
