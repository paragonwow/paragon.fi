Crimble wrote:My actually question being, would it be worth it to keep the 4 piece (since the fix it's been doing me a lot more good) or should i give the hardcap a go, as it's fairly doable and i believe i'd be able to compensate for the loss of a jumping rejuv.

And is it actually worth the abacus vs. the mana regen, and last but not least, isent glyph of Rapid Rejuv making you spend a fuckload more mana?

The 4t10 bonus is very good. I don't have any numbers handy right now, but suffice to say that you don't really want to drop it for a few stat points. You already have a decent amount of haste, so if you can't comfortably get more, spec to 3/3 CF in balance. Shouldn't be too hard to find talent points for that, as you've opted to forgo revitalize.

As for your second question, in a word, no. Consider that in a 25-man raid, there is always going to be someone without a rejuvenation on, which means that you should never be idle during a normal fight if your mana allows it, with or without the glyph.

In 10-mans the glyph does necessitate casting rejuvenation more often, but consider the alternative; if you're not idling, you're casting some other spell that also costs mana. Rejuvenation isn't that expensive of a spell. Mana-wise, the only real effect comes from the option to pause when running without the glyph.

If you're having mana problems and come by the other solace, it's not a bad choice for regen to run with two of them. That trinket is so ridiculously overbudgeted that it's not even funny. If you don't really need the regen though, stick with more throughput.
