This was during t11 content when there was no moonwell chalice available, If you don't have chalice you should go for the 1993 haste rating (1846 goblin). Keldion wrote:You've got the associations backwards. Tick frequency is affected by haste in a linear fashion at all levels, and thresholds are triggered when the tick frequency would bring the DoT's total duration more than one half-tick below the base duration.

Exactly. And that's why thresholds are mostly irrelevant for single target, since you're always refreshing your DoTs before they reach their final tick.

I say mostly because you do get some advantage from them, mainly because the DoT durations increase at the thresholds, allowing for less frequent refreshes. Also, for Demonology, if you use the last minute refresh on Corruption during Meta, you also get an extra empowered tick. I highly doubt those are reasons to actively pursue them though other than for specific fights that would greatly benefit from this, like Valiona and Ascendant Council, which are already obsolete anyway...
