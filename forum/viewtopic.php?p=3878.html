keymone wrote:so i decided to ask a simple question here: does any of paragon players play without addons at all?


Far as I know, no.

keymone wrote:and what do you think about importance of addons overall? what addons are absolutely musthave for tanks/healers/dps?


The Blizzard standard UI simply doesn't provide information in a clear and concise format. While you can play with it, you'll have to waste part of your concentration on fighting the UI rather than the bosses. Thus, addons that bring the information out in a more easily readable way are beneficial.

Stuff everyone needs:

Some bossmod addon with proper warnings and that can be customized to only show stuff important to you. BigWigs, DBM, DXE or whatever tickles your fancy - we mostly use BigWigs for progress since that's the one I find easiest to add our own custom stuff into.

Raidframes that can show debuffs, aggro warnings and such. Grid is a common choice.

Buff/debuff/cooldown timers. NeedToKnow is a good basic choice.

I think Iiris runs with the least modified UI from our guild and still performs excellent. In my opinion the only "must have" high end raiding addon for all classes/roles is some kind of boss mod. I don't believe we would have been able to get Lich King heroic down even nearly that fast without using Bigwigs, which had the defile/shadowtrap yell and cooldown timers for boss abilities. Of course some encounters are easily doable without one, but Lich King certainly isn't.

In addition there are some addons that are nearly a must depending on the class. Playing my shadow priest without an addon to track my dot timers would be a nightmare. I believe grid/similar addon which can be modified to show debuffs etc is nearly a must for healers.
