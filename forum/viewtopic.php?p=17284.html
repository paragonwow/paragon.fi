thats very unusual. I used MBI and CombustionHelper simultaneously for about 3 weeks to compare the two and test for accuracy and i never had a time where one displayed a different number then the other. I highly prefer Combustionhelper over MBI, But if you're having accuracy issues, i'd say that pretty unusual. I did some searching around the interwebz and didn't find too many people commenting on poor accuracy.

Honestly just use whatever works better for you. If you're having consistent accuracy issues then it's not helping you at all, go with MBI. If it was just a fluke one time thing and you like the extra time Combustionhelper gives.. stick with that.


They both operate in similar ways however MBI doesn't display the damage until the hit actually occurs and the game server confirms that a crit has taken place and that ignite will be applied and a Ignite tick occurs. Thats why there is a lag. CombustionHelper takes the hit straight from your combat log and displays 40% of whatever it hit. for me, Combustionhelper has been highly accurate, ive never seen a incorrect ignite display. (not even a little bit off)

Both have setting that allow you to set thresholds to be more noticeable when you reach a desired ignite level, and both are extremely light weight and easy to operate and understand. It's all down to your preferences.



On a side note - 35k combustion ticks are pretty sweet in my books. i wouldn't be too shook up about it.

Rahmrod,

Keep the 4 set. It will do more damage if you are using combustion correctly.


Here are a few things I noticed that you could easily change for more dps:
  • Bottled Wishes is really bad for fire. See previously Posted BiS trinket list.
  • Herbalism does not provide as strong a buff to dps as other progressions such as Tailoring, Enchanting, Engineering, Alchemy, Inscription and Jewel Crafting (which is not very good in 4.3)
  • Rithak is a very weak weapon choice. Anything other weapon from Dragon Soul of equal or higher item level would do more damage

Without logs there isn't much more I can help you with except link you to sites with good information. Elitist Jerk's has always been a great source of information, but their mage thread is a little bit outdated. Most of the information is accurate except for the mana conservation portions.
