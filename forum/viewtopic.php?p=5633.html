Can't think of anything big really, EJ has a good thread on these aswell. I usually just vanish the sindragosa bombs and shock vortex @ blood princes.

Oh, the one thing I don't see any other rogue doing is using blade flurry wisely on saurfang. If you use your first one straight from the beginning you'll get a second one ready just as blood beasts pop out. Then you target a blood beast and stand so that your flurry hits are on the boss. It's pretty risky as the blood beast can hit you (or the guy you're tricksing) and it's not always guaranteed that your BF hits the right target, but if it works you get like 100k free damage which isn't even worthless as the beasts need to be killed anyway.

The t10 bonus is weak for combat, but it's just enough to make 4t10 BIS. It depends a lot on your luck aswell.
