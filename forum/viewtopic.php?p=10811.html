Keybinds

I don't have a decent screenshot of my UI at hand because I'm not home atm.
The most important things you need are:
Threat colored nameplates - aloft or threatplates (and whatever the other addon for that is)
Rotation cooldowns - eventhorizon
Bigger cooldowns - I just use an extra action bar for this
Raid frames with threat indicators (usually highlighted border) for righteous defence and raid member debuffs. Default raid frames are decent.
Needtoknow and/or Powerauras to track important stuff like tanking cooldowns for easier chaining, other tank's cooldown usage so you know when to use sacrifices correctly and boss abilities. Also warnings for missing RF and seal.
Addons that help you see more information easier. Unitframes and castbar addon for moving stuff where it's easier to see.
And of course some boss mod addon configured so that you can easily see a warning (red flash) for stuff you want to use divine sacrifice (nefa's crackle and magmaw's spew for example)
