It's hard to tell if you did right or wrong with just your dps value. Was it 10m, 25m? did u have spriest or ele shaman? Anyway it's normal that your dps will be higher in shannox pull since you'd be multidotting like hell.

What you can do to improve your dps is to pay attention on your cd/procs. For example:
Rune of Zeth is an amazing trinket for affli lock, it has 50% cd of your demon soul, so it means you will use it once with ds, once alone.
This means your bane of doom will never be "empty", and always charged with some bonus sp. If you will be able to sync your trinkets and your rotation with your procs, you will notice a huge dps gain.
Set auras or tellmewhen or whatever you want to have a better vision of your temp buffs!!

Another tip i can give you is try to reach 2589 haste. This wont change your life, but will boost a bit your UA.

Last but not least....try DEMO!

refreshing corruption with the original spell is the same as with haunt. You just put a corruption with your current amount of spell power that will last till next haunt refresh.
This means that if you manually refresh corruption with 10k SP and after 5 sec you do an haunt with 8k SP, your corruption will count as 8k SP till next refresh. So refreshing it manually just results in a waste of gcd.
