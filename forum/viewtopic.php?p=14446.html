The CDs you should use in your initial burn phase are: Any Trinkets, Arcane Power, Mana gem, Mirrior Images(Using a macro to pop these will do wonders for your dps). It's also very useful to prepot on a fight if you can. It's worth mentioning that you should wait until you 4th stack of AB to pop your CDs. If you are using hero right off(Bale), then you will want to hold flame orb for your regen phase, if it's being held for later(rags), I pop it right after my 4th stack but before I blow my CDs; it should be of CD by the time you are in your regen phase. Another trick to squeeze out some extra dps is use mage ward/mana shield when some raid damage is getting ready to go out. You'll gain some spell power and help your healers out.

I forgot to cover dps on the move in my last reply. Sadly, Arcane isn't very mobile, if you're not casting, you're not doing any damage(meaning no DoTs). Now to address this, try to move as efficient as you can and use PoM Arcane Blast too. Depending how far you need to move and if PoM is on CD, use arcane barrage if your AB is going to fall off.

Btw, how much mana are you maintaining during your regen phase?
