There can be also other reason why your healing done seems to be low. I noticed that some healers have way to long time reaction for dmg and before they will cast their heals target is already topped (so your healing will become full overhealing or even you will interrupt your cast since there will be no need to heal certain target). My healing tactic is maybe not the best, but for sure pretty effective - i'm constantly spamming my heals on target that are prettended to take most dmg at this moment of fight. I'm not waiting untill someone will take dmg, but healing all the time so mostly my targets are topped straight away.

How to predict which targets might require more healing than others? Just think about boss tactics and roles of your raid members - for example spamming melee during ooze explosion on PP, casters with Unchained Magic during Sindragossa encounter or players linked with Pact of Darkfallen by BQL is always very effective and can help to avoid deaths.

With overhealing around 50% i can compete with any other raid healer and mostly i'm on top of meters. If you are affraid that constant spam might cause you oom to fast - remember about rotating mana cd's, ask your druids for innervate, rotate Mana Tide Totem with other resto shamman or eventually ask your priests for Hymn of Hope when you will be in serious troubles. Also as Dygwyn said , be aware of your water shield. Refresh it when you have to move for example, to avoid loosing time for it.
