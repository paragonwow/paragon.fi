Hi there.

I play a demolock myself ;) To give a referance, i have 6283gs and do 15k+ on a boss like Saurfang hc. I am geared for Demo so have lots of spirit gear and going for spirit+Haste when possible.

Dont know how u manage your procs from molten core and decimation, but those are one of the keys to upping your dps.
I use PowerAuras to keep track of when they proc

And then its just making sure your dots are always running.
Immolate is maybe your best dmg, and Corruption procs Molten core, so that needs to be up always. And if u can see that the boss wont live long enough for another Meta, then w8 to use it until the boss is under 35%

And as others have said, try to time your movements with spells like lifetap and corruption.

And on fights where there are adds, try and make sure u hit them at least once while under 35% to proc decimation, for a little more dps.

And should u have a Decimation proc and going back to the boss, im pretty sure u will still have to renew immolate instead of just spamming soulfire until proc is over.


That was just a few pointers, if u are still in doubt take the time to read Diivil's guide that was linked in another post ;)
