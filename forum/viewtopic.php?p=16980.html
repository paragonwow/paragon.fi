Hail,

I've noticed a few things that may help you out.

Glyph of Mirror Images is a dps loss for fire. A small one, but everything counts.

Your reforging should look like this

Haste to 1505 > Crit > Haste > Mastery


Head : HasteRating -> HitRating
Neck : MasteryRating -> HitRating
Shoulders : MasteryRating -> HitRating
Chest : HitRating -> CritRating
Waist : MasteryRating -> HitRating
Legs : MasteryRating -> CritRating
Feet : HasteRating -> CritRating
Wrists : MasteryRating -> CritRating
Hands : HasteRating -> HitRating
Ring1 : HasteRating -> CritRating
Ring2 : HasteRating -> CritRating
Back : MasteryRating -> CritRating
MainHand : HasteRating -> CritRating
Ranged : MasteryRating -> CritRating

Mastery is really bad, especially at higher gear levels. I would suggest reforging to these specifications. I used Wowreforge.com to acquire the values.

I do want to note that with your gear you should be able to do at least 41k dps with full buffs on Ultraxion. Anything lower and there is a problem (or you had bad RNG).

That's all I noticed as far as gear is concerned and I don't have many tips for Ultraxion because it's mainly just stand and nuke. Our guild lusts at the start and has healers press their button so they do not become sated. DPS will get the burst they need at the start and healers get the boost when the damage gets rough at the end. Not sure how well it works in 10m, but it may be worth trying.

Hope it helps,

-Guromin
