As I can understand from your post you decided to go fury. Are you using your cooldowns correctly? (Recklessness and Death Wish) seeing as 2k in a random dungeon is a bit low, and by a bit low I mean you must be doing something wrong.

Your rotation priority is something like this.

Single target

BT
RB (if active of course)
Heroic Strike (If 50 rage or more)
I usually just ignore slams as it is right now to be completely honest, the amount of damage it deals is not worth the amount of rage it costs in it's current state.

What you can do instead of using slam procs is that you can swap into Battle Stance, apply Rend and swap back into Berserker stance quickly.

And basically repeat. Also remember that Berserker Rage can also provide you with an enrage-effect if you struggle with getting enraged.


Multi-Target

Cleave (if 50 rage or more)
Whirlwind
Bloodthirst
Raging Blow

Additional Info

Socketing.

Blue sockets - Str/hit
Yellow sockets - Str/crit
Red sockets - Str


Stats.

27% hit
34%+ crit for optimal Flurry uptime.
I'm seeing average 15% haste, so I'm assuming that's a decent amount to aim for.
26 expertise on both weapons, a few less is acceptable.

Spec.

http://eu.wowarmory.com/talent-calc.xml ... 0000000000

That's the optimal spec atm, though you can swap out the one point in PH(Piercing Houl) and fill out (RI) Rude Interruption.
