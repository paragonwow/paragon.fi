"Haste in general is hard to model accurately as it has somewhat of a stairstep effect (you don't really see significant gain till you gain an extra shot from it, then it shoots up in value), not to mention that in a movement fight you're going to lose some shots affected by haste which reduces its value. So any automated tool will generally overvalue haste and you should take those values with a grain of salt. Settings such as latency, wait time and your current haste values will factor into whether you hit these kind of haste ruts as well."

Source: http://elitistjerks.com/f74/t30710-wotl ... heet/p171/

Also I'm speaking from experience when I say that Oathbinder is a dps increase over H-Distand Land. I had H-Distant Land but when I got Oathbinder, I was able to hard cap ArP with an ArP elixir + food buff and I saw a significant dps increase on every fight in ICC (11/12 HM).
