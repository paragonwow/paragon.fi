Chimera glyph:

CS - SS x2 - AS x2 - SS x2.

You can have 2 arcane shots and 4 steady shots in any order you please, but the above is the most focus effective.

Without:

CS - SS x2 - AS x2 - SS x3.

Aimed Shot MMM procs replace an Arcane Shots.

There's more to it, but that's the basic idea.
