ENCHANTS

*Replacing the 50 int enchant on bracers sounds very questionable to me. I would suggest getting the 50 int bracers enchant instead, unless theres something super special with Shadowpriests, like a haste-softcap breakpoint you can't reach without enchanting for haste?
* Use the Synapse Springs tinker on your gloves. (Increases your intellect by 480 for 10 sec, 1 minute cooldown)
* Use rocket boosts or Plasma shield on belt. (Both theese enchants stacks with the normal enchants. Yeah, amazing!
* Enchant your back with Lightweave Embroidery.
* Enchant your shoulders. If you dont have the Cataclysm one, get the PvP one for Justice Points, or even the Wrath of the Lich King one if you don't wanna spend JP on an enchant.

* I am 96.5% sure haste is better than mastery for Spriests, like it is for most classes and speccs. If that's true for Spriests, get 50 haste instead of 50 mastery on gloves. If it's not true, get 65 mastery on gloves.

GENERAL STUFF

* Get hit capped as close to 17.00% hit as possible.
* Make sure you use Int flask and Int food.
* Remember to PRE-POT. This will increase your dps a lot! 2 pots per boss fight.

VERY MINOR STUFF:

Having a close look at your specc: (Unless this is for a specific boss encounter) Get 1 point in Inner Sanctum instead of Psychic Horror, which is a PvP talent. 2% Movement speed WILL increase your dps, since you don't have the movement speed enchant on boots. (Do they stack anyways?) Plus the armor is nice.

Also, you lack a lot of haste (Int is always better unless you can't reach a breakpoint/softcap?) (Look that up if you haven't aleredy)
I don't have a Spriest, and I never played one, so that's all I can think of for now.
