Deptdint,

When talking in terms of of dps anything that provides an increase at all is important. For instance if you have the ability to change your reforging from 2 over hit cap to hit cap exactly with no decrease in other stat's you should do it. When talking in terms of simulationcraft and wow there is an element of chance involved which is why we run multiple tests. Generally anything that gives you an increase of 100 or a decrease of 100 is significant. By significant I mean that it's not because of RNG (chance), but it was a result of the change you made. You said your damage showed 300 higher. That is a lot. That is very significant. The one that was 300 higher is clearly, the better one.

I just want to point out how significant 300 dps is from one gem. If you were to upgrade a 359 boots to 378 boots that would be about a dps gain of 300. Your doing that with a gem. One item. I can't even begin to describe how much of an impact that has. It's a lot of little increases that add up to big ones.

Just keep in mind anything that changes by 100 is significant.

-Guromin
