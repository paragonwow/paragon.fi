Hello fellow retridin.

It's not mandatory to have 2-3 leather items. I just use them because they are better than some plate pieces for that gear setup (or I just dont have better pieces for them). Leather items usually have less ap than plate items but they have more crit and maybe some other stats so you just have to calculate whether the crit+other stats overcome the strength loss, easiest way for this is just testing different gear setups in program named Rawr. You should also always use conse before exo (even against undead). You should also try to get some gear with less expertise so you could use the expertise glyph (ofc without going too much over the cap!), its more dps than exo glyph.

I hope this helps :)
