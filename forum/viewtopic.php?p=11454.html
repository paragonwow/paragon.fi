Hello Radaz!

Paragon is sponsored by Steelseries. The Steelseries headsets are considered one of the best headsets by many gamers, however, it seemed to me that you were looking for a keyboard/mouse. The Razer mouses have been considered the best in many years. I highly recommend it, but it can be expensive. Personally I use an "Edge 3200" (Search up on google) and recommend this one as well. As a keyboard the Razer steps in again. Razor keyboards have also been considered very good, but unfortunately, these are expensive. You can also buy the wow-dedicated mouse. This mouse has absolutely 100% of what you need in-game, but it can be too nerd-ish in my opinion. Xenophics uses this mouse (at least what I saw during Dreamhack summer 2010). I would not recommend using lots of money on gaming-gear. When everything comes to an end, you don't really need it and i recommend you to buy a cheap (but still good enough) version of something you want. Use these money on an education.
