Hey goldesin,

I'll describe the beginning of the Saurfang HM from my point of view:

Before the fight starts I check that I am at max melee range from boss so I can't get initial aggro when the boss comes active, precast tricks on tank so I can start nuking instantly after fight starts.

I start the fight by using haste potion just before combat starts so I can use another potion later on in combat when we use bloodlust.

Combat starts--
1x SS->SnD
2-3x SS depending on combat potency procs
Alright, my energy is now below 40, boss has 3-4 sunders at this time, I use my berserking and bladeflurry followed by immediate killing spree or one sinister strike if my energy is rising fast due combat potency procs.

After killing spree ends I am most likely very high on energy so I use 1x SS to use some and then I'll use my AR, I use 4-5 cp evi at this point so my SnD wont drop (one sinister strike glyph proc at the beginning at any SS crit pretty much gurantees my first evi to be 5cp).

So my SnD has few seconds left in it, I use sinister strike to get 2cp and use 2x 5cp evisecrates before refreshing SnD (AR on), if I get bad rng with combat potency and zero ss glyph procs I might need to start pooling energy with 3cp after first 5cp evi and use SnD instead.

After that I just use 2-3cp refreshing SnD and eviscerate with 5cp.
---
About the Cd usage.

Atleast in our mainraids and specifically in saurfang the boss dies well before I could have a chance to use 3rd AR, and I always had time to use second AR so far. For me AR is used no more than max 10 seconds later than all other cd.s so I doubt changing the CD rotation would be more efficient, since when using killing spree as early as possible you might get one extra killing spree in the fight.
