You shouldn't be able to top those classes so don't worry. Just Felstorm a bit in advance, land a SoC or two if you can and run away with Hellfire. If you don't get fixated, you can run up to them and Shadowflame and then Hellfire in the middle of them but that's all you really can do every wave without risking it.

You can tell your guild Demo AoE is dependant on 1) Cooldowns and 2) Melee range, neither of which can be consistently used against the Molten Elementals.
