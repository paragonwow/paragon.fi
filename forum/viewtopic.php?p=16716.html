For Warmaster:

Use Glyph of Starsurge instead of Wrath for this and Lunar Eclipse is just as viable as solar with Starfall off CD everytime you enter it.

When in Lunar, prioritize using Moonfire on all targets (with Starsurge off procc/cd). Use Starfall everytime you enter Lunar eclipse if drakes fly by, or there are 2 or more targets up, then use Insect swarm. In Solar, prioritize Sunfire on all targets, then Insect Swarm. Focus on using Starsurge asap when it proccs to reduce Starfall CD, and keep those dots up on every target at all times (Probably not Sapper)
