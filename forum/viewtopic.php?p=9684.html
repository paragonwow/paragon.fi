Rammurg
 
Posts: 1
Zefa wrote:It seems kind of odd to me that Heart of Solace is ahead of Crushing weight...since the str proc should average 285 str and the haste proc on Crushing weight should equal 321....which should mean its completely blowing away Solace

With the 1/5 uptime of Cataclysm proc trinkets, Heart of Solace averages out to 342str assuming it procs as soon as the ICD ends (even if it doesn't, it's very likely to stay ahead on average Strength). Also as the secondary stat is the passive one on Heart of Solace, it can be reforged to a more beneficial one - which in the case of Ret isn't significant, but noteworthy nonetheless. You would need very bad luck with the Solace proc for it to not beat Crushing Weight

Crushing Weight might be the better option in an encounter with low melee uptime, as wasting part of the proc is less significant when the proc is a secondary stat. Although, a Haste proc when not in melee range is wasted (almost) entirely, while a Str proc at least boosts your last resort of Exorcism/Judgement spamming. Bleh, taking the encounter in consideration in trinket choice tends to go a bit too deep into number crunching.. :P
