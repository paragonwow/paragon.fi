Heya!

A short answer: SP

Gemming for haste as disc isn't really necessary. You'll gain enough haste from those shiny epics so you need not gem haste. Personally I'm trying to get as much SP as possible. If there is a 5 or more SP socket bonus you should get it. Use SP/mp5 gems and SP/int (or crit) for blue and yellow sockets. If you have jewelcrafting make sure you get 39 SP gems for red sockets.

A simple 57/14/0 should be your choice when it comes to talents, picking up everything you find the most suited for your playstyle.

Hope this helped. Feel free to drop questions here, on IRC or ingame.

-Nalena
