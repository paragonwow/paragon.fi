*Start of at 75 towards Lunar Eclipse
*Pre-pot!
*Cast Starfall on the pull
*Wrath x1
-(Enter Lunar Eclipse)
* Incarnation: Chosen of Elune + Nature's Vigil Macro
*Moonfire + Sunfire
*Starsurge
*Spam Starfire with Starsurge on proc
-(Losing Eclipse, you are at 0)
*Starfall (When other runs out)
*pop Celestrial Alignment (so the remaining ~15-ish seconds of Incarnation: Chosen of Elune will scale with CA, and the first ~15-ish will scale from Eclipse)
* Moonfire (Auto Sunfire)
* Spam Starfire with Starsurge off CD/proc
* Re dot just before CA runs out, and wrath spam to Lunar Eclipse.
* Starfall (When other runs out)

Few things to note:
When you cast wrath to enter Lunar, make sure to start of with Sunfire, then moonfire else you wont benefit your *Moonfire from Eclipse due to travel time.

*When multidotting, start with dotting all targets with the dot you are in elcipse in.

I am not completely sure about stat priority as I dont play Balance, but I think its something like this: (can confirm on the link from Lappe, ofcourse)

Single target fights you go for hit-cap>int>haste(5273 cap)>crit>mastery

Multidot you go for crit->haste to cap-> mastery.

Could be wrong, though.
