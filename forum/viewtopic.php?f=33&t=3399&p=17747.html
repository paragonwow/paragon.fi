Hi,

Here are some things holding your damage back:

-Glyph of Mirror Image is a DPS loss
-Your Main Hand weapon is not very strong. Try to get Lightning Rod
-Put mastery on your gloves; not haste.
-Change Herbalism to a non-gathering profession

Other than that you can of course read up more on mage theorycrafting. Elitist Jerk's is your go to resource for that so if you aren't familiar or haven't read through the main thread and comments it would be well worth your time.

If you have any specific questions feel free to let me know.

-Guromin
