Pardon the late reply, Colaisgoodlol, Ashylol, and Pheromone,

Colaisgoodlol wrote:For what bosses would you prefer disc or holy?
Our healing setup is - Priest/Pally/Shammy.

Paladin-Shaman is such a direct-heavy combination that the Priest is almost always better off as Holy, barring Deathbringer Saurfang and the Lich King himself.

Ashylol wrote:Why do you have two priests, one of which is 14/57 [Zhinn] and one that is 17/54 [Jhazrun] and why have you Zhinn opted to invest in gem bonuses while Jhazrun has chosen to go full SP?.

I have chosen to go all out haste in order to maximize as many renews in between POM and COH whilst the CD are ticking out on those two abilities.

The specs sport slight variance as I have little love for Prayer of Healing, myself, and as such prefer to spend said spell's points elsewhere.

My gems of choice are also wildly different due to the fact that I'm the primary Discipline Priest at the Lich King, and for that have squeezed out every single point of Power Word: Shield absorption to combat Infest.

Personally, I place relatively little value on Haste rating, as Renew is to me simply something secondary I weave in between my primary cooldowns of Circle of Healing and Prayer of Mending, and the latter two are blissfully ignorant of the caster's Haste. Spell Power, on the other hand, plays a part in everything you cast.

Not only does the absorption component fail to contribute to cleansing Infest as Holy, but won't so much as show up in a Discipline one's Power Word: Shield blanketing.

Thanks for your interest, zuff,

zuff wrote:1) Is there any difference in the way you heal slow steady dmg fights (e.g. IC HM, BQ HM, Twin valks HM) ... versus say raid wide spikey dmg fights (e.g. Freya+3 , Algalon 25, kologorn) where the raid's health is effectively reduced by half immediately?

Do you still just use exclusively POM, COH with (renew + flash heal) as filler spells in both of these scenarios?

You have the carriage before the horse there. What you're looking at is a major shift in encounter design itself that dramatically narrowed the viable parts of the toolbox.

Spells like Prayer of Healing and Flash Heal gather dust BECAUSE they abandoned Ulduar-style raid spikes in favor of arbitrary start-to-finish damage auras (I believe ICC houses half of the encounters with said mechanic in the entire history of the game. Why…!?).

As such, yes, there stands a difference, but one that rarely winds up relevant today.

zuff wrote:2) Also, what are your comments on pre-hotting renew as a holy priest? Do paragon's priests do it when dmg is to be expected (e.g. marrowgar's incoming bladestorm), or do you largely cast renew only when you see the person's health bar drops so that the maximum effect of the intial pre-heal from empowered renew can be realized?

With the way mana works there's little incentive ever to actually hold back a GCD. At times when everyone is stable I simply keep chaining Renew on targets that might find it useful before the end of the duration.

zuff wrote:3) Are there any situations left for which 3 stacks of serendipity + hasted POH remains viable?

Even if Prayer of Healing was to hold niche use, spending three deep talent points to get to waste three GCDs on null spells just to free up less than a second off another doesn't strike as an attractive trade to me.
