Hello there, Adelee,

Adelee wrote:1. Are surge of light and holy word: sanctuary still not viable even after so many improvements they've got?

Surge of Light will never be anything more than a waste of talent points as long as its trigger chance remains at 6%.

HW:Sanctuary is a trap for inexperienced Priest healers who (understandably) trust one of the flashiest spells in the game to be actually worth casting (Cue 90% mistakenly defaulting to Holy for Chimaeron…). The high-profile "buffs" to its numbers and graphics only amplified the allure despite it still being no better off in terms of practical value.

Adelee wrote:2. I feel that the role of discipline in heroic 10 man and 25 man is rather different in the sense that in 25 man, my job is primarily to absorb as much dmg using shields as possible while other classes, e.g. holy pally and holy priests, who are more competent in healing up ppl, heal ppl up. On top of that, I can occasionally throw in a few heals when my AA is up. Since shielding is my main job, mastery is the way to go.

I don't personally distinguish between heals and absorbs. A consumed shield is a successful heal, and the hard numbers take it from there.
Your only task is healing, and for every situation you just pick the tool you expect to serve you best.

Adelee wrote:However in 10 man, there are only 3 healers and it would not be enough to solely depend on the other two healers to heal up the raid. Therefore, my job is a hybrid of helping the 2 other healers heal ppl up and shielding ppl up. Since haste is the most useful stat for heal throughput, I should reforge towards plenty of haste, with a slight focus on mastery.

Haste is indeed the preferred secondary stat of ten-man Discipline, though similarly to the above, it's not such because of perceived healer roles, but simply on grounds of the format's relatively restricted PW:S usage.

Adelee wrote:3. WTB Jhazrun's PoV encounter videos. Like, just your PoV for the entire encounter. :D

I'm working on bringing my hardware up to the level required for recording. My UI, though, is somewhat… eccentric.

Adelee wrote:4. What's that cartoon picture in your forum dp?

See for yourself. It's worth it.
