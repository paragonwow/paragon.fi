TOTAL HIT REQUIRED

Arcane: 17%
TtW/fire: 17%
FFB: 17%
Frost: 17%

HIT FROM TALENTS

Arcane: 6% (Precision+Arcane Focus)

Frost: 3% (Precision)

1% alliance from Heroic Presence
