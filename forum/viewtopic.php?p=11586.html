I don't think gear scaling is going to be a problem, and even if it is I bet they're going to fix that pretty quickly. Most of last tier I felt we were scaling about as well as other classes, maybe even a bit better. They overbuffed a some classes in the last few patches so we aren't really the top melee dps anymore in the current tier. The next tier seems to help with that as our set bonuses are looking pretty damn good right now.

We don't respec for valiona. Boomkins and such usually take care of the shadow realm. I've heard that sub can pretty much stay there for the whole fight but I haven't been there myself so I can't really guarantee anything.

Blade flurry isn't as good as fok spamming with asssassination and it shouldn't even be. Good large scale aoe is pretty much the niche for mutilate whereas combat focuses on taking down small amounts of targets fast. I can't see the black phase being a problem with either spec.
