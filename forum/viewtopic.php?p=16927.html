Baadu,

I've been quite busy this week after being in a small car accident and missing some school, so I apologize for not being as prompt as I like to be. Grats on Dragonwrath :)

As far as Haste VS. Mastery goes, I would first run a Simulation craft test to see what it believes is better for you. I'm guessing that it will think haste is better for you than mastery, as it is for me. I would consider 1505 to be the marker for the lowest your haste rating should be. A lot of top end mages with 1800 haste or more have the ability to reforge it off, but only into mastery. Since haste becomes even better as your gear progresses, it's better to have haste over mastery.

There are a few fights were it may be more ideal to reforge into mastery, but I would only consider this on new progression fights with heavy aoe, or multi-doting (25m Yor'sahj, gunship 2.0, and maybe madness since I haven't gotten there yet).

Yes, your spell crit should be roughly 50% on the boss with FM and Critical Mass active.

-Guromin
