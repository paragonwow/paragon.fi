Thanks,

Well that's a funny story.

I wanted to have some variation as it had been quite a long time since I actually played BM. I specced for the early bosses in the instance, but also wanted to give it a go at Yogg. It turned out to be alright, as I could give the 3% damage buff for people outside the brain room.

My phase3 dps seemed to be at same level with our MM/SV hunters, sometimes even a bit higher. We used 4 hunters distracting the adds with shadow beacon, so there was a lot of time when I could not dps the boss at all, but my pet could go all out.

My spec was not the optimal BM spec as I didn't have imp tracking (which is useless on that fight). The reason I didn't have imp arcane was that I've got ArP gems on my gear (did not use it on my rotation).

Optimal choice or not, I don't know. But what I do know is that it was quite fun to play (and probably less stressful than other specs) :>
