Refer to the spreadsheet in my previous posts here. The value of expertise differs so much depending on how good your gear is. For me, now that I'm close to the arp cap, expertise is the 2nd highest in terms of ep, ranging from 2,3-2,5 while armor pen is around 2,8.

There are no magic numbers for any stat that are required for you to "perform well". Your dps doesn't suddendly drop to 0 if you don't have any expertise ect. from your gear.
