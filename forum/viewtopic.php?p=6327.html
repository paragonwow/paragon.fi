Rupture should nearly always be better, weapons are about 100+ dps each going from 264->277. Get rupture up asap while keeping snd up. You don't need to use over 4 point snd:s with high rupture. As long as it's still on when you have the combo points to refresh it.

Lucky crits play a big part in rogue dps as damage mostly comes from autoattacks and poisons. There's not really much you can do if you understand how the rotations should work (which is by no means hard). Otherwise it's just using the right potions at the right time and trying to time your cooldowns with possible damage buffs.
