What I basicly did was:

get haste cap: 856. SInce you have 2 druids, i would be using glyph of rapid rejuve.

5x rejuve, 1x wg.. regrowth on tank... In some cases I would throw up lifeblooms, not that much anymore. and Im alsways hotting up many tombs with regrowth since other healers did rejuve / renew. I never throw wild growth at the tank since melee is to far to get it aswell, most of the time i threw it at range and other at melee. Keep rejuving in a good order so you dont lose any uptime. Example: grp 4+5

ANyway thats just how i did it :P Nourish I dont use much, its after swiftmend and my i shit button my help button in case of need.

Also you do want to get the 4set if you dont have access on all 277 items yet. Its a nice upgrade.

Suppa pretty much said it. You shouldn't focus on healing the tank too much unless you're meant to. I usually keep a rejuvenation and maybe a regrowth up on the tank, so that I can swiftmend in an emergency. Nowadays I primarily use lifebloom for the bloom effect, but it takes some guessing to predict when it's going to be useful. Raid healing is pretty much your usual wg/reju spam with an occasional spot heal.

You seem to be in moonkin gear now, but shoot for 856 haste, and when you get there, drop CF from your spec. 4t10 is also very good, no point in skipping it when you can afford it.
