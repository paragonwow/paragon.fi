Tiny Abo is a waste, like cap just said. I picked up one for Cataclysm though, just because of the fact that it scales somewhat better than the trinkets with ratings on them.

You most definitely do not want to pop ERW at the start of the fight. You've got Merciless Combat, which means you're gonna do +12% extra damage with Obliterate, Frost Strike etc below 35%. When your procs/trinkets/whatever line up below 35% then _that_ is the time you want to use it. It gives you significantly more damage for ERW.

Other than that, if you're applying your diseases only once, there's a good chance that your Frost Fever isn't getting the +10% damage bonus. You need to have someone's Frost Fever on the boss as you slap IT on it, otherwise it does not gain the extra damage.


Also, one last extremely important tidbit: NEVER allow your runes to recharge past the rune grace period (RGP). I'm not sure if your priority system takes that into account. It's a significant blunder if you do and the runes aren't charging up.
