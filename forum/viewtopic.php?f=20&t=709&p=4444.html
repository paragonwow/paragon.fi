Well timed macro is simple you just need to add one more line, like this for example:


#showtooltip Divine Sacrifice
/cast Divine Sacrifice
/raid === DIVINE SACRIFICE USED ON RAID !! ===
/in 10 /raid Divine Sacrifice Faded

Considering that Divine Sacrifice lasts for 10 seconds. Of course you can change time to whatever you want.
