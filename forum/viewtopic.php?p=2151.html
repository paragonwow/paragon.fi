You have to calculate the rDPS gains yourself. I think someone calculated in EJ that the average caster gains 1.5 dps / 1 spell power and that's what I'm using.

Simplified example:
Your pDPS profile does 100 DPS

Your rDPS profile does 90 DPS but has 100 SP more
You have 7 casters in your raid, including yourself
7*1,5*100*,1 = 105 DPS
(number of casters * DPS for every 1 SP the casters get * SP gain from demonic pact)
rDPS profile would yield more DPS in this example.



(You can see how much buffed spell power you have from simulation craft results)


1.5 dps / 1 spell power might not be entirely accurate but it should give you good enough idea. The exact thing to do would be to calculate how much DPS everyone in your raid gains from 1 SP and then use the avarage of those numbers but that's just too much work and still wouldn't be accurate because the people in your raid change.
