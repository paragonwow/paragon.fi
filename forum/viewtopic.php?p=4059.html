I posted some pve questions - but I thought I could answer this question about PvP. Although I'm not the best/most experienced pvp warlock by far, I want to try to contribute.

The 2 pvp specs available are destro and affliction. If you're curious about spec specifics and glyphs the two best warlocks on my server (and arguably in the entire Ruin BG) are Bandijr and Imbad. Sometimes they have a pve spec (for the occasional raid). (Server - Magtheridon, U.S.)

My experience: Which one you choose depends on your teams and your partners. I'm currently messing around in 2's with a great shadowpriest friend of mine so I use affliction. I believe destro is less viable in 2s but potentially more viable in 3s and 5s depending on the comp (there are however 3s comps that use affliction warlocks).

There is also a gear component - affliction+healer will be slightly more durable than destro+healer at low gear/experience levels (you have to really be familiar with your portals, stuns and fears as destro). Finally, as I hinted to above, destro requires more experience to play at higher levels - you often see experienced warlocks with destro pvp builds (imbad and bandijr) - but both of these guys are incredibly good.

If you're gonna do BGs, destro is fine - and a bit more fun. Even a well geared affliction warlock is less effective at quick finishes (its more of a cc+outlast build). And nether proc's are great for going head to head with 2 or more casters of the same magic school.

Hope this helps a bit.
