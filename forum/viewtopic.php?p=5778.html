The DPS you do in that fight is entirely dependant on how the other people in your raid do. Taking a brief look at that, it's honestly not pretty at all. That's my first impression anyway. I don't want to browse WoL logs thoroughly with a microscope; I much prefer WMO for everything.

You should just really focus on people not even doing damage to PP. Simply idling and dodging the goo/gas, handling the slimes without screwing up. Just take it to P2 and don't do any damage. Keep doing it for some tries, hopefully hitting enrage before anyone screws up. After that goes _damn smooth_, push it further. Should make it easier to learn.

You can easily save your AoTD for a bad green slime - the ghouls from Army reduce the blast damage as well, as long as you remember to pet attack it. Or use it twice during the fight.


It's slighly based on assumptions, but it looked like your raid has some survivability issues. Just focus working on those and then worry about DPS. Also have everyone really go all out on the slimes DPS-wise - that's extremely important on the first kills.
