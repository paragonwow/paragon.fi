Gothen,

Your damage is sufficient on Yor'sahj 25H. The only reason the other mages are ahead of you is because their combustions did more damage. If you consider how many black adds you got on the kill this week strong combustions could have put you in the 70k-90k dps range.

If you have not already read my Ignite Guide I would recommend doing so.

Doing lots of damage on Yor'sahj is dependent on two things; opportunity and preparation. You need to be prepared and set yourself up to accept good rng.

If Blastwave alone isn't giving you an impact proc you should wait on using it. Use it when all the adds are crowded around the boss so it nearly guarantee's a proc. Do not use it while an ignite is on the target unless you have already cast Combustion. Anytime you use an instant cast that will not cause a large ignite you either munch the ignite on the target or prevent that ignite from being able to stack.

When it comes to the adds I wouldn't be worried about timing trinkets up with combustion unless they are use-based trinkets, however, do use your Engineering Gloves right before a Pyroblast! you are hoping crits. The extra intellect makes it more likely to crit and it will hit harder.

Worst case scenario I would hard-cast a pyroblast and hope it crits to spread ignite. If that crits, put LB up if it isn't already and then use Combustion. Even a small ignite is enough for a big combustion.

I would recommend watching some video's of mages doing the fight to get a better feel for the timing of spells and maybe learn a few things I haven't mentioned here.

-Guromin

Hey Gothen, Quick suggestion if you're having timing issues on Heroic Yorsahj.
