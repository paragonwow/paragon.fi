Blayde,

As far as Zon'ozz is concered you are being beat primarily because others are switching back to the boss sooner than you. I'm not in your guild to know if that's good or bad, but either way they much more damage on the boss than you do. This link will show you the damage done to the boss. Check yourself and another player to compare when and for how long you are attacking the boss.

http://www.worldoflogs.com/reports/rt-y ... 4&target=1

If you are clipping Living Bomb you are losing a lot of damage. It's okay if it's not on the boss for a GCD while you cast a fireball. It's more important that you let it explode first. Since you posted it I assume you already know that :)

If you have not read my Ignite Guide for Combustion I would advise that you check that out because it will help to improve your combustion damage.

It's difficult to come up with any other comments (especially while World of Logs is in Peak Hours), but if you have more specific questions I'd be happy to assist. Maybe looking at a particular fight of yours, or a question on how another mage was able to succeed so well. You are doing really well. I would advise you to start looking at others' logs (if you don't already) to find the little things in fights that will make you better. I also like to watch kill videos of lots of fights as well.

Hit me up with any other questions,

-Guromin
