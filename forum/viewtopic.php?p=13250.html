You should finish gemming, enchanting, and runeforging your gear.
Next reforge to make sure you are hit capped (you are at 7.5%, should be at 8%).

You shouldn't use Blood Boil as Frost, so you don't need the Blood Boil Glyph.

As for which presence, you should be using Unholy Presence. I'm not sure why you would be getting more DPS in Frost, unless its during trash/AoE situations, at which point Frost may come out as better DPS, but for boss fights, you should be getting higher DPS in Unholy. So if you aren't, I'd say work on your ability usage and making sure you have the correct priority for when to use abilities.

Good luck with your new guild.
