From my understanding, when you use meta it takes a "snapshot" of your stats right before entering meta. It keeps these stats for the whole duration of meta. So if one had say 17 seconds left until meta came off CD you could pop the Moonwell Chalice as long as you used meta before the 20 seconds of moonwell chalice ends, theoretically the effects of the Moonwell Chalice would last another 36 seconds while in meta form. I don't have my moonwell chalice yet and haven't personally tested this. But I do intend too, as this could be a very helpful DPS increase. Though if someone does have a Chalice and does test this, please let me know the results.
