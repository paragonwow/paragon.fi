Sanalpha,

I time Synapse springs with Power torrent, my other trinket, when I am about to use combustion, during periods where the enemy takes increased damage, or when I intend to put Living Bomb on multiple targets. I re-rolled engineering because it gives you the ability to choose when you use it so you can time it with other spells and intelligent increase your chances of good rng and ultimately increase your damage output.

Anything worth casting in a single target rotation is worth casting over scorch in a mobile rotation. Hot streak procs, refreshing Living Bomb, Mirror Images, Flame Orb, and Blast wave have the same priority order as single target. If you can use one over scorch, do so. I've seen some research that states Blast Wave is worth casting on Cool Down if there are two or more targets present, so if that is the case, use it.

Never use scorch to "conserve" mana. It's not needed anymore and the only time I would ever use scorch is if I am 100% out of mana (like on Yor'sahj with a blue globule) or if I have managed my mana poorly on Madness of Deathwing. Scorch weaving is obsolete it's something I am removing from the EJ's thread.

-Guromin
