Okay, so this one has been a headbanger for my guild. Lucky for me, I have a mature guild of peeps who keep coming back for more.

That being said, before the patch, we were working our DPS and heals into doing the right thing. Got that down pat, then the patch hits. Now, our extremely competent tanks are dropping like flies, and it seems no matter how good (or the number) of healers, nothing seems to be able to stop at least one from dying after the p1-p2 transition. I even went from my hunter to my drood in the latter attempts to assure heals were on-par. Truthfully, defile and valkyr's are now old-school and it seems keeping the tanks alive is our new enemy.

With alot of tank mitigators now gone by the wayside, I can use any advice which I can give my tanks about surviving this encounter, as even in the log reviews, I'm not finding any avenue not taken.
