We've had *alot* of P1 rag hardmode experience.. and from what I can tell;

-prepot, aimed at 2sec till pull, full CD burn, aimed spam (2x steady then aimed, usually the last few sec of RF I do 2x steady, aimed, 1steady, aimed to take advantage of the last little bit of haste),
-do 2 steadies without RF to refresh ISS, readiness, 2nd RF, continue aimed spam.
-generally the last 5 seconds of this the boss hits 89%, and I use the last bit of focus from rapid recup for getting Srs and Chim fired.

The above yeilded more dps then the following:

-prepot, aimed at 2sec till pull, srs, CD burn, chimera, 2xsteady, aimed spam.
-fire Chimera only when Srs has 6-5 sec left (due to travel time to rag)
-attempt to setup a situation where I fire chimera, readiness, chimera, RF, continue aimed spam


----------------------------------

The dps difference is on the order of around 4k for me -- but keep in mind the first 10% we're talking 45k vs 40-41k .. so around a 10% loss.
There is of course issues where you get punted, have to move for traps, etc.. but on those lucky times where I barely have to move, this has definitely been the way to go.

Note: if your healers are struggling, you can save readiness, and deterrence the first trap detonation, THEN readiness and RF/aimed spam continue.. However, usually you'll lose out of 10seconds of CA + RF time. -- the dps difference for me here is 45k vs 38k at boss 89% hp. -- You would then readiness, RF again, due to NOT being in CA, you would priortize Chimera over aimed.... and you'd have another deterrence available for the 2nd trap detonation in P1 (hardmode)

Good luck

The following has been working the best for me, been trying to do a regular Aimed Shot "focus dump" rotation, using two AI's per cycle and variations hereof, among other things.

Anyway, here goes:

Prepot.
Apply Serpent Sting (precasting Aimed is an option, but I would not recommend it, because it messes up the next step)
Chimera Shot
Readiness / Cooldown trinkets / Rapid Fire / Call of the Wild / Everything.
Chimera Shot again.

Now simply keep up the ISS buff, and get as many AI's off as possible, sounds easy enough, but it is a little tricky to deal with, I usually do the following when the GCD of the Chimera Shot has ended:

Steady Shot x2
Aimed Shot
Steady Shot x1 (If you got the focus to then fire an AI)
Aimed Shot

Rinse and repeat, any Steady Shot that does not bring you further up the focus bar, and / or helps you refresh ISS -just in time- is wasted.

Try and keep these guidelines in mind, and practice. ;)
