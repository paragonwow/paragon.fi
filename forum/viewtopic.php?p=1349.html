1 spirit is worth 1.1 spirit (kings).
1.1 * 0.2 = 0.22 sp from glyph of life tap
1.1 * (1.30*.30) = 0.429 sp from fel armor.
1 spirit = .649 sp

10*.649 + 5 + 12 = 23.49 sp from the gem in gloves and spirit boosts life tap so it's clearly better.
