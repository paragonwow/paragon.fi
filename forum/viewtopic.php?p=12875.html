We did felstorm followed by 1 or 2 seed of corruptions (depending on haste and if you think you can get 2 off before the molten elemental hits you) followed by hellfire while running away. Makes sure you put you felguard to attack one of the molten elementals right when they active. That way it will follow them at least a little bit when they start moving.

We prefered to have felstorm already running when they activate instead of charging in and starting felstorm a second later. The whole point for us was to kill them as fast as possible.
