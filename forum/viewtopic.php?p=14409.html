They are right to say you can do more than 22k dps sustained with your gear. I would say hitting 40k would be pushing it quite a bit, maybe if you pop all your cd's and get good procs from trinks or w/e else. But 10 mans vs 25 mans also must be taken into account as well. However 22k dps in Firelands in 10 man while working on heroic Baleroc seems kind of low from my point of view. Although since I am not a mage myself and neither do I play one extensively at all, I could not go into depth on anything and perhaps I could even be wrong. Only reason I say you could do more is noticing other mages in the same type of gear putting out higher numbers than you are, which leads me to believe you can do better.

If you would like a more serious discussion as well from other mages on the forums, list what you do for your rotations. List good info so people can see what it is your doing during an attempt and then they can perhaps provide you with better advise.

You should keep in mind that each fight is different and so will your dps, even more so if you are assigned a task for a particular fight. The one thing that I would point out is keeping your mana above 90% during the regen phase. I try to maintain 80% and up. This will give you more arcane blasts at 4 stacks which might bump your dps up some. As Guromin has recommended to me, download SimulationCraft and import your character; it will tell you the dps you should be doing.
