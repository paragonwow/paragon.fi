The Fester Blood will cause the Adherents to bleed out themselves, and they will die after a while depending on how much health they had left to begin with. This made them constantly cast a shadow AoE with about 20 yard radius that also caused heavy corrupted blood, and after they died they would spawn Blood of the Old God on that spot. However the AoE cast by the adherents was changed to infinite radius in last patch so we had to re-work our tactics. So now you actually have to kill them before Fester Blood casts or they will hurt your raid.
