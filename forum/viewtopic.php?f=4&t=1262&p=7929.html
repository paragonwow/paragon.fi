It's still far easier, much because of the reasons that were stated in the posts prior to the raid testing. They're surprisingly accurate - could write the same stuff now after playing the 10-man heroics. They're a challenge, but they're just not equally hard compared to 25s. It's not even at the "pretty close'ish" level, they're just quite a bit easier. They're still good bit of fun, though, and definitely a big step up from the Wrath 10s.


Now, keep in mind they're still not done with the overall balance. It's still beta and not the final release. There's going to be further numbers passes prior to the release. If you're a hardcore believer, it'll be perfect by then.

I'll remain a skeptic until proven wrong.
