You as a Disc Priest are neither a raid healer nor a "DPSer". Your role within your raid group is to presume all incoming damage and mitigate it as much as possible. Whether that comes from Aegis stacking via ProH or PW:Shield, the choice is yours, but NEVER should you be asked to pull 10k DPS (assisting with it on fights like Ultraxion or when there is downtime is the exception).


As AA/A spec, I believe you start at with Smite x2, Holy Fire, Smite x2 and then you proc your AA. From here on out, it's up to you to decide what happens next. If there is no huge incoming damage, then keep smiting/HF away at the boss, while keeping your PW:Shield up on your tank(s) to proc your Rapture off it's CD. Your raid leader must have bumped his or her little head and gone crazy. Either or, you should ask them to provide you with a current WoL link of a Disc Priest within a 10M raiding enviroment who is pulling 10k DPS purely from smite/HF... it just isn't going to happen, nor should it.
