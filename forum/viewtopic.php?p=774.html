Hello!

I guess you spotted our World first Anub'arak kille where I did 7,3k dps. The numbers look like that because there is 4 adds up most of the time in the fight, which means I can Mind Sear out of Anub'arak and hit 4 targets at the same time. That also explains our rogues and death knights pulling off 9-10k dps.

On our second week on Anub'arak we managed to get World first Tribute to Mad Skill, and there my dps was 6,7k. This is because I was on the boss the whole time in phase 3, so I didn't get to dps the adds.

If you want to check out the video of our Mad Skill kill you can download it from wmo:
http://www.warcraftmovies.com/movieview.php?id=125179 (recommended!)
or youtube
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OuLK7uzW ... re=related (phases 1 and 2)
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O6OYKH-Vu08&feature=fvw (phase 3)

If you are looking for good shadowpriests guides you can check for example this one out:
http://www.mmo-champion.com/class-priest/(priest )-shadow-dps-101/ (thanks to Worshaka and others for a good guide and constructive feedback). There are other guides and tips on shadowpriest.com, ensidia.com and elitistjerks.com

For this boss especially a shadowpriest should normally dps the boss and then aoe the adds down as fast as possible (I let my dots drop from boss while aoe:ing). When the boss burrows dot up the remaining adds and incoming small adds. Nothing too special really.
