1000 less mana/per 5 second and 10% haste > 10% less haste but you will restore 1000 more mana every 5 second? I don't think so. So lets pretend you are in an encounter that lasts 420 seconds = 7 mins. If you had spirit reforges you would restored extra 84 000 mana during the combat, which is around half of your total mana pool instead of 10% more haste. I bet everyone must notice the difference in mana.
But the question is do you need to restore those extra 84 000 mana in addition to Shadowfiend + Volcanic Potion + Hymn of Hope. The answer would be NO for most of the bosses that are on farm and people are doing enough dps and have superior gear. To me a progress boss currently is Spine of the Deathwing 25 heroic. The lenght of the encounter is near the highest: 10 min+, which means I'll benefit from spirit a lot more than from haste. It's also a very mana consuming fight. In the end haste is way less valuable than Spirit even as a Disc.
My goal is to kill all the bosses and clear the content, not top silly HPS World of Logs.

Haste is definitely better If you don't run oom with your mana CDs. If you can manage with lesser spirit then sure reforge them out. But I personally feel more comfortable with Spirit reforges than with Haste.

ps. PW:S spam is more viable now with 4-set!
