Madness hc is an interesting fight currently (at least for my guild) because the actual dps numbers hardly reflect the benefit a certain player brings to the raid. There are multiple situations where doing most dps is actually hurting more than helping the raid (dps'ing arms too low, AoE'ing bloods too fast, ignoring the tentacles when cataclysm is on etc) and affliction is pretty efficient for meter whoring there. Depending on your raid dps on each area the best spec might vary but I would make the choice personally between demo and affliction.

On spine you should be playing demo if you have enough time between the tendons to get your meta up most of the time, otherwise I don't even know, I have played affliction the last 2 weeks and I haven't been very good in terms of tendon dps.
