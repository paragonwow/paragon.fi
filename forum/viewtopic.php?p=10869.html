I'll write down how we started with the 10-man group (keep in mind that this is the casual friends group, nothing official). Take in the information you think is important.

For the first raid we made sure people had enough item level, rights specs, gems and enchants and consumables in order. Nothing makes me as angry as people who don't prepare good enough and make others suffer. Preparing good also ensures that wipeing happens because of bad performance, and nothing else.

For the first encounter we did Baradin Hold, then we went for Halfus. If you have Slate Drake it might be very hard on your low geared healers. We one shotted Omnotron system in our first raid, but that might require a bit more knowledge of the mechanics to get done easily. Maloriak was also fairly easy, as Theralion and Valiona. And Atramedes when no one makes personal mistakes and dies to sound level.

Not sure what else to say. Let me know if I didn't answer your questions at all.
