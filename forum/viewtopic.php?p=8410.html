Currently there are no cata agility enchants on wrists, so leatherworking is worth around 100 agility while blacksmithing, alchemy and such are worth 80 (jewelcrafting gives 81 agi).

Engineering has no melee enchant at the moment but there's a 480 int use that has a 20% uptime. Assuming that there will be a similar agi enchant when cata releases engi will be worth 96 agility + it has some other useful tools like rocket boots and the belt absorb use. Engineering also gets a free epic helmet, which may or may not be better than our tier part but atleast you can get it easily.

Personally I've farmed the lw materials, but I'm still not sure whether I'll drop JC or engi or if I even want to drop either of them. There's still a chance that they will include a 50 agility wrist enchant (which drops the worth of lw to 80 agi) and the 480 agi use for engi on launch (which makes engi clearly the best profession as it also has useful tools), so I'll wait for that before I decide.
