First thing i notice is that your gemming for socket bonuses...... bad idea. the socket bonus is a waste compared to the gain you get from gemming pure agility/arp. also some of your gear isn't the best, your staff, your belt, your gloves/shoulders are good examples of this. Your trinket [Needle Encrusted Scorpion] is a terrible choice for an MM hunter, try and get [Whispering Fanged Skull] from deathwhisper 10 man instead. your shoulders and gloves should be replaced with T10 ones either 251/264. If you have access to it try and get the Sindragosa 10man/25 man neck.
Thats all i can trink of at the moment, i'm sure some of the Paragon hunters will be able to advise you further but i think i have covered most things.

Right now femaledwarf suggests slightly over 10k dps for you, if you can't get anywhere near that with the ICC buff there's something wrong with how you play - but without a log and/or video I can't comment on that. You can easily improve things by fixing your gear and spec, though:

I'd recommend replacing that NES with Herkuml War Token (or WFS, or any other high end trinket really) and gemming full arpen, it shows as a definite dps win with that gear level. Also you really should get rid of some of all that hit so you can take Focused Aim - you can get 1/3 for "free" and even 2/3 without really losing any other dps talents. Suggested pieces to replace are the neck and 2h weapon.

In any case get rid of those purple gems. One Nightmare Tear to activate meta, every other gem should be arpen. Or if you don't want to go that way, every other gem should be agi or agi/crit if you can get a socket bonus with that, but never agi/stam or arpen/stam.

Spec like this, and once you have less than 8% hit move the point from IHM to Focused Aim, and if you can get to 6% hit from gear move one point from Rapid Recuperation to Focused Aim also.
