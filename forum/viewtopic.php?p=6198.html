zYN

Do the hunters and rogues in your guild have the sense to give Misdirects and ToTTs to the tank at the start? Because you getting aggro really shouldn't be happening. Sounds like some "L2P" issues with the tanks or like you need to have a chat with your rogue and hunter department.

Other than that, that priority list looks like it's copypasted from EJ. In a word, it's horrible.


In a nutshell: Use Glyph of Disease, Obliterate and Frost Strike. Apply IT+PS. Smash away with Obliterates. Use Rime procs if you're soon'ish gonna go out of RP otherwise. Use AMS every single cooldown to soak RP. Use KM Frost Strikes asap, but only as long as you're not going to go past RGP (Rune Grace Period). Use ERW at the start only if you're gonna get another one up at <35%. Swap SotHM to SoA <35% during a GCD. Get a decent proc monitor so that you know when to pop ERW - double procs and such give it a _massive_ amount of power.

Also, after checking your armory, you're horribly under the expertise cap. That's something you really, really don't want as frost; anything that'll give you a GCD for nothing hurts DPS by a ton. Every GCD should be filled with a damage ability.

You're also quite a bit over the 11% spell hit cap (assuming you have spell hit in the raid). Do note that spell hit and melee hit have a different value.

Swap your Sigil of Virulence to SotHM (once you get T10 first) and learn how to hit your buttons once every 15s so that it doesn't fall off. Only fights where it'll be "better" is LK during Frostmourne phase (at percentages where you should be using SoA anyway) and on Valithria. If it drops in rest of the fights, notice it and do it better next time.

What you're really gonna want to do though is get some badge gear first. It's not hard at all to get the previous T9 pieces for example by running heroics. Slap some gems on your sockets - half of them are empty. Try to put some effort into it. Otherwise it's gonna be pretty much moot.
