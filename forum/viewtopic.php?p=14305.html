I would not get the Fiery Quintessence instead of core. The current BIS trinket slots look like the following for Holy paladins:

Shard of Woe>Jaws of Defeat (HC)>Tyrande's Favorite doll>Jar of ancient remedies (HC)>Jaws of defeat(Normal).

But I do, on the other hand, like my HC Fall of Mortality+Tsunami more than Normal Jaws or Jar instead of one of them. Personally I don't like Jaws since it is an on use trinket and to get the full use of the trinket you need to cancel the Haste buff that Judgement gives to make your casts 2 seconds+ and you being able to get 2 stacks in one cast. At 381 item level, I don't feel the need for Jar since I like some intellect and I am not having trouble with mana regen at all.


Then my advice for you is: Go BoT, get the normal Fall of mortality, or if your guild is still progressing HC BoT try to get the Shard from Sinestra. In my opinion the Fiery Quintessence is not that great of a trinket for Holy paladins.
