Hey Diivil, I have a little idea I'd like you to consider. I noticed my Felguard runs oom after about 4-5mins, which makes him cleave a lot less when oom. Without Cleave the dps of the Felguard drops quite a lot and Demonic Pact has a higher chance of dropping without cleave crits. I realized that I can fix this without any dps loss on my part, by taking 1 point out of Master Summoner and putting it in Mana Feed. This makes your summon to be a 2sec cast. This doesn't matter much when out of combat, but while in combat my pet doesn't ever really die (at least not in icc), and if he dies, I only lose one extra second summoning him.
