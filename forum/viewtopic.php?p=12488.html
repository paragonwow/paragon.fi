I am a prot tank and this is how i survive scorpion cleave. we still havent killed him yet , we are learning till. But it has worked so far good for me.

Since we do not have priest heal (for bubble)in our raid this is how we do it.

we engage boss in scorpion form and i use my Ardent defender for the first cleave. even though his cleave is something like 2M damage i will get healed to 15% health by using ardent defender. We spread out once he reaches 50% rage before he cleaves for the second time. We let him stay in cat form for like 7-8 stacks depending on the raid damage. then we would stack and force him to take scorpion form. this time we use Guardian of ancient kings + divine protection(with glyph) + 2 shadow priest using disperse to share the cleave damage. once he is done with his cleave spread out. then stay in cat form for 6-7 stacks again and then stacks again. By this time ur Ardent defender will be up and u take the cleave alone this time without any one stacking on tank.
Keep this rotation and tank will not die at all for cleave damage. The only drawback i have seen by this mechanics is that I will not have any CD at all except my holy shield in cat form and more over tank should be the only one taking damage in cat form so all heals would be on you.

I have seen few videos where they stay stacked for atleast 2 cleave by using priest bubble for tank + pain suppression.
You need the dps to save more stacks to put in more damage as the boss sits with ~458M HP. if ur dps takes damage during cleave there is no way u will kill him before he one shots tanks in cat form
