SybanDraenor,

As Saintlyn stated, I would recommend a Paladin and a Death Knight for the two tanks, though for different reasoning.
Dragon Soul has a ton of Magic damage compared to the amount of physical damage taken. Because of this, it makes DKs very strong in this tier. Death Knights by far have the best magic mitigation of all the tank specs / classes.

The reasoning behind the paladin is that Protection Paladins have a good amount of self Cooldowns and Raid Cooldowns when needed, compared to all other tanks. For example: Guardian of Ancient Kings,Divine Guardian, Argent Defender, Holy Shield, Divine Protection, ect.

Warriors are higher dps in general, and all the major "tanking" cooldowns can be brought by any warrior with a simple macro or other cast. Think of things like Shield Block, Rallying Cry, ect. All warriors can bring it with a simple macro.

Druids just lack the CDs and the mitigation for the most part for Dragon Soul. Due to a majority of the damage taken in Dragon Soul being magic, druids will get still get hit by it and cant dodge it (which is mainly what druids are about). In order to have good magic defenses they would have to spec into Perseverance, which is a pure-tank build only, which would hinder their ability to dps in certain situations (Such as 1 tank fights, ect).

So in conclusion, you should aim to have a Deathknight and Paladin tank combo throughout heroics (at least at this point.)

Normals modes are whatever you guys wish to do, and tank set-up doesnt really matter to much.

I hope this helped. If you have any other questions, feel free to ask and I will try to answer accordingly.

- MtHyjal
