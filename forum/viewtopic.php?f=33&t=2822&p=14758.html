Rileytucker,

While I understand and recognize your concern for the damage boost of the T12 4 set, I can assure that it is better than the previous tiers. Your gear's intellect will be a larger dps gain than the haste from T11. It appears to me that the source of your damage problem is not your gear, but instead your play style on the fight. On Heroic 10m Ragnaros a mage should be able do enough damage to kill an entire add. When you should be doing is casting arcane blast all the way until the very last second when Ragnaros submerges, then blink to your side of the hammer and activate PoM. This will allow you to hit the add the second it spawns and keep all 4 stacks of the Arcane Blast debuff active. In doing so your cast time will be shorter and in addition it will hit harder. If for whatever reason you are still having a problem you can push the add back by casting Mage Armor and running into the hammer. I know it is generally bad to inflict this damage, but in this case it will give you a spell power boost and push the adds back. In our 25m raids this tactic has saved the raid from certain perish several times and allowed us to progress further on the fight as we slowly endeavor to grind away the final percentages of this boss.

I imagine your other problems have solutions as well, but they do not relate directly to your gear, but instead play style. I can't give you a definitive answer without knowing the exact situation that surrounds these other problems.

I have stopped going through all the effort to take screen shots and show people exactly what to do for gearing, because anyone can look back on those posts to understand for themselves. If you want a better reforging calculator download Rawr. Easy to find on google along with instructions. If you have questions on how to make it work or anything else let me know.

If there is anything I missed or you have any other questions please make a post and I will get back with you as soon as I can.

-Guromin
