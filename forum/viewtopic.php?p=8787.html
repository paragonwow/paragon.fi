Anyway, we do very competitive damage at this part of cataclysm atleast. Our singletarget is not _as_ great as some classes (nerf hunters..) and you probably wont be top 1 damage on those fights. At the moment I feel its horrible for us to wipe on a boss since our eclipse bar resets. For example getting bloodlust and having adds up just when you gain solar eclipse, with dots.. Its just too delicious compared to having it in another state. But either way, yes, cataclysm is going to be full of awesome for owls.

Hello,

hit rating is more important than spellpower, or any stat for that matter until you are capped. Usually you do not need to worry about this too much, since it is easy to reach. Just reforge your gear to spirit/hit. I see you have not done so for some parts of your gear. Check your character sheet, you will see how much you are missing from the spell hit % cap. Cap is 17% (or 1742 rating) for bosses. Dont get me wrong, int is still a godly stat for us, but I mean, actually hitting boss for 200 less with a spell doesnt matter if your spell doesnt hit at all, right? :)

I know you cant do this for your trinkets since you are wearing int ones. You should be able to reach the cap without a spirit trinket, also if you run some dungeons theres some nice trinkets there that you can reforge to spirit if it is needed. Also an all stat enchant on your chest and a new leg enchant will give you more spirit. Or gemming int/spirit gems in a few slots helps too.

~Xephros
