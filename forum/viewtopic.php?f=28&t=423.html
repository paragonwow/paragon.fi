Welcome to the Hunter thread!

Feel free to ask questions related to Hunters in this thread. Please do use the search function and see if the question has already been asked first, though. When asking for dps help, please post link to your armory profile - for best results posting WoL/WMO/WWS link will also help.

Frequently Asked Questions

Question: What's the correct shot rotation?

Answer: There is no fixed rotation. Both MM and SV use a priority list, and always use the highest priority shot ready to use.

MM: Silencing Shot (does not use GCD) > Kill Shot > Serpent Sting (if not already up) > Chimera Shot > Aimed Shot > Steady Shot.
SV: Kill Shot > Explosive Shot > Serpent Sting > Black Arrow > Aimed Shot / Multi-Shot > Steady Shot.

Question: Should I gem agi or arpen?

Answer: "Depends." If you're SV, definitely agi, however if you're MM and have decent gear the question becomes harder to answer. Use the available tools (spreadsheets, simulators) to figure out which is better for your gear.

I dropped your armory profile into simulationcraft and it suggests around 9.2k dps with full raid buffs and debuffs on target, so 7k sounds a bit on the low side.

There's some details about the rotation, or lack thereof, in this thread which I won't repeat here.

Some suggestions for improvement: drop herbalism, get a real raid dps profession instead (JC is the best choice for ranged). Replace the helmet with T9, even the ilevel 232 T9 is massive upgrade due to the 2piece setbonus being very good. You're also pretty far (very far, if you always have draenei in 25man raids) over the hitcap, so might consider trying to pick up some piece(s) with less hit. If you don't always have a draenei I'd suggest running with 2 specs, one with 1 point more in the hit talent.

Check out this wowhead profile, this shows about 9.5k on simulationcraft - 300dps from just switching one item, a few talent points and JC as second profession.

PS. Note that as MM with arp build you're relying heavily on buffs/debuffs from others; especially if sunders/expose are missing you'll lose a huge chunk of dps.
