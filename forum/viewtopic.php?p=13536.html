roxodon wrote:I'm a disc priest and I've got a Jaws as trinket, as my second trinket I'm having a bit of wonders. I currently have, Tsunami, Moonwell Chalice and a Necromantic Focus. And of course I've got the Avenger's rep trinkets. I'm wondering if Necromantic is worthwile to use, or if you find holy fire not that available for use in FL.

Atonement & Co. don't really work in most of Firelands, so the Focus is right out.

Tsunami's SPI isn't particularly impressive for Discipline, but neither are the actives if you can't use them. Like everything else, it depends on what you're planning to do...

mannaroth wrote:Would you say this all applies to 10 man as well? Particularly the part about shield spam. I'm wanting to try disc but I don't really have any experience playing it in a 10 man environment. I read somewhere you said it values haste more than it would in 25 man but I'm not really sure why.

Tens already limit Shield-spam simply by number of available targets. PW:S and non-PW:S spells scale by diametrically opposed stats; PW:S doesn't benefit from Haste, and others gain virtually nothing from Mastery. As one would expect, this makes Haste less valuable in 25s where Shields make up a much larger portion of one's healing.
