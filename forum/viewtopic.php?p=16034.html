In order to break the next platea of 37.5% you would need to obtain 3002 haste. With your current gear this is not possible even if you were to sacrifice hit (which wouldn't be a good idea). Theoretically going for this haste mark is a good idea, but in actuality the amount sacrificed to obtain it isn't worth it. In full BiS gear it may be something to consider and I will have to look into it, but right now (in your gear) I wouldn't advise it. In addition I don't think one extra tick of living bomb will make the Living Bomb glyph worth using. If your ever unsure try reforging and running a simulation craft to see how things compare.
