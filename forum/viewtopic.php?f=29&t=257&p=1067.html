Thanks!

The choice to opt for the chest offpiece is two-fold. First off, as a badge loot, I was sure to get it. We did have one of those shoulders drop, but even that was only this week. Second, I wanted to get rid of 3/3 CF, so I needed to grab 856 haste from somewhere. The chest provides way more haste with a bigger slot modifier and better stat spread. If you're short on badges or already have the tier chest, I wouldn't sweat over it - the difference isn't anything ground-breaking.

As for Trauma, the proc essentially replaces spirit and haste on the weapon. While it probably isn't the best thing since sliced bread, the proc usually contributes around 1-2% to effective healing. Since I've already attained the necessary amount of haste, it essentially comes down to either mana regen or the proc. I don't really need the extra regen that much.

Oh, and there's also the fact that the loot gods hate me when it comes to weapons. All I have is a 258 staff and some +hit one-handers.

Eventually I'll probably wield 277 Trauma and Sindragosa OH, since it's pointless to even think about including heroic Arthas loot in gearing choices (game over).

(edit: Surprisingly enough, Sindragosa, not Sapphiron)
