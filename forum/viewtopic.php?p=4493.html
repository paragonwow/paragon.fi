1) Personal preference, I used to play with DP when you had to be in 30 yd range with your pet in order to use DP and imo it sucked ass. I haven't speced back since then because I don't have any issues with mana as most of the ICC fights require some movement and there is plenty of time to LT while moving. With LT spec I don't have to track my pet's mana or have 2 LOL I WNAT MANAS -buttons for both LT and DP (if the pet is oom, DP gives 0 mana).

You might think that you save 1 talent point when you spec to DP, but where are you going to put that talent? Drain Soul, threat is never an issue in 25 man raid unless your rogues and hunters have some serious l2p issues. Fel Concentration might provide a minor dps gain in some fight which has a lot of pushbacks, but I don't honestly remember any except LK's pain and suffering and Blood queens megabolts.

I was DP speced when we got the LK firstkill, but because of the 30 yd range I speced back to LT because the content is under farm and 1 little pushback talent isn't going to change anything. I have recently thought about specing back to DP since the range has been changed but we'll see.

2) Pretty much yes, if you happen to have t10 proc active while you have to move, feel free to apply a new corr. I don't know if someone has made some massive corr t10 proc sub 35% math, but I've come to conclusion that reapplying corr is better dps if the boss lives longer than 30 seconds roughly. If you can find some theorycrafting/discussion about this, feel free to post a link.

TBH I've never given this a much thought because I've usually been able to cast a corr with t10 proc at 35-30% and then it surely is better dps. Or I've had to move while I've had t10 proc.
Goddamn, this fissure skill is inaccurate.

No, you use glove enchant for the first time after you have NIC corr rolling on the target.

Chamz wrote:And one question regarding the Life Tap glyph, should i refresh the buff it gives if it falls off say in the middle of a DM or Eradication proc or should i wait until the proc ends?)


Wait for the proc to end. If you have to move, feel free to life tap unless you can do something else like shadowflame or dot instants to some add.

Chamz wrote: If Bloodlust comes at the start of the fight is it better to use a Wild Magic pot then or to wait until the boss goes under 25% and pop a speed pot asuming you dont have the pre pot cd up?


You should prepot before a fight, next pot you can take is ready after 1 min. In the case you described you should take a speed pot after 25%. however if you lust 1 min after the fight, you should use a wild magic pot during lust.
