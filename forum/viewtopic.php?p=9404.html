Hello Raily

I think mastery sucks only because theorycrafting says so, there isn't much more to it. I think its pretty hard to try to imagine if this kind of stat is good or bad without doing any calculations.

When you think about our mastery you are most of the time replacing one of your global cooldown with 3hp TV, so you can replace one of your less hitting ability with harder hitting ability which isn't even the largest priority for us (exorcism and hammer of wrath are higher). Mastery also works against us when you use zealotry since it delays our next crusader strike making zealotry even less beneficial but this is probably the smallest problem associated with mastery.

I hope the change in mastery will make it a stat we might want more than avoid it like a plague.
