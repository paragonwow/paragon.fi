Let me elaborate further. WoW is a game with the current playerbase exceeding the population of my country, twice. No matter what level you're at, you'll always find someone who isn't nearly as good as you are. This has lead to a community where it's common for everyone to share their post-traumatic experiences of their encounters with bads. You look down to everyone who are on a lower tier (while disregarding everyone that might be above you, they're all disgusting nerds anyway) and shudder to think they are being given to chance to enjoy as much of the game content as you do.

I will play as long as I have fun playing it and feel like I'm being challenged by the game. I don't mind if everyone who wants to gets to kill the same boss in a different mode or a week later or wear the same items I do as long as they'll enjoy it.

If your guild isn't even close to 6/7 heroic, you probably wouldn't get there before next patch, even less so have a shot on Ragnaros. Personally I really don't think the amount of 7/7 heroic guilds there will likely be by the launch of 4.3 is going to justify this uproar.
Manni
