Most of the times you rather have [Glyph of Growl] instead of [Glyph of Maul], with [Glyph of Survival Instict] and [Glyph of Frenzied Regeneration]. In most of the fights the tank rotating is pretty crucial and you can't have RNG on the way the fuck up things in case your taunt misses. And the only real fight where [Glyph of Maul] is useful (in my opinion) is Lady Deathwhisper and Valithria Dreamwalker due to the different tempo those fights have and the job the tanks have (had) to pull out due to the strict DPS requirements they brought forward, although this isn't true with 30% anymore nor for any good tank who knew how to manage their threat in the first place but anyhow, [Growl] > [Maul]
