Good to see you, Ivanoize, juandilol, bash, Mehra, bubat, and Stowned,
Ivanoize wrote:I wanna know which of these items is best to my holy starter gear, Merlin's Robe or Royal Moonshroud Robe and the wrists: Bejewled Wizard's Bracers or Royal Moonshroud Bracers?

[Royal Moonshroud Robe] and [Bracers] without so much as a moment's doubt. Not to mention for starting gear the effectiveness of Haste is even further downplayed.

juandilol wrote:How would you convince a Holy Priest to take Improved Renew and Empowered Renew talents?

While Flash Heal is quite a viable tool in five-man dungeons, Renew far outpaces it in true raiding, courtesy of a dramatic difference in Spell Power scaling and the catered-to-by-encounter-damage-mechanics nature of HoTs.
Even though at this point one may feel comfortable with Flash Heal, the style will fall further and further behind in output as the level of gear rises.

bash wrote:Hi, This is my char http://eu.wowarmory.com/character-sheet ... =Narkissus

Would like to ask what items should I get or change + enchants/gems etc.

Well, like many you're in the position where there isn't much one can do to actively upgrade their equipment. Given your progress I would recommend that you spend your next Emblems on [Ermine Coronation Robes], but beyond that your following pieces are left to Lady Luck, as Holy even cares none for stat planning.

Now, you can definitely fine-tune your setup by picking up SP-SPIs to replace those Mp5 ones, and spending one of those Empowered Healing points on Inner Focus ― even "Reduces the mana cost of your Divine Hymn spell by 100%." for 1/1 is still too cheap to pass.
Outside those your current course is looking mighty fine to me.

Mehra wrote:Couldn't another alternative be to take the San'layn Ritualist Gloves instead of the tier 277 gloves, and then grab the tier 277 chest? This combination of gear would yield the same amount of spell power, but you would trade ~100 haste for ~115 crit.
bubat wrote:I have both the gloves and the best and i dont know which one to pick.
Stowned wrote:I'm also interested what Jhazrun has to say regarding this as I've been thinking about what piece to get myself for quite some time.

What we're looking at here is at the same time a scenario of accessibility.
Even though I already consider the Haste-SPI preferable to the Crit, it's not where another choice lands in the timeline.

Gloves sport a lower itemization budget than leggings or chestpieces. This results in an upgrade from 264 to 277 yielding a greater stat increase for the latter slots.
Imagine one already with both [Sanguine Silk Robes] and [San'layn Ritualist Gloves] at 277, and finally picking up their first HC token. Which slot do they opt to upgrade?

If they were to spend the token on the 277 tier robes and keep [San'layn Ritualist Gloves], they'll find themselves at a 277 chestpiece and 277 gloves.

In stead, they choose to wear [Sanguine Silk Robes], and go for the 277 tier leggings. This way they'll end up with TWO HIGH-BUDGET PIECES AT 277.

The supply of HC tier tokens remains low to this day, and one's second will continue to take its time. 'Til then the above stat advantage rings true. Now, while this does partially fall within the realm of min-max tinkering, the SPI > Haste > Crit rule explains the dominance of [Sanguine Silk Robes] even in a long-term view.
