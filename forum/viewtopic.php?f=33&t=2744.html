The gemming/enchants look fine to me. I usually opt for the +50 Mastery to boots but I see a lot of casters that prefer the run speed.

As for the moonwell chalice vs. Darkmoon Card, I much prefer the Darkmoon Card for most fights. The only time I generally use the Moonwell Chalice is on fights such as Alysrazor where there is a clear burn phase and I can make good use of the Mastery use on the trinket. But for the most part, I stick with the permanent mastery boost from the Card with the int+damage proc it has on the equip.

One other thing I would say is that your professions could squeeze out more stats for you. For example the embroidery to cloak from tailoring which I much prefer to the +50 intellect enchant to the cloak, +40 intellect enchants to rings from enchanting, or the +67 intellect gems from jewelcrafting. Not to mention you can make your pants enchant from tailoring for free :D!

Tailoring is by far the best profession for mages hands down, however blacksmithing and leathworking provide the same benefit as enchanting, jewelcrafting etc. Blacksmithing, with the 2 extra sockets = 80 intellect, Leatherworking, with the 130 int to bracers enchant instead of the 50 intellect enchant available to non leatherworkers.
