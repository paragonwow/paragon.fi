Hey!
According to multiple calculations and spreadsheets the arp gems pull ahead agi gems when you reach around 400~ armor penetration from gear with grimtoll or mjollnir runestone as second trinket. Arp also becomes better the more you have it, unlike agility or attack power which increase your dps by flat amount. I myself swapped to arp gems in process of preparing to get rid of my 4 piece bonus from t8 which was the reason to gem agility in the first place.
About the incoming arp nerf, I think the arp is still gonna be good stat to gem if you have a great gear with high amount of arp in it, if not attack power might might be the way to go once again without t8 rupture bonus.

You can try out diffrent gem & gear setups with some of the spreadsheets to see which works the best for you. You can find the spreadsheets in here:http://elitistjerks.com/f78/
