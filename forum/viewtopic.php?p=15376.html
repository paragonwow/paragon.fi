1. Probably your priority system, or just general lack of practice with the spec.

2. From what I see H Rag trinket surpasses the chalice and maybe H necro both together, then from there DS trinkets, not sure yet.

3. Absolutely nothing is "Patchwerk" In dragon soul so far. The closest you come is Ultraxion and it's still not quite patchwerk. You still have that ~5 seconds of absolute dps stop at every twilight shift, thing.

I personally believe that the incinerate build is superior to that of the shadow bolt one. The only debate to that is you lose the cast time on immolate and for fights that you need to "double DoT" it's better to be affliction anymore (mostly General so far)

Make sure you're pet swapping efficiently. If you can find short down times, like between killing bloods on spine, then use that to summon one of your pets. A little tip, try and watch your fel hunters shadow bite and put shadowflame up so that it will be up when he bites so he gains another boost from it.

I know I"m not a paragon warlock but maybe my advice will be helpful
