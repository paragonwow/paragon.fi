domtheskater wrote:just a simple question i was wondering which food and elixar/flask buffs i should be using for raids atm im using spirit.

Also whats the role of holy priests in raids now

Spirit has recently become very potent for Holy. Intellect, however, is intertwined in far too many factors to find itself truly challenged as an end-game optimization target. Budgeting into Spirit is primarily a ten-man concept, though an effective one at that, as the format leaves little room for manipulation of one's resources.

While no true roles tend to exist in ten-man encounters, fully-fledged raids most commonly see Holy as a tool for countering streaks of raid-wide damage through brute force.
