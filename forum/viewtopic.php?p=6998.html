Hey all!

I'm that postgraduate researcher who's doing her PhD on WoW raiders (I posted a couple months ago about my interview with some of the Paragon members over the summer). As my blog is way less exciting than this one, I thought I'd post a little link to my own site to get your help with participating in my latest poll. I'm looking at where we set our computers up to play. For some of us that's in our bedrooms (especially if we live at home still or if we share a house with others), for others it's in the more communal areas of the house, like our living rooms. What I don't necessarily believe, but what the media and alarmists love to suggest, is that we hide away from the world to pursue our so-called 'antisocial' craft.

But regardless, let me know where you like to sit (or stand? :) when you raid! :)

http://www.raidingresearch.co.uk

Thanks,

Ladan
