BM is best spec in most of the fights. KC is the highes in your priority system from "normal" abilities which doesn't include cooldowns. Focus Fire is bit random when it stacks to 5x, but normally you should use your focus fire right after when your BW is going to end or bit after that, but wait that it stacks for 5x.

Talents depends on fight really, but mostly i have been using Lynx Rush cause its no focus cost and does high burst and good dps, but for example on amber shaper you should use MoC because there is 2 mobs tanked next to each other and other mob takes 99% less damage, so 50% of lynx rush attacks are going to do no damage. And another good example is Tsulong in terrance, been using blink strike on that boss because on p2 pet mobility is really key factor to maximise your dps. You need to swap targets all the time in p2 so you can have most uptime by taking blink strike and you need to remember NOT to use KC and blink strike on same target, if there is any other targets up. Mostlikely there is big adds and some other small adds running around so there should be alternative targets for your pet to blink on.
