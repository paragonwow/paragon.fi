We always try our best and play as good as possible. If that's world first we are happy, but it isn't the end of the world to us if we don't come up first. Basically we have been preparing for Cataclysm for months already. We follow class changes to have as good roster as possible, check out the new profession and guild perks etc. The usual stuff.

Now that we know the release date people also have arranged their schedules to have freetime from December 7th on.

Playing in beta is also very useful; learning the quests there and finding out which is the fastest way to reach level 85 makes us a bit more prepared. One also gets familiar with the zones, encounters, factions and mechanics beforehand.

After all it still comes down to the leveling session where you need to stay awake and just grind that xp.
