Hey Cornflare. I think I can manage to answer some of your questions and give you some hints.

First, the warrior's main stats are strength, crit, agility and armor penetration. In some cases haste would be good as well, but not as important as the three mentioned above.

A good geared warrior has 90 % + passive ArP, 1800-2000 strength, and between 45-50 crit %.

Gear tips;

Use all your frost badges on the Tier10 set (Ymirjar's Battlegear), until you have 4/5 pieces, which will give you the set bonus needed. The items you should get is the head, shoulders, chest and legs. When you have the 4/5 set, spend your frost badges on Gatecrusher's Gauntlets and Vengeful Noose. You got nice boots allready, so I'm not talking about them.

(You get the frosts by doing random heroics every day, as well as killing bosses in ICC. You get 2 frost badges per boss)

Btw, try to get a hold of some nice trinkets. A good start is Banner of Victory (TOC5 normal) or Mirror of Truth (vendor). If you are able to get into ICC, try to win Deathbringer's Will from 4th boss (25-man), and Whispering Fanged Skull (10-man). Some new weapons would increase your performance as well.

Caps and gemming;

Cap means that when you reach the specified number on a stat, you dont benefit from more of that stat, which makes getting overcapped a bit worthless.

Expertise cap = 26
Hit cap = around 160

You should never get below these ratings, because if you do, you will miss on crit strikes, and the enemy will be able to dodge and parry some of your slices, and that isnt good right?

You should allways make sure you are capped, then i would say that you go for full ArP on your gear. Don't gem strength on your gear, until you are either soft capped on ArP (around 740 rating, but that requires NES) or hard capped (1400 rating).

Rotation

BT --> WW --> Slam (on proc) / Heroic strike --> BT --> WW --> Slam (on proc) etc. When you got a lot of ragegiving gear, try spamming Heroic Strike on bosses. And be sure to use Death Wish in a good situation, as well as Recklessness. On AoE packs you should prefer doing some Cleaves as well.

Well, I'm a bit tired of writing now. Hope this helped, good luck! :)

Mirror of Truth is better than Mark of Supremacy as long as you are hitcapped. Because just as I said, you never want to get below Expertise and Hitcap. It's the most important thing in gearing.

It's not bad to be overcapped though, until you have aquired better gear.
I would advise you to fuck the socket bonuses, and gem for the stat that you benefit the most from.

I cant really say that it is worth to go for ArP all the way, until you can get a good amount of it on the gear(gems excluded).

And I can promise you one thing, you won't do good dps until you have a decent gear. Good luck.
