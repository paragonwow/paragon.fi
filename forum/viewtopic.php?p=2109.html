Kruf wrote:
So, if there are no sunders up and you have 100% arpen, effective armor on your physical attacks is:

Code: Select all
arpcap = (10643+16635)/3 = 9092
finalarmor = 10643-9092 = 1551


With sunders and faerie fire up, this becomes:

Code: Select all
armor = 10643*0.80*0.95 = 8088.68
arpcap = (8088.68+16635)/3 = 8241.23
finalarmor = 8088.68-8241.23 = -152.55


Thus, as you can see, having the armor reduction debuffs up does in fact matter even with "100%" arpen from gear.

Anyways, I have done a lil bit of work on ArP and for few who have a question like : Well I have certain percentage of ArP and I want to know how much percentage of armor i am exactly ignoring.

PS: This is based on the GC's quote that Arm Pen tool tip says UPTO% armor ignored but not excatly X% is being ignored. So u will notice how much your UPTO means when you have certain amount of % Arm Pen.
WITHOUT ANY ARMOR REDUCTION DEBUFFS ON BOSS

For 90% Passive ArP we have : Effective Armor Remaining on boss = 2460 ; Thus you have ignored a total of 77%
For 95% Passive ArP we have : Effective Armor Remaining on boss = 2005 ; Thus you have ignored a total of 81%
For 100% Passive ArP we have : Effective Armor Remaining on boss = 1550 ; Thus you have ignored a total of 85.5%

WITH ARMOR REDUCTION DEBUFFS ON BOSS (Faerie Fire (-5%) + Sunder Armro x5 (-20%)= (-25% Armor reduction on Total boss armor)

For 90% Passive ArP we have : Effective Armor Remaining on boss = 671 ; Thus you have ignored a total of 91.85%
For 95% Passive ArP we have : Effective Armor Remaining on boss = 260 ; Thus you have ignored a total of 96.85%
For 100% Passive ArP we have : Effective Armor Remaining on boss = -152.55 ; Thus you have ignored a total of 101.85%
