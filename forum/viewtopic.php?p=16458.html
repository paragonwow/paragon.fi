Affli perform very well on this fight. Make target macro for drakes, if executed properly you should have all dots up on both drakes when they fly by.

Start the fight on the helm, apply all dots and Haunt, soulswap and fel flame once or twice while running into pos for drake number two (depends if first drake bugs out and stays for another GCD). Transfer all dots and apply Haunt.

After that its a simple matter of applying dots on melee mobs and dotting new drakes as they fly by. Enjoyable fight as affli!
