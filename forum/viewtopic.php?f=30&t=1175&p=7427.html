1. Double Solace is the current BiS.

2. Let's look at the numbers.

Libram of Veracity:
+28 Spirit
Libram of Blinding Light:
+8 Intellect
+34 Critical Strike
+6 Haste

Personally I'd take Blinding Light, and reforge some of the critical strike rating into spirit. That will give you +13 spirit. Intellect is still our main stat.

3. Depends on your play style. If you're good at using Holy Shock every time it's available, you're much better off taking Crusade over Divinity. Don't know why Ilonie/Smirk has taken Improved Judgement, but can only assume it has to do with some specific role they're playing on a specific encounter which doesn't allow they to move close to the boss for Judgement of the Pure.
