It also depends on the content you tank. Stamina is the most reliable survival stat, agility/dodge reduces damage taken most.

Seita tanks heroic content in previous content's gear, in other words, undergeared. With raidmembers who avoid much of the incoming damage, allowing healers to focus on the unavoidable damage.

ETA: To clarify: If I run a 5m dungeon with a poorly geared healer I'm most worried he might go oom if I soak too much damage, if I run a 25m heroic instance while undergeared I'm most worried about reliably surviving those big hits.
