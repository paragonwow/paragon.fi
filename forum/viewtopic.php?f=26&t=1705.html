The advantage in rupture is that it's essentially free damage, while eviscerate always costs atleast 10 energy. The combo points / sinister strike damage you gain from that extra energy just barely outweigh the "loss" in finisher damage. In practice though I find myself having to skip rupture because I'm either capping on energy, blade flurry is on or deep insight isn't going to last long enough for the whole 8 ticks to do damage. Now I'm not sure whether it's a good idea or not, but that's what I'm currently doing :P

I'd like to see them add something to combat that would encourage the usage of rupture (think venomous wounds), as now it's completely reasonable to just skip using it completely and concentrate more on the fight mechanics instead. The ability to do that isn't necessarily a bad thing, but running an evi-only rotation isn't really that exciting or fun.
