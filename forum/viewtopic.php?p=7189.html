So,

I was wondering how other fellow Resto Shamans are speccing now with 4.0.1. at level 80. Most of the talents are no-brainers but there is some room for variety.

I will go with 0/5/31 - http://wowtal.com/#k=fvy5ZfNs.a5o.shaman.g6jE-

Some words about it:

- Took Focused Insight to test it, can't say the idea of doing damage to heal more thrills me, feels more like a burden

- Left Soothing Rains out since Healing cannot be gained as of now.

- Telluric Currents... WTF is that even doing down there. Should be Tier 1 / 2 talent for Elemental Shamans to reach it. What use does Resto have from it at all.

- Ancestral Resolve or Nature's Guardian is a choice of whatever makes one more happy I suppose. Or leave it out and take 1/2 Soothing Rains

For Glyphs there ain't much choice either. There are only 3 Prime Glyphs that make sense, unless you are fresh on 80 and have mana problems, then the Water Shield Glyph might be useful. Major has only 2 useful ones and Glyph of Healing Stream Totem could also be replaced with Hex, Totemic Recall or Ghost Wolf.

All in all we're looking pretty good I'd say. Full on arsenal of fast heals, big heals, group heals, instant heals, HoTs and a shield.

Questions for others from me would now be:

- What is your (theoretical) opinion on Focus Insight? Will you drop the points in there or not?

- Soothing Rain - worth taking just for the Healing Stream Totem benefit as of now?

- Will you change gems? I was pure Haste or SP / Haste so far and w/o reading up much on number crunching, I don't feel I should do changes until Cata hits.

Remember, all of these questions etc. applies for 4.0.1 on level 80 to 80 content. I expect Cata content to be designed in a way that, for example, a new talent like Focus Insight might be required to use more in order for one to be effective.

I'm pretty sure there hasn't been any real number crunching regarding the gems, I'm probably going to stay haste, I can't see single-target'ing ever being better than Chain Heal spam again.

I feel that Focused Insight may be worth the points, if you know some major damage is incomming, you could shock the boss, and pop out a powerful Healing Rain, or Chain Heal.

As for Telluric Currents, it can be a decent source of mana if you're low, no doubt weird talents, but somewhat useful.

I would use this for 80: http://wowtal.com/#k=f1AAhgmP0.a6d.shaman.
^ If you're lazy, use Nature's Guardian instead of Focused Insight.
Enhancement talents are pretty much inarguable at level 80.

I would use this for 85: http://wowtal.com/#k=f1ADo0lY1.a6d.shaman.3-upE-
^ Same thing with Nature's Guardian and Focused Insight, get Totemtic Reach or Totem Focus, it's up to you.
