Despite being Russian, I still wasn't able to figure out what's "drawing for maximum loss" supposed to mean. But if you're just asking about macros and rotation, answers are fairly simple.

Macro /startattack into every damaging ability to minimize downtime on target switching. Basic DPS priority is: Templar's Verdict @3 > Crusader Strike > Hammer of Wrath > Exorcism > Judgement > Holy Wrath > Consecration. Aim for the haste soft cap (3 sec CS cooldown when raid buffed, somewhere at 1000+ haste), separate Zealotry from Bloodlust, don't use Seal of Righteousness on boss fights, and you should be good to go. To hear some more advanced tips you probably need to ask some more detailed questions.
