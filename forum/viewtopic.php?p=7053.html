Hey,

First I'd like to mention that you are a bit over the hit cap and would benefit from swapping Valanar's Other Signet Ring, which would result in 294 hit (which is only 5 over the cap). Also, you are wearing a lot of gear pieces which have spirit on them. Try to aim for the best pieces from here: http://www.shadowpriest.com/viewtopic.p ... a4&start=0

On the other hand Cataclysm is coming soon (4.0 in 1 week anyone?) which might explain those spirit items (if you want to stack spirit for leveling, I wouldn't do that tho). They are sub-optimal at the moment.

Your haste is a bit low and I encourage you to use simcraft to find out what's best for you. With your latency I'd advice you just finish your mf third tick and then mb. That latency makes timing very hard. If you can do it though, then just keep on clipping.

About 4.0 and the spec you linked I'd skip Paralysis and put the talents to Masochism. That's a great talent especially while raiding when you have a lot of damage incoming every now and then. We'll still have to see how oom we run during raiding/leveling and how it will change with Cataclysm items. I'd use Masochism while leveling as Paralysis is kind of meh. I like to have the mobs come to me while I kill them so I can loot them faster.

As you already said there has been a lot of talk about the rotation in 4.0 and people still have different opinions about Dark Evangelism and Dark Archangel and whether to Mind Spike or Mind Flay during DE and DA phases of your rotation etc. I think it's still a bit too early to consider anything written to the stone as we'll most likely still see changes. Myself I believe DA should be used to at least regen mana, we'll see what kind of dps loss/upgrade it will be. We'll have more information once the patch is out and all the last minute changes have been made. For now you can only log on ptr and run some tests or read http://www.shadowpriest.com
