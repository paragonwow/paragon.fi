The list takes into account highest overall DPS gain.

The personal DPS gains is a different list, where feral druids are higher. This list is only what YOU gain from who you put on DI. Meaning while you get stacks from a feral druid better than you would an elemental shaman. They gain almost nothing from the stacks you give them. And I may be wrong but I don't think haste is their best stat.

The overall takes into account what classes benefit from haste in what ways. For example, an elemental shaman would gain from the stacks you give them, and from the haste.

Overall Raid DPS differes from Personal Gains.
