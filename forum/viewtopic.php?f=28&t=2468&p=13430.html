Hello fellow Hunters,

I've been reading through several different forums regarding the 1850 Haste plateau and the general 12.93% Haste recommended by Whitefyst from the 4.2 MM guide on EJ. I currently use the 12.93% Haste because my gear is not good enough to reach 1850 without reforging dramatically from Crit and Mastery both to Haste.

Anyways, I decided to do a Femaledwarf evaluation between the 1850 Haste plateau and the 12.93% Haste plateau and before I continue I do realize FD only simulates Tank n' Spank type fights and other factors that do not make FD quite accurate when it comes to boss time but when it comes to comparing gear and finding upgrades I believe FD is a great tool to do so.

So... here are the links:

http://www.femaledwarf.com/?setting_id=47430 - 12.93% Haste
http://www.femaledwarf.com/?setting_id=47427 - 1850 Haste 3/3 Pathing
