Outto, your stat values are going to change, slightly between t13 and t12. As i'm sure your aware t13 2 set provides 500 haste once you get the stacks ramped up. this means we need a bit less haste to reach that all important 2005 area to get that extra tick of living bomb.


Essentially you want Intellect > Hit to or near cap > Haste to 1505 > Crit > Mastery > Haste.

That stat priority is for single target (or mostly single target) fights. On AoE encounters you may place a little more value in mastery.

Another thing to keep in mind is you need a fairly long fight to even see the t13 in action. If you're only doing a little bit of damage on a dummy (~3 min) you wont even see the benefits of the extra combustions.
