Let's see. No 2t10 setbonus? Major dps loss right there, the setbonus is good. What's the point of putting a crit/agi gem into a blue socket (gloves) - if you are going to socket anything except pure agi (or arp) make sure you're using it to get the best socket bonus you can.

Simulationcraft estimates about 9.9k sustained dps over a 5min standstill fight, so 9k is not that far, though there shouldn't be that much random in the numbers so you should be hitting 9k+ every time in standstill.

Here's a quick and dirty improved setup, showing 10.5k dps in simulationcraft: http://www.wowhead.com/?profile=20850521

Scaling factors (as dps increase for 1 point stat increase) for this build are:
Agi=1.7958 Int=0.8373 AP=0.7204 ArPen=2.3084 Hit=3.4653 Crit=1.5595 Haste=1.1312 Wdps=9.2019

Could be improved a lot with more arpen items, but I don't know what you have access to. See this link for list of upgrades. By far the best upgrade you could get is of course Deathbringer's Will.

PS. Note that I preferred an arp build over agi - the dps difference with this level of gear is rather minor, but makes it a lot easier to pick upgrades and you won't have to regem later when the difference becomes larger.

Kruf
 
Posts: 473
Stay away from NES, it's complete crap. Even greatness is better in most situations... The Kill Shot vs Chimera Shot glyph choice is somewhat dependent on encounter, raid and preference. Kill Shot is more dps on paper, but if your raid has Bloodlust/Heroism during execute phase and a lot of classes with high dps in execute range it can actually be less overall dps, and the chimera glyph makes fights where you have to switch away from the main target temporarily much less irritating since it makes rolling sting on 2 targets much more comfortable. Obviously for fights where execute phase takes a very long time (Anub'arak, anyone?) and fights where last phase dps is critical (Putricide, Sindragosa) Kill Shot glyph makes a lot of sense (which is why I carry a stack of both glyphs in inventory and swap when needed).
