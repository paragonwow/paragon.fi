We have group loot on. Lately Ilonie always rolls greed on every item and distributes / disenchants them if needed. People roll need if bids are already done before they have passed or if they already know they are going to get an item. To make adding the items to the DKP site easier (since with 6+ items dropping and open bids on raid chat can get pretty spammy) you have to whisper with the item name and DKP you paid for it to whoever is adding that stuff if you loot something.

Most paid items from this tier have been Ragnaros 2h mace, Ragnaros bow, 2x Majordomo neck and conqueror head piece.
