I'm probably going for the 4 part bonus when I get enough 277 tokens. Tier chest and head are very good. Tier gloves are a bit better than krakens. Tier shoulders are worse than random part from gunship iirc but I guess the tier bonus is decent enough to lose that 30 stamina or so.

Last word is pretty horrible. For bosses that do much magic damage other weapons might be better than Ardent Guard but I'm pretty sure I'll be using AG for a long time.

I use the first spec for raid tanking and the spec with SoC for heroics and easy raids. Many paladins take more threat talents instead of divinity for example but I don't find them necessary so I take all the survivability I can get.

LW gives resistance bracer enchant. It is the best way to get resistances if they are ever needed for something. Thats the reason why both our tanks have LW.
