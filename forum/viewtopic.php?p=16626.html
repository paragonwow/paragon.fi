Combustion will tick every second for 25,000. It doesn't matter how much "DPS" each dot does, only how much damage one tick will do. Ignite is so powerful because it only ticks twice, so now the dot that would have ticked once every 2 seconds for 4 seconds will tick once every second for 10 seconds (or more often higher haste).



I'd argue this is incorrect, Guromin. To my knowledge combustion does indeed take the sum of the damage per second of all periodic damage effects placed on the target.

If you crit for 100k and do a combustion with 0 mastery you would have a 10k combustion, not a 20k one.



If you read the combustion tool tip it states that it combines the periodic effects of all your dots and deals the per time equal over X seconds. Lets say for the sake of making things easy that X is 10 seconds. it would essentially make a 10 second ignite, pyroblast and living bomb combined and it would tick (depending on your haste) for the same damage per second that your current period damaging abilities did, of course it gets to crit and should be higher due to mastery.
