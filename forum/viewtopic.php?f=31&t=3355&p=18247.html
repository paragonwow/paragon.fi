If you want to get the most burst possible you need to gear gem enchant an entire gear set with as much mastery you can get. It does not matter if that piece has str agi or what ever you just want your mastery as high as possible (I have around 6400 mastery with mastery pot and MWC activation). Then you meta pre pull and then switch your gear set to your regular gear, Chug a flask, and a volcanic pot (I have a macro that does this). Doing this since meta effect is a snap shot of your mastery my first meta causes me to do 120% damage increase. Doing this along with poping all other cds can lead to 70-80k burst at the start of any fight.
