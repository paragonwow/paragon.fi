Yeah. I tried Marrowgar, Rotface etc kind of fights with the 0/2 build. I'm leaning more towards the 2/2 build - I'm definitely not having constant RP overflow. Even in very heavy RP regen fights (well over 100 extra RP/min), I occasionally get free GCDs. That is not a good thing.

Doing LK right now with 2/2 spec - even if it loses out on some AoE damage, the full CotG build seems to be overall more optimal regardless.


RGP = 2 second window after any rune comes off of cooldown and is again ready to be used. The rune will start regenerating up to two seconds in advance if it's not instantly used. Two seconds is the maximum time, after which you will harm your DPS - the runes don't ... PREGENERATE (wowzah!) further than that.

Examples: If you use a rune as soon as it comes off of cooldown, it will be ready in 10 seconds. If you use it 0.5s after becoming ready, it'll be ready in 9.5 seconds. If you throw a FS right as FU (oblit) runes become available, your runes will become available in 8.5 seconds, as long as you throw an Oblit after FS.

This is very important in scenarios where you'd overflow RP, you have a KM proc, you're low on RP but have a Rime proc up that's running out etc - it's quite simply just significantly more DPS.
