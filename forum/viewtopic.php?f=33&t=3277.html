Hey Thelynt.

Went over some of your logs and your armory. Noticed a few things that may be helpful to you.

First lets look at your armory.

- 3 unenchanted items. You're missing out on 70 intellect and 50 mastery/haste. Thats a pretty considerable amount.
- Your reforging looks good, but it appears you've done it in a inefficient way. For example, you've reforged out of crit into hit rating on one item, and reforged nearly the same amount of hit into haste. Essentially you reforged your crit to haste in that scenario. which is a DPS loss.
- Try to chop off more haste. 1505 is all you need and you should be able to get their at that gear level.
- Your glyphs look decent, however it looks like you have some Arcane glyphs in your major slot. You're not going to get a DPS increase out of them, but i'd recommend changing them. Arcane Power doesn't do you much good in fire.
- Socket Bonus on your ring wasn't worth taking. You should use a 40 int gem in it.
- Max your enchanting out. Thats an additional 80.

Now, onto your logs. I'm not sure which fight in particular you're curious about.

For now i'll be looking at your Ultraxion log.

- It looks like you hard cast a Pyroblast after the pull. Pyroblast is great for a prepull, but the only time you want to hard cast this is if your haste goes so high that your fireball cast timer is shorter then your global cool down timer.

- You used 2 Mirror Images outside of fading light. Which is a small DPS loss, however you used mirror images at the start when a lot od your CD's were stacked as well, which is a pretty considerable DPS loss. The global CD wasted on mirror images is actually less DPS then fireball spamming. So only use it when you're on the move, spell locked, need to drop threat, or have nothing else you can do. (fading light)

- You used your second combustion just moments before your trinket proc. Always always always ALWAYS make your first and second combustion line up with velocity.

- The combustions that you did get off were really weak with the exception of your first one. Always make sure have a good ignite before using them. Guromin wrote an excellent guide for stacking ignites. Highly recommend it.

- There are periods where you're not refreshing your living bomb for 5-6 seconds. While it's important to never clip of a living bomb. A living bomb should take priority over anything but a good combustion window.

- Using a potion both 1 second before the fight and during heroism or molten fury range would be a great DPS increase.


Overall you're doing pretty good. It's a bunch of minor mistakes that are adding up. Another thing to keep in mind is the raid composition you guys had for that log wasn't providing you with many caster buffs. which would be a very significant DPS increase.

If you'd like to me to look through a particular fight, lemme know.

Oh and just to note, critical mass isn't just for you. It applies to all spell damage. So the other mage benefits from your debuff and you benefit from his. it just goes back and forth since its being refreshed by pyroblasts
