@Glondz: Generally you would want to have SnD and BF up when you pop KS, as KS increase all damage done during its duration. You do perform autoattacks during KS, so increasing the attack speed as much as possible is desired.
About AR and BF: you will not gain any extra benefit from not using them at the same time, so there's no reason why you would want to alternate them. My advise is to use them as soon as possible (a few seconds into the fight so debuffs are applied to the target), and as often as possible, all while avoiding energy capping. And of course avoid using them at the wrong times (using AR while you are kiting an ooze on Putricide, for example, is bad).

Here's how I generally do the start of a fight:

TotT (tank)
Haste pot
+++Entering combat+++
Sinister Strike
Slice and Dice
Blade Flurry
Sinister Strike
Killing Spree
Adrenaline Rush

I know TotT'ing at full energy is a waste, but it gives more room to avoid energy capping during the start of the fight.

@clabbehala: Pretty much all of those questions are best answered by using a spreadsheet. Think there's a download link in Rakez signature. I can say though that the 4p bonus is not worth much in theoretical dps. It does have some situational benefits however, like potential burst damage on Val'kyrs.
