Congo wrote:What stat is it that you can focus on for leveling up?


Intellect is the best, I'd throw in some hit and/or spirit to miss (and get frustrated) less. Don't bother with crit, mastery or haste that much.

Congo wrote:What stats have priority at 85? Well i can guess my self that hit is number one but after that?


At the moment the list goes like this (for "medium gear levels"):

Int Spi SP Hit Crit Haste Mastery
Scale Factors 2.8786 1.0538 2.1101 1.0549 1.1680 1.1994 0.9710
Normalized 1.0000 0.3661 0.7330 0.3664 0.4057 0.4166 0.3373

(Taken from http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/820 ... iest-Guide )

List: int > sp > hit > spirit > haste > crit > mastery

Congo wrote:What is the major addons to use while pve'ing?


Everyone prefers different addons but I'd advise you to get grid (you'll need to dispel and assist the raid someway anyway as a priest), some unit frame addon, some actionbar addon and a dot timer is a must!

Our ranged dps UI discussion thread can be found here: viewtopic.php?f=20&t=352

Congo wrote:Any tips on leveling a shadow priest? :D


Leveling up is a great time to learn to use your trinket and mana cooldowns correctly.
