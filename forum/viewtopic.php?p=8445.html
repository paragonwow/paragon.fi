Playing an orc is pretty much a gamble though. In my gear you'd get 30 dps more for combat if you had an axe main hand, otherwise it's a 80 dps loss. Now combat *should* be the top spec for single target damage according to shadowcraft but most of the data shown in world of logs for example suggests that subtlety is a bit ahead, which often means that subtlety is what you're going to end up playing.

The other thing is that orcs do not have the utility of the other races which sometimes (though rarely) come into play. If you for example get back into melee range with the goblin jump even a second faster than an orc would, the goblin -- racial which isn't accounted for in the spreadsheets -- makes them vastly superior to anything else. Worgens get the best of both worlds with an extra sprint and the best passive racials. The last thing to keep in mind is that sometimes there are bosses that are beasts (chimaeron and bethilac in this expansion) and on those trolls just tend to be the top damage dealers even if they don't even know what their abilities do, which frankly is pretty stupid.
