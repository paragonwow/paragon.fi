This isn't ICC any more, you can't possibly have enough rage to just spam HS on cooldown, especially at the beginning of a fight. You should be saving Inner Rage and using it with Berserker Rage (If it's up) when there's a lot of raid damage going out, this is pretty much the only time you'll have enough Rage to support HSing on cooldown.

You're rotation (Priority) should be: BT > CS > RB > Slam, HS when above 70 Rage (50 during CS), or when Battle Trance procs. For SMF just switch Slam and RB. Try to keep your rage around 90 (without ever capping) outside of CS phases so you can get in 2 Heroic Strikes during CS. You also shouldn't just be using BShout and Berserker Rage on cooldown, BShout when you have absolutely nothing else to do or if you don't have enough rage to Bloodthirst. Berserker Rage should be saved to activate Raging Blow.
