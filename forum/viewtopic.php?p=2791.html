Unlike the slows and stuns afforded by other classes, the slows available to Death Knights during the Val'kyr phase are only available via dropping talent points into each respective slow (Chilblains/Desecration). It's simply not worth the DPS loss to spec 2 points into each for a marginal slow when other classes have full stuns and better talented slows. Typically, if you are having trouble with slowing the Val'kyr, it'd be more advisable to just spam Chains one of them. However, if you really don't have any classes that permit a slow or AoE stun [only for 25man] (Prot Warr, destro lock, etc.) you could probably talent into your slows. On that note though, maybe your other raid members are not doing their fair share of the work to get the Val'kyr down. At this point with the ICC raid buffs, you should have no problem killing a lone Val'kyr.


The only fight where putting talents into Desecration/Chilblains would be advised is probably HC Saurfang for the Blood Beasts. Even then, hunter traps, totems and even Prot Warr Shockwave would probably be enough.
