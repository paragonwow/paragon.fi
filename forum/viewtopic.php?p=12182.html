If you look even more closely to the Simulationcraft profile, you can see that the main hand weapon is in fact a caster weapon: http://www.wowhead.com/item=71777 . The basic idea is that you trade in a LOT of main hand dps for a huge chunk of spell power.

Consequences are that spells are becoming stronger - lava burst starts becoming worth using, and melee swings are becoming weaker. This, plus the fact that you would be casting a for a significant amount of time (which turns off your auto-attack), causes your melee swings to be quite low on the damage list.

This also doesn't answer your question, and I cannot provide numbers for lower gear levels, but it gives an idea about the motivation behind the enh_caster profile.

Simply put, you would need a caster main hand with relatively slow swing time (such as the caster axe mentioned above). But as Blizzard has announced (quite some time ago already) that they want to make sure enh doesn't use caster weapons, we can expect some kind of correction in this direction.
