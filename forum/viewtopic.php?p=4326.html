One could argue, Marchen, that the [Scientist's] stand at the top of the podium by virtue of the sole (PUN ALERT!) 277.

More often than not, however, the comparison is left hypothetical out of Holy's disproportionately low gain in face of mages, warlocks, shadow and discipline priests, holy paladins, elemental and restoration shaman, and balance druids, to all of which said ones come up best-in-slot by much more significant a margin. No, boots were indeed not the crown jewel of ICC's itemization.

The remaining duel, for reasons rather obvious, strays little from a mirror match, and the commercial model's ever-so-slight advantage of SPI > Crit is counterbalanced by an aesthetic disaster of an icon (see the final choice of Yours Truly, YMMV).
