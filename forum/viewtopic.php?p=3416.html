Hi! :)

I'm not sure if my rotation is the best one to use but I'm doing good dps on our ICC runs.

I use about the following rotation:

TotT (just before fight start) -> SS -> SS -> Killing Spree -> SnD -> SS (to 5CP) -> Rupture -> SS (to refresh SnD). I do not use Evicerate or Envenom in my rotation at all. CD's like BF and AR I pop everytime they are ready(if it fits the fight).

For example at the start of the fight you might want to pop AR so you can get your CP's up fast and so you get rupture and snd up.

TotT (tank)
Haste pot
