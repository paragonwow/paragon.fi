Proc weapons greatly benefit DW frost and Unholy sub-frost specced DKs. The reason is, the proc itself benefits from Frost talents like Black Ice, as well as Blood Presence and Ebon Plaguebringer or CoE if you have an unholy DK or lock in your raid. Simply put, being a DW frost DK, your talents and raid debuffs value the proc's damage more than the stats provided from any other weapon.

Moreover, this weapon's proc has NO internal cooldown, which makes it that much greater for AoE fights where Howling Blast and Blood Boil are used more. Additionally, it should be noted that the proc on this weapon can happen off any attack, ranged or melee.

All things considered, this weapon's slow speed and powerful proc are great for DW DKs who don't have access to 258-284 weapons yet.

Recent sims show Fierce Ametrines to be pulling ahead for both Unholy (sub Frost) and DW Frost. The DPS increase for DW Frost however, is not as high as it is for Unholy.... At max, 50 DPS. In fact, the Elitist Jerks Unholy DPS thread states that the DPS difference for even Unholy is marginal.

The extra haste makes more of a difference on fights with movement, which might be good for Unholy but which we know Frost doesn't like. This is one of the reasons I stay Unholy for Putricide, Blood Princes, etc.

I would recommend looking at your gear and deciding if it's worth it- personally I plan to wait.
