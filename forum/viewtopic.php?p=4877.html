Hello, I'm none of the warlocks-in-chief I believe you would rather see reply, but because so many of you seem uncertain about which stone you should use as demo, please forgive me for chipping in.

As a baseline I did a quick Simulationcraft run on my profile to determine my demo DPS without either stone (1000 iterations, 300 sec fights, patchwerk-style.) You should however bear in mind that I've played affliction 98% of the time since 3.3 and my gear isn't what you'd expect from a demo lock.

Average DPS: 12066
Scale factor for haste rating: 1.5535
Scale factor for crit rating: 1.1639

Now, instead of just simulating the results with both stones, let's first take the mathy approach to determine the stones' DPS values. I want to keep this as simple as possible which is why I will use the assumption that the 1% modifier from the stones is multiplicative with all the other modifiers for spell damage, which will further increase the inaccuracy of this post but presumably by a small enough margin. I'll also use the old scale factors to determine the value of combat ratings.

Firestone

1% increase in direct spell damage + 196 crit rating with 2/2 Master Conjuror
-According to simcraft direct damage spells were ~67.6% of total damage, thus

1,01(12066x0.676)-(12066x0.676)=~81,6 DPS
196*1,1639=~228.1 DPS

=~309,7 DPS

(I wasn't certain if Immolation would be considered direct damage or not, but I did not want to make my hypothesis that Spellstone is better to benefit from a possible mistake and thus assumed it would be DD.)

Spellstone

1% increase in periodic spell damage + 240 haste rating with 2/2 Master Conjuror
-According to simcraft periodic damage spells were ~13.6 of total damage, thus

1,01(12066x0.136)-(12066x0,136)=~16,4 DPS
240x15535=~372,1 DPS

=~388,5 DPS

Now I will run the simulationcraft to check if I got it right:

Simulation with Spellstone: 12451 DPS (+385 DPS)
Simulation with Firestone: 12387 DPS (+321 DPS)

These results seem to be well within the margins of my inaccurate math models, so based on this, I would conclude that Spellstone is indeed better. This however depends highly on your scale factors (which depend highly on your gear), so if you want to be perfectly certain, I'd warmly recommend you to consult your spreadsheet and simulationcraft with your own profile.

Sorry for the inaccuracies and possible mistakes.
Manni
