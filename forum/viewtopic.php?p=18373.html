Which T14 encounters do you find the most enjoyable?

14 Aug 2012 20:40

Kennyloggins
 
Posts: 1
Hello, this is Kennyloggins from Midwinter (US-Ysera). I made a survey to see how people would rank the T14 encounters relative to each other. You can see the results of the data I have collected here: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc ... wYlE#gid=0

I decided to start this project for no real reason other than to satisfy my curiosity. However, I think this type of collaboration among the raiding community is worth the participation, especially when communication between guilds can be rare. My goal is to get about 100 responses; after that I plan on doing more analysis, breaking down based on role, guild, etc.

If you wouldn't mind, take the time to fill this out! You can find the survey form here: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/vie ... THhWc0E6MQ

Reminder: when filling out the form, make sure you rank based on a STRICT ORDERING. That means you must give each boss a unique rank (1 being favorite, 16 being least favorite).

If you want to have access to the spreadsheet itself, you may contact me at [email protected] .

Thanks for reading, I hope you find this interesting.

Edit: I would really like to see tanks and healers respond, since they are significantly fewer in number than DPS.
