With typing /uf in chat you can move every unitframe including Raid-/Party-/Unitframes including the Petframe.

Offtopic:
Tukui is based on Ouf (Otravi unitframes). At release of Patch 4.0.1 there was an taint issue with /uf so tukz (Author of tukui) removed it a short time from the commands.lua. At the moment it works fine.


Short slashcommands overview:

/uf - Move Unitframes
/mss - Move Stancebar
/hb - hover buttons
