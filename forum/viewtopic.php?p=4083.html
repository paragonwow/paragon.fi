edgon wrote:I'm wondering which glyphs I should be using in ICC10/25-mans (normal). So far I've healed with swiftmend+nourish+reju but I'm not sure should I use RR or WG instead?

This really depends on the encounter. In 10-man, you might not always see the full benefit of the WG glyph, but it's very good in 25-man.

RR is good in 25-man fights where you don't need to blanket the raid, like Saurfang, and in 10-man fights where raid damage is extensive and shaped quite evenly, like BQL.

I usually run with SM + Nourish + WG for both 10-man and 25-man now during farm. During progression I swap to whatever's best for a particular fight.

Since you have limited raid access, I won't really comment on gear selection.

You want more haste. You need 735 rating with 3/3 CF and 856 rating without to reach the minimum gcd. That's counting all buffs, which you might not have available in a 10-man setting anyway.

I was going to write that you ought to change your gemming to haste for yellow slots, but you seem to already have done that since I initially looked at your armory profile. You could go even further and gem pure haste to yellow sockets, and perhaps even sp/haste to red sockets, but you have the general idea now. Your meta gem choice is also quite... unorthodox. Use the insightful meta.

You should also spec 3/3 CF from balance. The exact talents you should drop for this depend greatly on your healing style and how much tank healing you're doing. As for glyphs, I'd personally go for some combination of Swiftmend, WG, Nourish, and Rapid Rejuvenation.

Also, if you're looking to optimize, drop herbalism in favor of some crafting profession that grants you throughput bonuses.
