Hi,

It's my personal opinion that Skada is better than recount. I used recount for several tiers of content and fairly recently, in retrospect, made the switch. There's a few reasons I like Skada more than recount and I'll list them out:
  • Skada seems to be updated quicker when new content comes out and work better for that matter
  • Skada causes less errors (in my experience) and less crashes
  • Skada has the ability to show Enemy damage taken. Probably the biggest reason of all that I use Skada
  • Skada tacks what happens before someone's death
  • It's easier to switch between different views in Skada. No clicking arrows 100 time to swap from dps to heals.
  • You can disable parts of skada that are not important to you
  • Skada has additional mods you can download for it (like Ignite munching for Fire Mages)
  • Skada tracks dps as (Damage Done)/(time in fight). Recount does (damage done)/(time active in fight). Damage done is better, but this gives more realistic dps.
  • Skada also has a few other features in terms of damage analysis that recount does not.

The one thing I miss that Recount has and Skada does not, is a live dps graph. Not something I should really be looking at in a fight, but I like to know what works and what doesn't. The feature was helpful but not necessary by any means and also caused latency issues in some cases.

I hope this helps you decide :)

-Guromin
