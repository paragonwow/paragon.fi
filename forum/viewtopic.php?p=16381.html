The proc more than makes up for the lack of stats.

with Sword in mh as Orc you require 781 expertise rating (you are currently @ 815).

With 2 pc T13 stat priorities are: 26 exp > Hit (to 17% marker) >= Crit (equal to Hit at 17%) > Haste > Mastery
