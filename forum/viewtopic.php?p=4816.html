I'm not Kruf but heres a list of macros that I use:

Steady Shot

#showtooltip Steady Shot
/cast !auto shot
/cast Silencing Shot
/cast [target=pettarget,exists] Kill Command
/cast Steady Shot

Aimed Shot

#showtooltip Aimed Shot
/cast !auto shot
/cast Silencing Shot
/cast [target=pettarget,exists] Kill Command
/cast Aimed Shot(Rank 9)

Multi Shot

#showtooltip Multi-Shot
/cast !auto shot
/cast Silencing Shot
/cast [target=pettarget,exists] Kill Command
/cast Multi-Shot(Rank 8)

Kill Shot

#showtooltip Kill Shot
/cast !auto shot
/cast Silencing Shot
/cast [target=pettarget,exists] Kill Command
/cast Kill Shot(Rank 3)

Serpent Sting

#showtooltip Serpent Sting
/cast !auto shot
/cast Silencing Shot
/cast Serpent Sting(Rank 12)
/cast [target=pettarget,exists] Kill Command

Chimera Shot

#showtooltip Chimera Shot
/cast !auto shot
/cast Silencing Shot
/cast [target=pettarget,exists] Kill Command
/cast Chimera Shot

Misdirection ( Will MD Your pet if you don't have focus targer )

#showtooltip
/cast [@focus,help] Misdirection; [help] Misdirection; [@pet,exists] Misdirection
/cast Kill Command


If you wonder about the "!auto shot" that will force a auto shot before or during the cast so you will start shooting faster.

I only looked at festergut, but your warrior didn't sunder once. Sunder Armor increases your personal dps by a lot, but it also increases the dps of all melee classes which will make the fight shorter and that will also increase your dps. Also, your festergut fight was 230 seconds long and you only had 21 chimera shots and 20 aimed shots, meaning you could have gotten in 2 more chimera shots or 3 more aimed shots (Depending which one you use first, Aimed > Chimera IMO) if you pay attention and don't cast steady shot when Aimed/Chimera is available. Your log also shows that you were moving around way too much, you only casted steady shot 69 times, in a fight that lasts 3:50 you should easily have 75+ steady shots. You also never casted Arcane Shot when you were moving, so you were running and doing no damage to the boss when you could have used Arcane Shot.

Edit: You also only shot 96 auto shots, meaning you didn't stop to let auto-shots go off while you were running. Get an auto-shot timer (Quartz, AzCastBar, etc.) and learn how to stutter step if you want to increase your dps. There are logs of Hunters on WoL with 30+ more auto shots in fights that are about the same length as the one you posted.

Edit2: You have 2 agi/hit gems in your gear that you don't need, you should use the +20 hit glove enchant and change those 2 gems to armor pen, since armor pen is by far your best stat until you are hard capped. The gems in your staff are also wrong, get rid of the agi/crit and ArP/stam gems and gem pure ArP.. the bonus isn't worth it. Until you are ArP hard capped you should have a red ArP gem in every slot, and a Nightmare tear in a blue socket with a good bonus (t10 chest) to fulfill the meta gem requirements.
