Hey bobbo,

I'll just get straight to your questions but i'd like to point out that disc priests are very versatile and there are many different styles of play, so my way may or may not be the best for you.


What should i socket as disc priest and why ?
-Often times people like go for SP. This is a good choice for overall play. If you want to take a more different approach you might want to try stacking up haste or even MP5. For beginners I recommend SP and some haste on the side. Once you get better and improve you might want to stack more haste, depending on your comp of course.


What trinkets are best for PvP ?
-If you are not human, pick the 264 ilvl PvP trinket as your first choice. After that it's pretty much free for all. You can try getting 258 solace. The battlemaster trinket is always a solid one, though. It can save your life if you are being silenced and interrupted a lot while focused.

What does spell penetration do ?
-It decreases the amount of resists. Try getting around 130.

What should i enchant ?
-The basic resilience and SP/resi chants will do fine. For legs SP/stam will do fine.

Is renew any good to use in PvP ?
-Sometimes. I use it randomly on people taking decent damage. Often times a prom, a PW:S or a penance is better, though. Also, if you are being attacked you can throw it on yourself (figures).

When i play vs like dks, palas, locks, etc. That can heal them selves, i go oom. How should i do vs those classes ?
-If you are being overgeared it's pretty much a certain loss. But against the same level of gear you do stand a chance. A lock can be a tricky one if he manages to get a spell lock on your cast and a few fears as well. The dots will eat you away.
Good DKs are hard to beat without reflective shield. The same goes for palas. After DK has used his trinket, AMS and all the other 1000 cooldowns you get an opportunity to try and burst him down, assuming that he is around 70% HP. Throw a fear, dot him up if you haven't already and burst him with everything you've got. Holy fire, smites, SW:D and if possible hand rockets.

What should i use my JC gems for ?
-If you are lacking resilience go for it. Otherwise you can choose freely from SP, haste and even MP5.


About my spec:
-Reflective Shield, Improved Flash Heal, Divine Aegis (full), Blessed Recovery, Spell Warding and Holy Specialization.
-Reflective Shield is best for 2v2. Probably could use it a double healer setup as well but don't have the spare points.
-Imp. FH is not really needed. Simple as that.
-Divine Aegis is good if you have enough crit and SP. I have gemmed/geared for haste so I lack crit a bit too much. I just keep it as a trash buff.
-Blessed Recovery gives a slight bonus if you are being focused. It also an expensive talent since it requires 3 points if maxed (or if you want an additional trash buff then it's only one). I don't have any real need for it.
-Spell Warding is good, I don't deny that. I find Divine Fury to be better due to the ability to do burst damage when needed with HF+smite.
-Holy Spec. is good if you have DA maxed. It's a tough choice whether to take 3/3 imp. renew or 3/5 holy spec. The crit is usually better.



what is your casting time on smite, in pvp gear and pvp specc ?
-1.5 seconds UB. 1.2 with borrowed time or PI and 1 sec with both.


Hope this was helpful.
