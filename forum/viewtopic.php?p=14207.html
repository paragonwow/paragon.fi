We run such aoe-heavy compositions on the boss that I really don't need to help on the molten elementals. You should probably aim to do the same, I bet there are lot of things for your aoe classes to improve on.

I guess what you could do is stand away from the group and blade flurry your elemental down / go fok the pack of elementals if you don't get one dropped on yourself. They fixate to the target they drop on, until they die or their target gets killed. When I tried this myself I needed AR and/or evasion to survive it alone but with the recent nerfs to their hp values I think it might be doable without them as long as you take as little damage from the explosion as possible.
