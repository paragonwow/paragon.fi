Even though you killed it already i might add, that a frost mage with Blizzard on adds will help alot on the adds.. Makes life for hunters ALOT easier, and will increase their DPS significantly. You should also get a destru warlock with Shadowfury (you are already), and i can also see in your WoL that you don't use a Demonology warlock either, this will increase your raid's overall spellpower by ~ 250.

A good tip is to assign adds for the ranged DPS... We did that, and i can see our ranged DPS are doing ~ 700 DPS more than yours on average :D
Congrats on the kill :P
