HI!

I'd love to have a shot at applying to Paragon, but sadly, skillzzz aside, I don't meet most of the criteria....however, speaking FInnish is one I do, even as a foreigner.

I just wanted to post this to give encouragement to any non-Finn wanting to learn Finnish. It is possible! A great way to learn/improve is to join a Finnish-speaking guild, or at least one that has quite a few Finnish members. I started my raiding in a Finnish guild (big up to any old <Kenit> guys!), who helped me to learn Finnish a lot. I then moved on to co-founding a couple of guilds with a mix of Finns and other nationalities, and now find myself happily back in a Finnish guild once again (props to <The Esoteric Order>!!), and realising how much I've improved in the 4 years since my first Finnish language gaming experience! :)

Tsemppiä kaikille suomenkieli opintoihin!
