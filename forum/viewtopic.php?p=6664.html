Switch to glyph of disease for LK if you haven't already.

1) It's not number 1 prio or anything but with GoD you will spread your diseases to them eventually.

1) Yes. It adds the spell damage taken debuff on Shamblers and gives some extra Wandering Plague damage onto LK

2) No, as long as the tank isn't playing with his feet. WP will be splashing out some random AoE damage - this is what will possibly over-aggro and kill you. Every single tanking class has abilities to secure the mob's aggro though, as long as they don't suck

3) Blood boil is a terrible idea. The goal is to maximize your damage on LK, not to pad meters. That kind of stuff would definitely make my blood boil
