Im no member but advice comes free :)

Yes it is possible to do it, and no 25 men hc and how it got killed for the first time ever has no comparison value what so ever.

To BL/hero/tm 2 times on the fight is possible, but the only phase you really need to be anal of the dps (the phase you want to be over as fast as possible) is the third phase, with 2 healers and good dps you can manage to get 1 meteor in P3, that will give you more dmg boost on boss then using 2x bl/hero/tm.

So my 2cents is, go with 2 healers if you have the setup for it, i think you will save much time in the end (it still will take some time if your not really the tip top, but then again if you were he would been down at this point)

And for the love of all that is sacred get a boomer in :p
