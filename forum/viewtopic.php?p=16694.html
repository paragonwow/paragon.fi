Flamage,

Option 3: Don't use combustion until Ice Tombs spawn and then take a 20k ignite and spread it :)

If you want the most damage, and the most effective damage it's better to wait until the tombs spawn and spread it. Also, I'm not sure what the difference is between your two options... Combustion is not affected by damage boosters, so if you cast it while it's up on a 30k ignite or while it's off on a 30k ignite it will do the same amount of damage. The dots need the buff, not Combustion.
