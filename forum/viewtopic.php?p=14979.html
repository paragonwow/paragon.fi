Hello:)

I'm not a boomkin from paragon, but I think I should be able to help you out a bit.
Firstly when I check your armory there's room for improvement there aswell:)
You have your shoulders unenchanted while you are in fact exalted with therazane,
aswell as that your chest and gloves are unenchanted. Then if the you have the money for it you could buy
Darkmoon card volcano and replace the brewfast trinket. Though the patch is coming soon:)

Then you have starsurge glyphed, which is actually only good on rather uncommon situations.
The only fights in firelands where it could be useful would be Ryoloth and Beth'ilac. If you are looking for a fast dps increase though, wrath is the way to go! Then in the major glyphs section you don't have glyph of rebirth, which is in fact essential for any situation. You don't want to combat ress someone and watch him die 2 seconds later.

In your talent spec you have lunar shower specced, which again is only good in rare situations and for more experienced players. (if you really know what you're doing you might get a little extra dps)

On to your gameplay questions!:)
On Shannox you shouldn't use mushrooms at the start, since Shannox is in fact untauntable. You could however cast a mushroom on the move and detonate it when you are moving again, but since on this fight the dogs and the boss are moving constantly your mushrooms will most likely hit critters. Furthermore you say that you're using starfire on riplimb and rageface while you are using the nuke tactic. Bad idea! Just follow your eclipse bar and in case no one in your raid is able to get rageface of someone cast starfire. Since everyone is on him the dog will go off the target nearly instantly.

On ryoloth you should be dotting the adds along with starfall and mushrooms. Your last resort is hurricane:) Not your first aoe choice. The mana cost is huge and the dps is really low. So if you have all dots up mushrooms placed and detonate on cd you could cast hurricane. For the rest it's a good idea to dot the legs. If you don't have a feral your melee will love you if you cast 3 fearie fires on them aswell.

On beth'ilac you're right to starfall here of course!:) Furthermore i like to dot the hanging spiderlings (though preferably the ones who are taunted down, died a lot here:D) and mushroom the spinners. You can also typhoon them back.

On alysrazor normal there is really no way to improve your dps on the ground^^. Though remember that the bird keeps flying circles, follow the bird with your camera and dps the bird when the initiates are down. Good idea to help on the hatchlings aswell though. (depends on your tanks dps)

On majodermo i must confess i really don't have a clue either (it's my alt:P) What I am doing atm is that i place my mushrooms on the stack spot where the boss is misdirected to. Then i start off with prepot treants starfall insect swarm moonfire wrath starsurge, when i have danced back from lunar to solar i detonate my mushrooms. However its a better idea to wait till you need to spread out and detonate on the move of course:)
On spreading out you should help out the raid by doing a stampeding roar.

On ragnaros its simply attack boss till transition make sure your starfall is off cd. Put shrooms under the add spawn points.
Starfall detonate and cast wrath/starfire. Possibly dot a bit aswell. If they get close typhoon them away, if one goes close go cat and stun it. Phase 2 depends on the tactic youre using what you need to do. With whatever tactic though, use sprint and stampeding roar. Make sure you're in solar preshroom the spot youre going to. Starfall detonate dot a bit and hurricane:)
Second transition is the same as the first but just make sure your cds are up for bl. Pop cds on the 2 lava scions when the small adds are down, but 1. Nukey nuke and loot! Just make sure you dont get a meteor on your face.

I don't really use a lot of macros. Keybinding, however, is a different story.
I have a Razer Naga mouse, and have a bind for [Wild Mushroom] and also one for detonate. Much easier to manage that than using it on my bars.

I enjoy the starfox action for alysrazor so i made a castsequence macro bind on my middle mouse button to make refreshing dots one less thing to focus on.

/castsequence reset=2 insect swarm, moonfire, sunfire

*it puts up IS and hitting the bind again puts up either MF or SF, depending where I am on my eclipse bar.

in the menu options, you there's an option which lets you hold down say, 'shift' and click a spell and have it cast @focus target. Pretty handy for a fight you want to roll multiple targets with dots: Reg Shannox, (Valiona/Theralion in BoT), etc..
