If you can stack the seeds with your group, you can still do moderate amount of AoE to take them low on HP and then be spread out and tackle them individually. The AoE requirement for 10M doesn't really require much "class stacking", as you only have 8 Molten Elementals to worry about which don't cap your AoE abilities (20 Elementals in 25-man cap AoE spells hard, making them only 50% effective.)

If you feel like you can do it like us, it's easily worth trying as it allows you to get more DPS time on the boss, and in 10M you will have time to spread out if your AoE fails and the Elementals won't deal too much damage. You can also do a middle-ground strategy where you stack the Seeds and then spread out and do whatever AoE you can to take them as low on health as you can. If you're lacking in coordination/timers to stack the seeds or you feel like AoE doesn't work for you at all you can go for the "Method" strategy of tackling them individually - it's a bit easier to organize in 10M where you have a lot of room to maneuver in without worrying about the Elementals getting stacks :)

Here's a tip that works in either strategy: Make sure you have addons that give warnings when you have aggro from anything, and you will know if no Molten Elementals are fixated on you - in which case you don't have to worry about getting attacked by them, and you can focus on helping those that are likely to need help (ie. Healers.)
