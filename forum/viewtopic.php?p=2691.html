So, due to a recent discussion on WoW forums I'd like to post a suggestion for how to open as unholy, when you are to roll as powerful diseases as possible (assuming you get ToTT on pull).
http://forums.wow-europe.com/thread.htm ... 0531&sid=1

What we've concluded in the above mentioned thread is that the best opener as it is now is:
DnD+AotD (Prepull) - grants you Advantage, 3% damage.
BS - Grants you Desolation, 5% damage.
PS - Grants you Blood Plague, which at least gives you 10% FF damage through Rage of Rivendaire.
IT - Applies Frost Fever.
(If no other DK applied BP and your rogue doesn't have ToTT glyph or the unglyphed ToTT was insanely awesomely timed) PS - Grants you 10% disease damage to BP through Rage of Rivendaire.

Any comments are welcome.
