Averaged mp5 value of Int trinkets (based on my Int, ~5k):

Tyrande's doll (350 mp5)
Tsunami (~240 mp5)
Fall of Mortality (N) (~230 mp5 at best, may be much less depending on procs)
Core of Ripeness (~192 mp5 if used on cooldown)
Tear of Blood (~195 mp5)
Figurine - Dream Owl (JC only, ~142 mp5 if used on cooldown)

Don't count on Tyrande's doll, I'm barely under 10k NE fragments right now, still no proc. That leaves Fall of Mortality if you're lucky and other trinkets which are farmable.

Without Mana Tide the best would be Tsunami and Tear of Blood, or Core of Ripeness and Tear of Blood if you can't afford Tsunami.

Mana Tide however changes the picture, boosting Core of Ripeness to ~465 mp5 and Dream Owl to ~344 mp5 if used in conjunction with Mana Tide for 15 sec every 3 min. This is under ideal circumstances.

Please correct me if I'm mistaken.
