DMC is still BiS for lot of casters due to his insane uptime and his dmg dealt.
Necro is tecnically a good choiche for demo, thing is that blizzard screwed up a bit about mastery values in demon form.

This effect doesn't works for every trinket that procs mastery, and that's why for example Moonwel Chalice (ilvl365) gives about 800dps more than Theralion's Mirror(ilvl372).
