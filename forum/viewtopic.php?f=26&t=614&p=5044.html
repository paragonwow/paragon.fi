Hey Bud,

If you have the ability to access a spreadsheet, it would definitely help you alot. You could punch in stats with or without ArPen gems to see the results.

As far as haste goes; it is one of the top stats for a combat rogue.

Hit (to cap) > Exp (to cap)
ArPen > Haste > Crit

Haste, from any sources whether it be BL/Heroism, Haste Pots, SnD, will always benefit you not only due to the fact that you are striking your opponent more often for melee damage and poison procs, but it also gives you those lovely Combat Potency procs (Main benefit of haste). +15 Energy more often due to increased strikes will cause you to see definitive DPS increases. Once again, spreadsheets will prove this point.

The values of different stats depend on the level of your gear. For example at near-bis level armor pen / spell hit cap / expertise are often the best ones with white hit, haste and agility hovering around 2,0 ep. As Berginyon said, you'll be best off by downloading a spreadsheet and checking the values for your own gear as the values can differ greatly.
