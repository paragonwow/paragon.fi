@Revelation

I dont see anything wrong with your gearing and about the legendary+haste I havent done much research myself on the matter but I think there was some discussion about it in the EJ forums. Getting the legendary wont affect you by much, atleast you shouldnt gem for pure haste, im not sure about str/haste gems tho. The main reason ppl get engi is not the haste enchant but the nitro boost which can be really useful in fights with lots of movement. Excluding nitro boost engineering and blacksmithing are at the same level but as engi, you have to use saronite bombs which some ppl would rather not do but if you get to get even couple hits more than others because of your nitro boosts it outweights blacksmithing easily.

You cant use 2x Whispering Fanged Skull so heroic Skull + Herkuml is the best choice. Herkuml is clearly better than Greatness. Haste is actually one of the best stats for ret when you get your tier10 bonuses.
