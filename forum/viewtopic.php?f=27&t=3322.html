http://www.paragon.fi/forum/viewtopic.php?f=20&t=1759

I know its not what you were looking for in asking paradar about his ui and i really cant help much with setting yours up like his, but this is definitely the best base ui i have found so far. In something like page 14 devai has also uploaded the strings for his Power auras so you can copy and paste those and simply switch the spells that you need to track from the firelands ones to the DS ones. GET POWER AURAS CLASSIC if you do not have it already - makes avoiding damage, eg shockwave, far far easier.

The only thing I have changed with this is i use a different castbar addon, mostly because the elvui one annoys me when my ms is high. also i recommend using http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/serenity which is an all-in-one hunter CD/focus/proc/reminder tracker. Other then that it really doesnt matter what addons you use, just make sure that the information you want to see is in a place on your screen that you can easily see it.

Also I dont currently have a recent shot of my ui on my hunter, but if you would like one to see what i am talking about I am more then willing to upload one and post it here for viewing.
