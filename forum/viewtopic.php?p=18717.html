Ask and you shall receive, it's WeakAuras btw. add-on with a lot more customization options than PowerAuras.
The ability cooldown "trackers" are just my Bartender actionbars with OmniCC, other spell icons you see are all done with WA.

There really isn't anything too special about my WA configs, most of the stuff are just simple icons with specific auras. I'm not gonna write up a WeakAuras 101 here though, something like that probably exists in the depths of the Internet already. I strongly recommend people to look it up and learn how to use it, it might very well be the most useful add-on ever made (you can practically do anything with it, given enough time.)

Good luck setting up your UI ^^

List of relevant add-ons:

Bartender (action bars)
WeakAuras (tracking all the buffs & debuffs)
BigWigs (bossmod)
Prat (chat)
Omen Threat Meter (threat meter)
OmniCC (cooldown tracker for action bars)
Shadowed Unit Frames (unit frames)
SexyMap (a better looking minimap)
Tidy Plates:Threat Plates (nameplate add-on)
TipTac (tooltip add-on)
SatrinaBuffFrame (hiding blizzard's default buff frames)
MikScrollingBattleText (combat text)
Grid (raid frames)
