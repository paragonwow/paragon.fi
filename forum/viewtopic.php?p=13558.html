I'm no super-enhancement shaman by far, but I will offer my advice nonetheless =)

Gemming: Don't go for socket bonuses unless the outcome is more agility then from pure agility gems. The other stats come in abundance through reforging, so agility is the #1 priority when gemming.

Mastery: this is the best secondary stat by far! The #1 priority should be to have this on every piece of gear (not including trinkets and weapons, different mechanics apply here)

Haste: this is the least valuable stat for us. As opposed to other classes, we do not scale well at all with haste. The only thing it affects is our white damage. We get no lowered cooldowns, and we have no spammable abilities that would benefit from increased resource regen.

Crit: the second most awful stat. More useful then haste in that it affects all our abilities.

Hit: should be stacked to 17% and no more. This ensures that our special attacks will always hit, and after that we can stack stats that affect those attacks.

Expertise: the dodgecap is 26/26, get it.

Rotation and spells: hard to read his exact rotation from the logs, but some things do stand out.
20,5% uptime on flame shock is awful!! This should be as close to 100% as possible.

I would recommend this spell prio:

#1 Searing totem (place, refresh)
#2 Stormstrike
#3 Lava Lash
#4 Unleash Elements
#5 Lightning Bolt (@ 5 stacks maelstrom weapon)
#6 Flame Shock (place, refresh)
#7 Earth Shock
#8 Use spirit wolves when your heart desires, or in burn phases.

Glyphs: Stormstrike and Feral Spirit are not that good glyphs. Rather go for Windfury and Flame Shock for more passive damage and better cooldown management.
