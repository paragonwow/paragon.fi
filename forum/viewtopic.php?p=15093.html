Hi,

Go for the 4 piece T12 bonus.
The +12% parry you gain after Divine Protection expires goes into avoidance, and avoidance takes precedence over mitigation in the formula. Let's say you have the 4 piece T12 and 16% dodge, 16% parry and 65,4% block. This gives you 37% avoidance (dodge,parry and miss) and 65,4% mitigation (block) for a Combat Table Coverage (CTC) of 102,4%. When you get +12% parry you'll have 49% avoidance and only 53,4% mitigation for the full CTC (the waste goes into block as it is a mitigation stat). Hope this helps.
