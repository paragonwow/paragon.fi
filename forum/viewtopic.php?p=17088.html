Fizziks,

Hi. I looked over your logs and I noticed a few things. For whatever reason you didn't blood lust, which is a fairly significant dps increase and can profoundly change the size of an initial combustion. It both makes ignite stacking easier and increases the number of ticks in combustion.

Your raid composition is also missing a few buffs (3% damage is a big one).

Your first combustion was weak and your 3rd was outstanding. Ideally, you should aim for every single combustion to be strong, but I realize RNG plays a large factor in that.

Your reforging appears to be correct, so no comments on gear. Living Bomb application also appears to be good.

There are no warlocks in your raid so you need to keep up Critical Mass. There were a few extended periods where Critical Mass fell off and believe me, that hurt your damage. On a good attempt Fireball should crit about 45% of the time (with your gear). For you, it only crit 36% of the time. That is more than just RNG. This also affected your Pyroblast! damage because that will normally crit less and you get less of them. Keep Critical mass up with a scorch if it is going to fell off.

Do not use Flame Orb if the boss is going to die before it has done all of it's damage. It wont give you the same damage per second if it only hits the boss for half the time it's out.

For the most part it looks like you have a good handle on Fire mage dps, but the 10m lacking buffs and combustion damage are holding you back.

-Guromin
