Bracer im waiting for the enchanter in my guild to buy the recipe which hes doing today he says.

Haggeragge90,

Mages are not supposed to be top damage on meters or be there for just the 3% damage to raid. There are other classes that can provide that same effect like Ret paladins. Mages are not the top damaging class for every fight even though there a few fights where they shine far above other classes on damage. As far as your damage is concerned, the biggest issue is gear. You can't really expect to be the top damage wearing pvp gear and using sub-optimal enchants. I know you mentioned it already so it is at least good your aware.

It's rather interesting how you think of the conservation phase as a damage loss. Think of it instead as the best way to handle a somewhat bad situation. You could defiantly spam down to 0% mana instead of your conservation phase, but what then. You would do even less damage than you would have if you were at full mana. Think of your initial burst damage as burst and not the standard for your dps. Your rotation is really pretty good. There's a few situation tips and tricks you would always add into it, but since I am seriously busy lately I will just link you to a site that has everything you will ever really need to know.

http://elitistjerks.com/f75/t118084-cat ... ompendium/

Read the comments. They can tell you a lot that helps you.

As far your reforging is concerned. It's the right idea. I can tell you get the idea, but there's a slightly better way to do it. It would really only result in about 20-50 more dps which is pretty insignificant. Anything that returns a simulationcraft increase in damage of 100 or more is significant. To get your most optimal gear set, use Rawr in combination with Simulationcraft. You can of course google them both to find a download link and google how to use them if you don't know. I'm always happy to answer specific questions if you have them so just leave a reply.

Sorry for not being incredibly specific, but I hope it points you in the right direction.

-Guromin
