I was going to just link thread from elitist jerks but I noticed they don't seem to have any compentium for unholy death knight so here is the unholy rotation in very simplified form.


For singletarget dps

First, use Unholy Presence.

1. Outbreak Frost Fever Blood Plague
2. Dark Transformation
3. Death and Decay
4. Scourge Strike
5. Festering Strike
6. Death Coil

Your first priority is to keep diseases up. Apply them by using outbreak and use enough festering strike to make them last until your outbreak is off cd again. If you somehow manage to drop diseases when outbreak is on cd you can apply them also with icy touch and plague strike but you should not need to do that if you use enough festering strike.

Second priority is to have death and decay always on the ground and on cd. After that you should use scourge strike with your unholy and death runes when ever they are up and death and decay is on cd. When your runes are not up use death coil and use dark transformation when it procs.

Festering Strike whenever you don't have Blood and Frost Runes, or need to extend your disease duration soon.


For AoE dps

1.Pestilence
2.Blood Boil

To several targets your roation is same as above exept you spread diseases with pestilence to all targets and use blood boil with your blood runes. You can also use icy touch to get rid of frost runes.


Cooldowns

Horn of Winter
Summon Gargoyle
Unholy Frenzy
Army of the Dead
Doing dps is a science, tanking is a job and healing is an art.
