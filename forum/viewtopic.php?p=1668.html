There is a thing called critcap, according to ej it goes something like this:
There is a 27% miss chance on lev83 bosses, 4,8% reduced crit chance, 24% chance for getting glancing attack (and ofc you know that glancing attacks can't crit) and 6,5% chance to get dodge when you attack bosses from behind.
I get 15,37% hit from gear on my character + 6% from talents so that's 21,37%, expertise on cap so bosses can't dodge when I hit them from behind, so my critcap is: 100%-(27-21,37)-0-24-4,8 = 65,57%
My character gets 51,42% crit from gear, about 11% more from raidbuffs and debuffs on boss and then 9% from elemental devastation (which has about 90% uptime according to enhsim). So my total crit is about 71,42 in raids and thats way over critcap still. Cause of critcap I got weird values for hit from enhsim so tried to even it out by gemming some :p
