Getting the Val'kyrs stunned is really just about assigning someone to stun a specific mark. If the person who was supposed to stun star for example and got picked up, then the back-up stunner for that mark should be stunning it.

It's not rocket surgery when you practice it a few times. Kidney shot (or was it cheap shot?) also doesn't go on the same diminish with rest of the stuns, which is very handy at times.
