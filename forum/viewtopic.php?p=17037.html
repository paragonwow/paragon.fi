First, I think you know that, you should stack critical strike as much as you can, so I think you shouldn't reforge your crit on your feet and wrists.

Go for a 20 caracteristic on your chest and 65 crit on your back.
And don't forget to precall call of the wild before each boss, it's 10 ~ 15s with 10% attack power for you and your pet, wich is great for your opening.
