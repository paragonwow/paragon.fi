Hey,

Using or skipping the 4 set bonus is rather straightforward - use it on fights where you're spamming rejuvenation. That probably means nearly every fight. I don't see much point in over-optimizing between set pieces and non-set pieces between specific fights at this point in the game. My advice is to simply get the 4-piece bonus and stick with it.

As for talents, the determining factor is pretty much what you want from balance. Before you can get 856 haste from gear, you should spec into CF (18/0/53). Once your haste is high enough, you want to drop down to either 14 or 11 points. After that it's a matter of opinion and play style and level of progression if you want to grab NG or not, and what talents to skip from the resto tree. I'd advice against skipping Revitalize, because with the current healing style of spamming rejuvenation and wg, it adds quite a bit of raid dps.

The heroic Halion trinket has the most spellpower out of any trinket, so obviously it's a sweet pick if you're not running out of mana. Quite obviously it's of more use to other classes though.

Following those lines, gear for 856 haste. I'd also get 3/3 revitalize. If you find you're not using the nourish glyph much, that's the first one you should replace for some other glyph - which one depends on your play style.
arx / xaar
