The Vendetta glyph is simply worse overall than the Backstab glyph. Above 35% you should be using Muti to build finishers of at least 4 points, keeping rupture up whilst using envenom to refresh SnD. Below 35% you should begin using backstab to build 5 point finishers instead, this is due to the fact that we gain 30 energy from Murderous Intent when we backstab a target below 35% health, effectively making the cost of backstab half of what it was.
