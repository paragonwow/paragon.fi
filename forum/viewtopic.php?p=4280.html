INSTALLATION

I will not take any responsibility if my files completely messes up your UI.

1. Backup your WoW Directory's Interface, WTF, and Fonts folders. I normally just copy them to the desktop. Make sure they are out of your World of Warcraft folder.

2. Download Aftermathh UI and extract all three file folders to your WoW Directory.

3. In the new WTF file folder, currently in your WoW Directory, go to the Account folder and change the name of the ACCOUNTNAME folder to the name of your WoW account.

4. Explore this folder and change the name of the SERVERNAME folder to the name of your Main Character's server.

5. Explore this folder and change the name of the CHARNAME folder to the name of your Main Character.

6. Open WoW.

7. Click the "Options" button on the login screen, followed by the "Video" button and make sure your resolution is set to 1680x1050 and all of your video effects are how you want them.

8. Login and choose your character's server. Click the "AddOns" button on the lower left corner of your character screen. Make sure the "Load Out of Date AddOns" option at the top of the AddOns window is checked.

9. Log your character in and enjoy your interface. A few tweaks may need to be made to some of the addons. Further instructions will be added to assist you with these issues.

10. The profiles are called either Aftermath/Muqq
