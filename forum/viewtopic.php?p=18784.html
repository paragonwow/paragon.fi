Hey, haste is good especially in 10mans. It gives you good survivability via increased hp generation (higher SotR uptime) and also more healing + dps which can be a pretty big impact with less players. In 25man bosses hit harder and you don't necessarily need to contribute so much on the dps/healing so stamina (and mastery, which is not bad in 10man either) is more valuable in comparison.

I'm not sure about guides @ the internets, there should be something in EJ paladin section at least.

Hope this helps :)
