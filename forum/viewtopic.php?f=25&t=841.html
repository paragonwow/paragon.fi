As long as threat generation isn't an issue (which it isn't on that particular encounter), it's more than fine. Will of the Necropolis is great again nowadays, but if you're lacking melee buffs then your spec is good.

What you can affect is how you handle everything. Is the enrage on adds getting removed? Do you use your cooldowns? Another easy way to handle them is building up some initial threat, after that you can just slap Chains of Ice and watch them crawl, never landing an enraged or frenzied hit.
