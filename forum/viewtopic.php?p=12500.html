My opinion here:

There are fights in FL that AGI based bears can be better then stamina based, but i stay with stamina gear

Point 1: More health pool more time the healers have to heal you this is fact and this is very effective on your survivability, more HP with the big cds make you survive better.
Point 2: Most of the fights has elemental damage, what means dodge cannot mitigate.
Point 3: The beginning of the fight is the most critical time to a stamina drood, but after some hits vengeance show it's real deal and threat show not to be a problem.

Agi bears:
2 specific fights for me baleroc with solo tanker and shannox. These two fights agi bear have a greater survivability, i usually get 40%+ dodge with my gear and they give my healers less work so they can keep up some mana, last week baleroc just hit me with 2 decimation blades so on these fights i recommend an agi based gear.

My group is starting to make tries on majordomu and it seems that stamina is good on him too!

I hope i could help a little! Good work on your raids!(luck is for noobs!)
