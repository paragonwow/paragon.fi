On the contrary, nobody knows if 10M is harder or not on most bosses. I haven't really seen any good argument on why they would theoretically be harder or any proof why they would technically be harder, apart from Aberrations on Maloriak and Twilight Sentries on V&T, both of which I assume have been fixed. Perhaps the reason for such design flaws is that there probably wasn't much testing done on 10HC in beta, which is why they might have gone unnoticed to live.

I rather doubt any individual or guild has extensively done both 10HC and 25HC, so nobody really seems to be in a position to give a fair evaluation. I don't claim that I have any experience on the matter, the only 10-man heroic boss I've tried since beta was Al'akir, which did feel easier there. Personally, I don't really want to take "bosses are impossible" for an argument because afterall, many bosses in our progress path did certainly feel more or less impossible to us (and I'm sure to many others) but someone always found a way.

As for if and when we are doing 10-man heroics, I'm not really in a position to answer. There isn't really anything for us in 10HC that would make many of us want to break our relaxing farm schedule.
