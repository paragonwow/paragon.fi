Hi,

This isn't the first time this idea has come up, and I'm afraid the answer to the question is still the same: we aren't interested in streaming our raids regularly. We save the streams to events like Dreamhack or Assembly, which also involve live raiding.

The reason why we don't want to sharte our raids with people is that we want our raids to be a fun, relaxed environment for our players. We like that we can do anything we want during the raid and not stress how it looks like to the outsiders (if we wipe for instance). There is also the aspect of not giving out that much about our players and tactics; we want to keep some of the details about stuff like group spirit and player performance to ourselves.
