This should show the essentials: http://massif.fi/~arx/ui.jpg

The main part of the UI comprises of:
- Grid
- GridIndicatorCornerText
- GridStatusHots
- GridStatusLifebloom

All should be available on http://www.wowace.com.

The point is to have everything I need visible on Grid. The plugins enable me to put my hots' timers to the corners of the unit boxes. I have Rejuvenation at upper right, Wild Growth at lower right, Regrowth at upper left, and Lifebloom in the second center text indicator.

Right next to Grid I have my important cooldowns with TellMeWhen, unit frames for my own HP with Shadowed Unit Frames, cast bar and gcd indicator with Quartz, warnings from BigWigs, and debuffs from ElkBuffBars. The entire point is that I only have to move my eyes minimal amounts to see all the important stuff.

In TBC I used to have Grid on the left side of the character, because human peripheral vision works better horizontally than vertically (citation needed, yeah yeah). It gives you slightly better vision of the playing field for free while tunneling Grid, but I felt it ultimately obstructed too much of my view and made overall UI design harder.

The specific addons aren't really that important except for Grid and plugins, which I find unparalleled. If you need some addon named, I can of course do that, but the functionality is available from many different sources.

(PS. Pay no attention to the SCT addon (Parrot) on the right, I haven't finished configuring it and I just sort of dumped it there for now.)
arx / xaar
