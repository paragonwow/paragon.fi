Hy, I don't really understand your problem. But if u use Grid, I think the best thing to do is tu use a Macro, so that you don't have to click anymore. This is the macro I'm talking about.

cast [modifier:alt,target=player] [target=mouseover,help] [help] [target=none] Croissance sauvage(Rang 4)

You change "Croissance sauvage" by any spell you want. For example:

cast [modifier:alt,target=player] [target=mouseover,help] [help] [target=none] Rejuvenation(Rang 4)

You also have to change the Rang.

This is my UI. But this is the one for my rogue. Just imagine Grid in the center of the screen.
