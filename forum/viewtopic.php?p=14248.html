His concern might actually be valid. From the very website you linked, I quote:

shadowpriest.com wrote:Just spent whole evening harassing Excel to shed some light on the Mind Flay vs Mind Sear @Alysrazor burn phase topic and finally belive I have some solid numbers to share. Basically the question i'm trying to answer is "how much haste does it take for MS to outperform MF pure single target DPS wise".

Instead taking average damage numbers from logs I decided to do the math with damage formulas from here and 10000 spellpower. +- 1k spellpower only changes the result by 0,4% so gear differences are quite irrelevant. I'm ignoring all the other modifiers(mastery, crit damage, extra damage taken buff on Aly, etc) as they affect both spell the same way. Only things that make a difference between 2 spells are coefficients, base damage, evangelism, MF glyph, cast time and number of ticks. So these numbers don't have to look realistic, they represent ratios of those modifiers @10k sp.

MF tick damage: 187,1+0,288*10000*1,1(evangelism)*1,1(glyph) = 3671,9
MS tick damage: 217,4+0,2622*10000 = 2839,4

Full MF = 3*3671,9 = 11015,7 (equal to DPS of MF since you can cast 1 every sec)
Full MS = 5*2839,4 = 14197

DPS from both spells is equal when it takes 14197/11015,7 = 1,289 sec to cast 1 full Mind Sear. In order to get your MS cast time below 1,289 you need more than 288% haste, which is achivable on Alysrazor fight. So, it can be better single target spell than MF with enough haste, with following assumptions:
1) Evangelism nor SWP falls off - need to cast at least 1 MF every 17 sec.
2) Shadowfiend uptime does not suffer. Fiend benefits greatly from the crit buff and is worth keeping up.
3) 3 orbs proc within 6,5 sec from SW:P+"occasional" MF alone. Even when MB cast time is significantly below 1 GCD it has higher damage per execute time than MS/MF with at least 1 orb and should be used every cooldown.
4) Dark Archangel isn't available. When it's up it would take 362% haste (1,08 sec MS cast time) for MS to outperform MF.

If I'm mistaken somewhere feel free to point it out.
