One thing that comes to my mind is that do you really need that big of a recount? It literally takes 1/5 of your screen. You could for instance put it down to the grey area under your unitframes. If you want it to be long you could move your omen and grid under your unitframes, and put recount to the left side of your screen (and make it a lot smaller at the same time of course).

By moving grid and recount to the grey area and squeezing stuff a bit there you gain more visibility to things around you. Having the space however is kind of a personal thing and lot a lot of people play with clamped up UI's and are happy with them.
