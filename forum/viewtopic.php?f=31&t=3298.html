Wortx wrote:And one more spec question. Now we're progressing spine. Demo or destro spec? Also,which trinkets? I can use next trinkets :HC Insignia of the Corrupted Mind,LFR Spine trinket,Moonwell Chalice and VP trinket. Which two?


Demo should be your best option. Try to make sure that you'll do your best to maximize metas for tendons by casting incinerates (or shadow bolts) as much as possible in the middle of them. Best trinket choices for Spine would be VP trinket and Moonwell Chalice, where you pop MWC around 25-30 seconds before tendon gets exposed, use meta just before MWC ends and use VP trinket at the start of the actual burn.

Edit: Advice I gave you is based on the fact that tendon damage is only thing that matters for you, if your raid struggles on something else, there are better trinket (and possibly even spec) options.
