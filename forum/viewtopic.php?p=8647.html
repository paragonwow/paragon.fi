I seriously doubt that we have to stay awake for inhumane amount of time, but I'll throw in my 50 cents.

My personal tip is not to eat too much sugar or junk food. Sugar gives a nice boost to your vigilance almost instantly, but your blood sugar will drop very quickly.
Ordering a kebab or pizza might sound like a good idea since you save time by not making food yourself, but junk isn't iron-rich and it doesn't have much carbs in it.
Carbs and iron can help you stay awake for longer periods.
Drink a lot of water or other fluids, you'll have to use bathroom more often but it'll definitely keep you awake and make you feel less tired. This is by far the easiest and most important thing to do if you need to stay awake.
Drinking coffee or energy drinks helps a lot, you can really drink either, they don't have much variation on their effects. However you should avoid caffeine overdose (~300 mg in a day), it can cause all kind of wonderful effects like psychosis and hallucinations or even kill you.

Now some practical tips:
Don't think you can overcompensate for sleep, your body is used to some certain amount of sleep. If you sleep more than what its used to, you'll end up tiring quicker and feel lethargic. However you should probably time your sleeping that you wake up ~2-3 hours before launch. This gives you time to sleep more if you STILL feel tired or you can do a physical exercise (lol wow players) to wake yourself up.

Don't go to take a nap. We had a guy in our previous guild, (who shall remain unnamed) He was in one of our wotlk dungeon leveling groups and he was really tired for being awake for too long, so he wanted to take just a little 30 min nap.
He came back 16 hours later.

I hope this helps!
