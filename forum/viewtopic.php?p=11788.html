I leveled my paladin to 70 with mostly dungeons. I tanked them, got 2-3 levels from the dungeons on my level range when I did all of them once, including quests. Then I quested 1 level and went to a new set of dungeons (higher level ones).

I really liked tanking and I absolutely love paladin healing so paladin isn't a bad choice. If you want to get the absolutely best class for raid healing... a priest might be a bit better at the moment after the mana cost nerfs to paladin. But paladin will be easier to get to dungeon groups (if you are willing to tank) and easier to gear up, since it doesn't share caster cloth loot.
