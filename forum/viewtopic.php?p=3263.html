P1 = Tank and Spank, basic single target skill priority.

Raging Spirit phases = keep dots rolling on all spirits you can, but make sure you don't reapply your shit if the targets are low on hp.

P2 = Stand at a ledge and dps the boss, use seed of corruption on valkyrs. Having some nice name plate addon is very nice for this as you don't want to seed on lk as it most likely has corr rolling on it. If you have targeting issues, just spam seed on the boss, It's not a massive THEORETICAL dps loss. (It could be a practical dps gain now that I think about it, dunno.)

(Raging spirit phase)

P3 = Don't life tap unless you move, cast as many dots as possible on remaining Raging spirits and LK himself. (if you use exploding spirit soaking tactic you can just keep rolling dots on the boss and nuke.)

You should also spam Seed in frostmourne room always you have a chance to do so. Shadow ward is a super cool ability, it makes your healers' job a little bit easier and it might save your ass some time.

You should also use death coils, health stones and shadow wards on infests if you don't have a priest shield to mitigate the damage. Dead dps is no dps. Play it safe.
Goddamn, this fissure skill is inaccurate.
