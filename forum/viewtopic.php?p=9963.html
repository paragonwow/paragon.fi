Macros aren't the problem. Looking at your armory:

- Wrong meta. Use Chaotic.
- Neck: reforge haste to hit
- Cloak: undo the reforging, you're overcapped on expertise
- Chest: reforge to hit, you're overcapped on expertise
- Gloves: reforge to hit, you're overcapped on expertise
- Legs: reforge to crit, you're overcapped on expertise
- Feet: undo reforging, what the hell are you thinking reforging hit to haste?
- Ring: never reforge hit, you're overcapped on expertise, if you end up under cap, reforge mastery or haste to exp, never hit.
- Trinket: your blue trinket is terrible, get something else, like a Right Eye of Rajh or the Tol Barad trinket (reforge the mastery on it).
- Weapons: Avalanche is not an appropriate enchant. Landslide is BiS, but for blue weapons go with dual Hurricane.
- Gemming - with the proper meta you should gem 4 Bold and 6 Piercing Dream Emerald, getting socket bonuses where you can.
- Spec - 7/31/3 is better until the patch.

You want to get a balance of hit and crit. Your crit is in a decent place, if you fix your gemming and bad reforging, you should see a significant DPS increase due to having more rage. You really don't need any macros apart from a /stopcasting Slam macro.
