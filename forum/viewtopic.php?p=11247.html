Anyway, I think haste vs mastery choice comes down to your guilds progress and healing setup. During normal modes and farm content haste will be better most of the times since people just wont get that low hp. But during progress or even heroic mode farming for some guilds, the healing isn't that simple and everyone is constantly going low. In that sort of environments mastery shines.

I myself have been trying between haste and mastery sets lately, and even if raw numbers show that mastery would be better, in practice haste just works better in farm content for me. Still I'm planning to swap back to mastery heavy gearing when next raid tier comes and also 4.1 buffs our mastery even more.

For me the choices on gearing comes down more on hps stats vs spirit. As there is few items that are better itemized overall but slack spirit, specially this is the case on trinkets (SHARD OF WOE LOL).
