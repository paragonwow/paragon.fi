Hey kold

As far as i Know MM is quite hard to keep an rotation. I would say SS - Chim Shot - Pop Rapid fire - Spam Steady shot but keep your Chim shot cause it's almost a free shot and it does quite alot of damage. Once your Rapid fire is over you pop - Readiness, you go the same way spam steady shot but keep your Chim Shot on cd. Keep in mind if Aimed shot Procs you just go for that then just spam Steady shot again. When your rapid fire is over it's time for a little change in your rotation. Keep Chim shot up + keep spamming steady shot till your like 100 focus.

My oppinion then when your 100 focus you should go for 2 or 3 arcane shots. So your rotation after your 2nd rapid fire is done, is Chim Shot - Aimed shot (till 100 energy) - 2 / 3 Arcane shots - (possible proc of aimed shot) - chim shot (always keep this on CD). Then repeat this or atleast try because MM is really hard to keep your rotation.

Don't forget MM is a spec you play on your own feeling. You acutally do alot of dps on Chimaeron for a hunter at least.
Remember Survivel is quite ok as well if you want to keep your rotation Survivel is the goal you want to go.

I hope your something with this since my hunter is my alt at the moment.

Many regards yanina (pheebe)

Hello Kold,

I did check out your armory and it was opposite as you said your statweights being?
You actually reforged more mastery and only having 700~ haste. If i were you id stick with this statweight:
Agi -> hit -> haste till 1350 -> crit -> mastery.

As far as the rotation goes i start with:
Pre pot -> Serpent String -> Chimera -> RF, CoW, Bloodfury -> Readiness -> 2x SS -> Chimera -> SS -> Aimed -> 2x SS -> Chimera -> 2x SS -> Aimed.
Mostly i dont like to use any specific rotation, its more like this: keep your sting up 100%, shoot Chimera every time it comes out of cd, When you are under haste effects (Bloodlust, RF, Prestor's talisman of machination) dump your focus with Aimed shots, you should be under haste effects whole first 20% so you get most out of your Careful Aim (ask your raid leader when hes about to call lust so you DONT stack your RF with Bloodlust). Under 80% dump your focus with Arcane shots. (if you are not under haste effects)

1) Simulation Craft
2) yes, you use aimed shots while you are under haste effects
3) its not so huge difference with 1 pt from bm -> sv anyways

Checked your Chimaeron logs, first thing popped to my eye was _LOW_ amount of aimed shots. Keep hardcasting aimeds with RF and BL
