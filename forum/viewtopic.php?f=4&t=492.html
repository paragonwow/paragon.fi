I currently use a G19 Keyboard & Roccat Kone Mouse

G19 is pretty much the same keyboard as the G15 keyboard, with a few tweaks and improvements, further light-modifying systems and cooler looks :)

Roccat Kone is extremely comfortable, it fits so well with your hand-shape, it's also very well designed, cool looking and the lights are awesome as with all equipment from Roccat.
I also like the fact that it has a sensitivity adjusting button (2 actually) ontop, right infront of the mouse wheel, that allows you to quickly change the sensitivity of your mouse with normal steps (10%) in either direction. It's also got the usual ''autowalk'' button on the left, as well as an additional button, which i have bound to ''Hunters Mark'' as i play a Hunter :)

So Kruf, go get yourself a Roccat Kone :D it's truely amazing ;)
