Energy you physically cannot use doesn't count as wasted energy, so if you cap during AR despite wasting no time on GCDs, you couldn't have avoided it anyway so you haven't lost any DPS. There's not really any way to avoid it, using BF to slow the regen is a bad idea, so you just have to live with it.

As a side note you could use an Evis only rotation whilst AR is up in order to use energy you would have otherwise lost.
