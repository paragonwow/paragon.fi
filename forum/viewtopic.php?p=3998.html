Hey Argiris..

A few pointers..

First of all.. You dont have a ''rotation'' as a healer.. being a healer is all about timing the right cast the right time, you have to learn about what the boss does and think ahead of it. If you want ''rotations'' i suggest you roll shadow spec instead and go dps.

As far as the addons go.. It wont make you a better player, but it might make some things easier..

Xpearl is complete and utter shit if you ask me! Get rid of Xpearl, get new player frames like..

Shadowed Unit Frames are neat and easy to setup:

http://www.wowinterface.com/downloads/i ... rames.html
