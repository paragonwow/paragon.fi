Right the rotation.
You have these abilities that you will use:
Hunters Mark
Explosive Shot (ES)
Black Arrow (BA)
Serpent Sting (SS)
Cobra Shot (CS)
Arcane Shot (AS)
Kill Shot (KS)

Allways start with Hunters Mark ofcourse since it will boost your Attack Power against that target.
After that you want to have your highest damage dealing abilities on the boss as soon as possible.

So the rotation should be in a perfect situation, without moving or using other hunter abilities such as Tranq-shot would be:
ES -> BA -> SS -> CS -> CS

Because of the Refreshing abilitie of CS for your SS you wont have to re-aplly SS so after the first set of attacks you will drop that one from your rotation. And because of the 30seconds cooldown on BA, you wont cast it that frequently.

Therefor after the first set it will look a bit like this
ES -> CS -> CS -> CS -> ES
And rince and repeat.

Now if you get the Lock and Load Proc
Best way to handle that is like this:
ES -> CS -> ES -> AS(CS) -> ES
Only use AS if you have enough focus left to cast the 3th ES and if needed the BA because its again a bit of dps lost if you have to cast a CS after the 3th ES to gain enough focus to cast BA.

And when the boss is below 20% you ofcourse want to use your KS as frequently as possible, so as soon as it is of cooldown you want to press that button.

hope it made a bit of sence... :)
