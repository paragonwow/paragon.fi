When expansion is as fresh as MoP is currently, secondary stats are still on quite low level and being haste capped won't really be a problem. Using all your cooldowns together with lust (DS included) makes you get most out of them. Of course it's better to use 2 DS without lust than it is to use one with it but I'm sure you were aware of that already.

If you have two builds that sim very close to each other, they are probably also similarly effective. Having a lot of haste makes the gameplay a bit more enjoyable (at least to me) because you will find yourself without soul shards less often and you are more likely to get the cast off before you need to move from the fire you are standing at (we all finish casts before we move, don't we?) but that's completely subjective opinion. I've personally aimed for extra corruption tick at 4717 but I won't claim that it's the best or the only way to be effective.
