You're playing wrong Ridance. The reason haste is valued lower is because of mechanics like borrowed time. For instance, technically the highest aoe HPS rotation should be something akin to pw:s -> poh -> pw:s -> poh. As for if you're primarily single target healing, you'd pick up Strength of Soul, increasing your frequency of pw:s and the uptime of borrowed time, again lowering the worth of haste.

In either case, you want to hit the holy 5tick renew cap, and beyond that go full mastery, behind int > spi prios.

Minuria wrote:
but whatever Haste I've forged in is there to (roughly) meet the five-tick threshold for Holy.



So with your Holy Gear you have 6,72% Haste, without any points in Darkness but with the 5% Raidbuff you come down to 11,72%. But the softcap for Renew is 12,5%, isn't it?

Haste is multiplicative, not additive.
The math for each point of Darkness plays out like this:

1.125 represents the 12.5% you need.
1.05 represents 5% you should get from being fully raid buffed.
1.03/1.02/1.01/1.00 represents how many points in the talent Darkness you have.
The last number is the number solved for to see how much Haste percentage you need on your gear and below I've put the Haste rating required to reach the stated percentage.
(1.0403 = 4.03%, 1.0504 = 5.04%, etc)

3/3 Darkness (3%) : 1.125 = 1.05 x 1.03 x 1.0403
...which would be 515 Haste rating on gear.

2/3 Darkness (2%) : 1.125 = 1.05 x 1.02 x 1.0504
…which would be 646 Haste rating on gear.

1/3 Darkness (1%) : 1.125 = 1.05 x 1.01 x 1.0608
…which would be 779 Haste rating on gear.

0/3 Darkness (0%) : 1.125 = 1.05 x 1.00 x 1.0714
…which would be 915 Haste rating on gear.
