I'm not in paragon, but I have been really studying WoL (especially DBS) lately for my own skill building so I took a look at your links.

A few things that are holding you back on dps.
1. Your fight time is 3:43. the longer the fight the less % of that fight is during Bloodlust. The longest ranked fight was 3:04 last I looked and most are in the 2:30 range. Along with that Blood Lust serves you better if it happens at the start not the end to buff your Gargoyle when all procs are up.

2. Your race is UD. If you were Orc you would get 5 free expertise from the axe, all your pet damage would be higher, and you would get Blood Fury.

3. Your gear is very good, but you have some room to improve. Dps will def go up when you replace a few pieces. Especially the trinket. The EJ UH thread has a ranking of trinkets that is awesome.

4. You use Blood Boil. On DBS any aoe on those adds is diminished by 70%. Pestulance is smart to spread EP for the ranged but Blood Boil is weaker than Blood Strike, and If you Blood Strike more you would have a greater uptime of Desolation


As for your questions,
ERW should be used either during a time when you aren't able to use a gcd for runes or DC (like right after the first garg), OR when you mess up your rotation
Same with Blood Tap unless you are using it to do an instant refresh on Bone Shield

There is no precise time to use army. The main priorities for army is don't pop it when they may taunt adds, pop it when it won't keep you from using other abilities (before pull or during a lul), lastly pop it during the fight.
Even if you use it instead of other abilities it's a dps upgrade, but its a bigger one if you can do it when you cant use other abilities

Death Coil hits harder than Blood Strike, but not harder than Scourge Strike. Death Coil is also not buffed by diseases so you can use it as filler when diseases fall off or instead of a Blood Strike if you are going to be RP capped before your normal DC time.

You're gonna want to pop ERW when you have the most procs up. Usually that's at the start of the fight when everything lines up and Bloodlust/Heroism is on.

Blood tap .. if you don't use it to refresh bone shield, just use it as an additional Blood Strike if you feel like it.

For AotD usage, there's really nothing fancy with it. Throw down a DnD ~12s before engaging and start summoning, then run in. There's very little to use it well on, unless you're struggling on Putricide's green slime (pets reduce the damage - you can basically have 2 DKs absorbing all of the explosions with army & pets). There's some very limited "good" use for it on LK too.

As for capping on RP, I'd fire away a Death Coil every single time as long as I'm not delaying my runes. Meaning, firing a DC during RGP is fine but not actually delaying any of the runes.

There's not really any sort of "tips & tricks" thread anywhere, as far as I know. I've usually just optimized every encounter week after week, cutting down on movement and the time away from a boss' melee range etc.. such as soaking the AoE blasts on Sindragosa and stuff instead of running away. If I wouldn't, I think that'd make the game really boring much much much faster.
