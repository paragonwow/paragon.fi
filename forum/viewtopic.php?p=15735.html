The problem with 10 vs 25 has always been that everyone thinks different about them. In our opinion, the brackets should be completely separated, and 25-mans have usually been harder than 10-mans.

In some people's eyes 10-man is just as important as the 25-man. Our opinion will of course depend on how hard Deathwing will be, and we can't say for sure before we've seen both 10- and 25-man.

Anyway, myself I see 25-man as the mode that offers more possibilities for making interesting tactics. It's also more demanding when one needs to work out with raid positioning. Just to name a few things.

The main problem I have with the 25-man raids I've been in is that I don't feel as if my role is important.
