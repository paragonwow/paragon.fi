Disc hasn't been bad at healing for a very long time. I assume the reason you have thought so is that your have used other tools to track healing than WoL (Recount etc) that hasn't shown how much absorbs we actually do with Shields and Divine Aegis.

The reason disc comes out ahead of other healers on many fights is due to us being able to absorb incoming damage before any other healer is able to heal the damage inflicted. However, this goes for a raidhealing disc (ie shieldspammer). If the discpriest is only on tankduty he/she will obviously get crushed on healingmeters by other aoe healers.
