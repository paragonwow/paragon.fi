To be honest with no intention to offend you, even with your current gear you should enchant it and reforge to the best possible. Try to get your dk and mage at a same gear level and have a look at what class you enjoy the most. Paragon is the best guild in the world true that but they are not a mind reader neither has awesome powers to know what will suite you the best especially when none of them know your play style. Most importantly though is that from your progression you were never near the end game content at any time of this expansion. Blood Legion are a high end raiding guild, making it in top 20 world and 3rd USA, no offence but I can't really see you fit in there. On the other hand although Death Jesters aren't as good as Blood Legion, they are still a good guild. If you want to go high end raiding, I suggest you to first start doing some pugs which will get you to maybe 2/7hc firelands, then find a guild which you can start having some experience in raiding environment and then if you really think that you have what it takes to join a high end raiding guild, make your move. Although if you find a guild which you really like and you're not a player who wants to sacrifice your time to raid then don't bother applying to a guild which fight for ranks. It's much better to stay in a guild which you love raiding with then join a guild which you hate being in because you have to sacrifice your time raiding with. But after all, nothing can be achieved without sacrifices. I don't sacrifice my time as these guys do. I raid in a high end guild but we don't fight for ranks. Paragon don't get me wrong, I have full respect for you and I love what you do, and yeah if I had the time for it I would be a player who wants to fight for world ranks but as I don't I am happy being a player who gets to end game content after some time but always before a big nerf :P

Hope this wall of text will help you out!
