C'mon it is only one second... and unless you are a robot you might even need the 0.5s longer anyway to press the button. But the why is pretty clearly explained. Explosive Shot is your highest DPS shot as survival, and in order to get the most out of it you want both of the ticks to take place before you shoot the next.

Since the ticks can crit quite high it would be a waste to put another minor shot in between which would essentially delay the next Explosive Shot even further, meaning you push back your number one shot.

Thats worth waiting 0.5 seconds. At least thats what I understood from the article. I am no Hunter.

This is the "old correct way", from Ulduar times. Delaying other shots is less dps lost than clipping the last tick of your Explosive Shot due to it being such a massive dps source compared to everything else.

However, even back then there were discussions whether it in fact is optimal and some theorycrafting has proved that the optimal way is slightly more complex, see https://sites.google.com/a/moonsaberrider.org/hunterdps/lock-and-load.
