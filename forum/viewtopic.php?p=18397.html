MyCelarAD
 
Posts: 57
So Chosen of Elune only provides its bonus while you are in any Eclipse state or when you have the ''Celestial Alignment'' buff active.

lappe wrote:just blow them all at once, all 3 of them have 3min cd so they align perfectly for both, single and aoe situations.


When you pop all 3 the same time, you will have some dead time with Chosen of Elune active (No Eclipse state), therfore a lot of damage will go to waste.

Would it not make more sence to make it look like this?

*Start of at 75 towards Lunar Eclipse
*Pre-pot!
*Cast Starfall on the pull
*Wrath x1
-(Enter Lunar Eclipse)
* Incarnation: Chosen of Elune + Nature's Vigil Macro
*Moonfire + Sunfire
*Starsurge
*Spam Starfire with Starsurge on proc
-(Losing Eclipse, you are at 0)
*Starfall (When other runs out)
*pop Celestrial Alignment (so the remaining ~15-ish seconds of Incarnation: Chosen of Elune will scale with CA, and the first ~15-ish will scale from Eclipse)
* Moonfire (Auto Sunfire)
* Spam Starfire with Starsurge off CD/proc
* Starfall (When other runs out)

So basically: Pop Incarnation: Chosen of Elune + Nature's Vigil Macro at Full Lunar/Solar during fight, and Celestial Alignment at 0 Power and re-dot with moonfire once you pop CA and just before CA runs out. Spam Starfire til CA runs out, and continue your normal Eclipse direction.

That way less of Incarnation: Chosen of Elune will go to waste.

Thoughts/ideas?
