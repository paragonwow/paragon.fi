I'm gonna throw in a similar opinion as well to make sure you stay comfortable with it.

First of all, drop the blood strike. During pre-cataclysm that might've been a spell you could use in either unholy or the frost specc. Now it's only beneficial for the blood tanks. However, a good rotation shoul start with OB (As this spell is instant and you don't waste any DK resources on it), then simply spam Obiliterate until CD and try to gather as much runic power as possible since you would want to use as many frost strikes as possible before having the opportunity to spam Obiliterate again. The main reason frost is such a good specc combined with unholy presence is the fact that you have two different "step-ins" during the fight. One is called "Rime" and the other is called "Killing machine". Both of which can occur several times within a certain amount of time (Let's say you get killing machine twice in 5 seconds or so). "Rime" is when howling blast and icy touch proccs (I asume you're familiar with the word "proc"). During "Rime" you would want to use howling blast instead of icy touch (OB can replace this free-disease pop on). The other happening is "Killing machine" (although you might not be too comfortable with this word, it have occured for you several times), where Obiliterate and Frost Strike proccs. You would always want to choose a Obi procc over a FS procc. Here's the explenation. Let's say a FS (usually that happens for several players) crits (You're promised a crit according to your talent points) for 29k while the obiliterate would procc for as high as 44k. Better gear compared with awesome trinkets/CD = much higher critts. As I mentioned earlier, the frost specc should be combined with the unholy presence (Fast and high critts = awesomeness)
