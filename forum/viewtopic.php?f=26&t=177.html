Looking at the spreadsheets both 15/51/5 and 18/51/2 give quite similar results. 15/51/2 seems to be around 10-20 dps ahead of the vile poison variant. I prefer 5 points in relentless strikes just because it makes a cycle so much easier to sustain thus making raiding lot more enjoyable.

At the moment I'm wearing the 258 ilvl helmet and 3x 245 ilvl t9 set pieces. You will have to check a sheet for your gear, but I gained about 150-200 dps from switching my t8 pieces to 4t9 according to the latest sheet.

Currently the best-in-slot gear for rogues uses 4xt9.258 and the gloves from 10 man heroic faction champions. However, the 258 set is near-impossible to attain so items like the hood you mentioned might be worth checking out.
