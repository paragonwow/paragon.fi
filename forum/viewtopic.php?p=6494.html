Let's try to stay civil here. Please remember that neither me nor Kripparrian is by any means some kind of un-erring robot from another universe, so whatever we do may not actually always be the best thing.

The choice between Potion of Speed and Wild Magic is complicated a lot by the rather weird haste scaling hunters have; if you have zero haste, 500 haste rating is probably better than 200 crit rating, but if you already have enough haste for your Steady Shots to hit GCD, 200 crit rating becomes clearly the better choice.

This is again, however, made even more complex by the fact that if you have very good gear you're very close to 100% crit chance on some abilities, especially when you have any agi or crit proc up, so some of the crit from Wild Magic might be wasted.

What this comes down to is that if you do stack, it would seem prudent to stack Wild Magic, as you're likely to gain more from it. As to which is better, stacking or not, I still haven't seen any real math done on it so I don't know for sure.
