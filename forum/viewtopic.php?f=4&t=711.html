I believe the feelings are generally mixed but on the positive side.

I think most people appreciate that raiding will become less grindy but are a bit uneasy about how this will affect 25-man raiding.

Personally I'm afraid that this change might split raiding population in two, both attempting to compete that their format receive most developer attention. Let's face it: each and every encounter is always "better" (more enjoyable) in either 10-man or 25-man. The problem will be further compounded by the fact that the difficulty levels will inevitably vary.

I think the main problem with 10-man format is counting healers. There's a huge difference between 2 healers and 3 healers just as there's a very significant difference between 5 DPS and 6 DPS (this is all assuming 2 tanks in all encounters). In 25-man the transition between (4)-5-6-7-8-(9) healers is a lot smoother.

In short: I'm personally not overly positive on how this will affect the quality of 25-man content. On the other hand, if it means more people raiding it would also have to mean more resources to develop raiding.
