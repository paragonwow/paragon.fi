With the new buffs etc by far the easiest encounters are: (from easiest to hardest)

Gunship
Rotface
Marrowgar (with decent healing)
Blood Princes (more about staying alive than anything)
Blood Queen (bite equal numbers of ranged and melee)
Festergut (split ranged into two spread out camps on opposite sides)
Valithria (target blazing skeletons asap and this is laughable now)

---

Harder encounters are:
Deathbringer Saurfang (Still needs decent healers, 2 paladins should make it easy)
Lady Deathwhisper (Extremely bursty damage + retard check with ghosts)
Professor Putricide
Sindragosa
Lich King

If you need specific help with a particular encounter let me know.
