There's no splash element of any kind to Shadowflame Barrage. The most you can do for your healers beyond keeping the Prototypes shut down is ensuring any pillar with a healer priest on it has its damage dealers consolidated into a full group of five for Prayer of Healing.

Shadow of Cowardice is kill-spell repeatedly cast by Nefarian whenever he's on the chamber floor at any player not on it, specifically those still atop the edge or the three pillars.
