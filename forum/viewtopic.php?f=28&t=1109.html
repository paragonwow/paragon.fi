This macro seems to be the one most people use for their SS, AS and CS:
#showtooltip *ability name*
/cast !auto shot
/cast Silencing Shot
/cast [target=pettarget,exists] Kill Command
/cast *ability name*

/#showtooltip
/cast [@focus,help] Misdirection; [help] Misdirection; [@pet,exists] Misdirection

Misdirects to your focus target if you have one, if not it misdirects to your pet. hope this helps :)

another useful macro for pet care (saves button space)

#show [modifier:alt] Mutton Chop
/cast [nopet] Call Pet
/cast [@pet, dead] Revive Pet
/cast [pet, nomodifier, button:1] Mend Pet
/cast [pet, nomodifier, button:2] Dismiss Pet
/cast [pet, modifier:alt] Feed Pet
/use [pet, modifier:alt] (Food name here)

revives pet if its dead, casts call pet if pet isnt summoned, mend pet if it is with you, hold alt + click to feed pet with selected food, right click to dismiss.
