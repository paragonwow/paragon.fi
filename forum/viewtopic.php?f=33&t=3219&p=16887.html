Kuja, your damage isnt really all that bad. You have pretty solid reforging, spec, and glyphs, and i don't see any terribly large mistakes in your logs. The main problem i noticed is you're getting fairly weak combustions. One thing to remember with combustion is your proc CD's. Keep in mind that your tailoring and power torrent proc will occur pretty quickly at the start of a fight. Aim to get combustions off when both those procs are active, even if it means taking a fireball crit (with those buffs) instead of a pyroblast crit (with out them). It might be useful to pick up an add on to track the internal cooldown of those procs. Sometimes its worth postponing a combustion for a few seconds to wait for them to pop up.

Guromin made an excellent thread about stacking ignites. I'd suggest reading it, has some pretty interesting stuff. .

It also looks like your clipping your living bomb sometimes, which is a small DPS loss since roughly a third of the living bomb overall damage comes from the explosion at the end. Never ever ever clip a living bomb.

Additionally it doesnt look like you're pre-potting. If you drink a potion just before an encounter begins, the effect will remain when the tank pulls and you'll be able to use another potion later in the encounter. Try to use your second potion during Bloodlust/Heroism/Time Warp, In Moleton Fury Range, or during a burn/AoE phase to get the biggest bang for your buck.

The final thing i noticed is you're not taking advantage of potential AoE situations. Impact spreading a wing tenticle/elementium bolt, Ice tombs on Hagara, forgotten ones on Yorsahj, and the melee adds on Blackhorn. Those are fights where fire shines through really well. And it's because of multi-dotting that they do so well, so you should attempt to make use of impact during those encounters are much as possible if there are multiple targets.




As for your weapon choice go with with hagara staff.
