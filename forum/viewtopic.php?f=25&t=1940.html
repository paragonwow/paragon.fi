I have a frost death knight, and I can tell you that to get the best damage out of frost, your procs are the key here.
With my frost death knight, I've noticed that if I just use Outbreak to drop both diseases down, spam Obliterate until my Howling Blast ability is proc'd, use frost strike (slowly) to dump RP, be careful if frost strike procs as it does not do alot of damage, whereas Obliterate does. Obliterate proc occurs on 5 melee attack hits. Festering Strike is a melee strike, deals lots of damage and keeps diseases up on bosses. This counts towards the Obliterate Proc.

I'm in full purps (359+ gears), maximised damage at 17k - 22k single target. But I am no great player. Will never beat others though :(

Hope was helpful to you.
