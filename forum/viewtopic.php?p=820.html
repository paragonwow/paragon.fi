Hey there!
According to EJ that 20/51/0 with 4/5 Reverberation is the current max dps spec, if you have 4 pieces of t9. Tried it when I got my 4th t9 piece and yeah it was a nice dps increase. Dpswise its great but you must be more careful with your mana (no elemental focus or imp stormstrike), so you must get full mana every time you use shamanistic rage or you will most likely go oom.
So I would say this is the best raiding spec (that's the only thing that matters right?), with 4piece t9. In 5man dungeons it can be a little crappy 8-)
