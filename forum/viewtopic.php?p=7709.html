Hello rusel83.

The best "rotation" if you want to call it that at the moment is still kinda similiar to our old priority system. You are still using specific priority for your fillers but on top of that you are pressing Crusader Strike every time its off cooldown to generate holy power and when you have 3 holy power you press Templar's Verdict. So the "rotation" you are probably looking for here is pressing buttons in this priority:

3 holy power TV > CS > HoW > Exo > Judgement > Holy Wrath > Consecration

So use TV whenever you have 3 hp up, cs every other global and fillers in between the cs in correct priority.

fookielol wrote:hola Iiris ! hows ret pallys looking @ beta ?

They are fine.. atleast for now. Ret was one of the best damage dealers before the recent changes pretty much topping damage meters with blue pvp/heroic gear or doing same damage with couple other classes with better gear. Now that they did their little nerf on ret damage it seems like we would be more on par with others in pure damage.

Ret also has a lot of utility: interrupt without any downsides(other than you cant macro it for seal procs xD), low ranged high duration stun (HoJ), pretty versatile long range CC (Repentance), ability to heal even tank IF NEEDED in some gimmick fights that needs hardcore minmaxing (Selfless Healer), Hand of Freedom, Hand of Protection... and so on. So I think retribution will be really wanted and useful class in Cataclysm raids. Lets just hope they dont nerf the damage too much ;).
