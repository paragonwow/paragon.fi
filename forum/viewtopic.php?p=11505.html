Its really simple actually:

Arcane = Low movement, Single target, Little to no adds
Fire = High movement, AoE heavy fights

Now on to the stuff you probably wont like hearing. Taking a look at your armory page I would recommend you change some stuff.

Head - Replace 54 crit 3% crit damage WITH - 54 int 3% crit damage meta
Legs - Replace 40 crit WITH - 20 int 20 crit
Belt - Replace 40 hit WITH - 40 int
Chest - Replace 40 int WITH - 20 int 20 hit (This makes up for half the hit from the belt and you still get 40 int total because of the socket bonus which I have no idea why you decided to not get in the first place)
Gloves - Replace 20 int 20 crit WITH - 40 int


I believe that is all the gemming in your gear as for the other stuff ill give you a bit of advice. Stop reforging your crit and haste into mastery if your playing as fire. Also stop trying to get 10 stat bonus sockets in most cases. You should never choose 20 int 20 crit just to get a 10 haste socket bonus. Maybe I missed some stuff that others can help you on but hopefully this helps out a little bit.
