I've been having a very strange week in ICC. I'm currently using Unholy and Blood, but Unholy is my main. As such, I gem for it.
This week, I was curious: How would Blood fare on single target fights (since Unholy is just a given for ones like Lady Deathwhisper)?
So I tried it out on Deathbringer, Rotface, Festergut and Marrowgar. The results left me quite baffled- from being number 3-5 in the DPS charts, I skyrocketed to number 1.

And I don't just mean the incredible initial burst where I popped Hysteria and DRW, I mean all throughout.
Don't get me wrong- I play Unholy just fine. It's been an awesome spec, and it's only 3-4 weeks ago, as we go further into ICC, that it's begun to lag behind Fury Warriors, etc. I can understand that of course, since warriors have always scaled way too well with endgame gear.

So the question is: Why am I doing more and more damage with Blood?

Yes, ICC gear has a huge amount of ArP, but that shouldn't account for higher disease damage, ghoul, etc. And lets not forget that the ICC buff is now affecting pets, etc.
There's no doubt that Blood does better burst DPS, but I thought it had been firmly established that Unholy was excellent at sustained DPS, provided you monitored procs and timed attacks well.

I feel a little silly at the moment, going Blood while in Unholy gemming. Unfortunately, I may end up being forced to go Blood soon; the DPS difference is just becoming larger and larger as I get into more ICC.
