@wjozw

If you check my armory here for talents etc.
http://eu.wowarmory.com/character-talents.xml?r=Lightning%27s+Blade&cn=Xodan&gn=Paragon
For the first kill I changed 3/3 Feral Instinct to 3/3 Thick Hide and
4/5 Feral Aggression to 3/3 Protector of the Pack and 1/2 Nurturing Instinct in order to get maximum mitigation.

I also changed glyphs of Shred and Rip to Survival Instinct and Growling. Gearwise I replaced 258 DC with 258 Juggernaut's Vitality and changed to a tanking weapon during second transition.

Now with 10% buff you can probably make due with only talents changes and Glyph of Survival Instinct. Just pop Barkskin and Survival Instinct when the boss comes to melee range and when Barkskin drops pop Enrage if you got 4xT10. Survival Instinct lasts for the entire duration you'll have to tank it if you take 2 debuffs.
