I'm pretty much never getting ToTT right away in the start of the fight. It wouldn't make much sense anyway holding it back.

Usually, I open with PS+IT (+grenade), 2x BS, SS and from there on out it depends on the fight completely.

If there's going to be Bloodlust right at the start, I quite often use hyperspeed accelerators and blood fury with my trinket procs. This is also the time when I'm going to pop my gargoyle. Next part depends on if I'm getting the #2 ToTT from rogues or not. If I am, I wait with re-applying diseases until I get ToTT. Depending on the fight, ie if there's no burst needed or no significant +%dmg gained, I also often use ERW if I have good enough procs/ToTT up.

If I'm not getting ToTT at all, I try and re-apply the buffed up (procs, advantage+desolate) diseases as quickly as possible.


Probably missed something, but can't right now remember what exactly.
