Spec is gear based 90% of the time. What can you play better and which are you geared for.

The only clear-cut winner for combat / assassin is Major Domo, where the cleave reins supreme. It is possible to use combat for every other fight, and is possible to use Assassin for Domo.

The only other fight where there is a minor advantage Combat vs Assassin is Alystrazor when flying, where assassin proves to be very good due to the nature of haste with each spec. As I previously said, it is possible to play either spec on any fight, some will have their preferences depending on the nature.

However if you are new to shadowcraft (and more so confused as to why it's telling you to reforge your gear - Most rogues these days use shadowcraft but understand enough of the stat weights and caps that it makes sense to them) I would hazard a guess that it would make minimal difference at your current playing standards.

Tl:dr - Play what you are geared towards / can play the best. - I'm not going to bother mentioning Subt.
