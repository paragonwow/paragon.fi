Hello Missakram

your enchants seems to be just fine expect for boots, you dont need the hit from icewalker so just swap it to tuskarr's vitality
gems on the other hand arent as they're supposed to be. always prefer sp over crit this means that you should gem sp for every socket that you dont benefit otherwise (socket bonus, meta requirement etc)
so go get Wrathcalcs check your stat values and then calculate if its better to gem 2x 23sp instead of 2x 12sp/10crit +7sp (socket bonus) etc for every item you have
