Another thing, mastery does not have diminishing return and it's the mainly mitigation status for feral tanker.

If you want some numbers I think:
Over 20 mastery
Over 21% Dodge(humanoid form stamina stacked) | Over 25% dodge(humanoid form agi stacked, if I'm not wrong if you're agi based tanker you should get over 38% dodge on fight)
Near 200k HP on bear form fully buffed

Hit and Expertise depends on how much damage threat is going around your raid, if you have problems holding aggro you should reforge some expertise and it should be enough.

I think with these stats and using cooldowns wisely you should tank all FL raid easily.
