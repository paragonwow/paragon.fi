The big ones:

Grid, GridStatusHots, GridIndicatorCornerIcons, GridIndicatorBorderTexts: raid frames

Bartender: ability bars (hidden)

PowerAuras (custom textures): spell cooldowns, RD overlay, mana

WeakAuras (custom textures): proc indicators

Gnosis (custom texture): cast bar

Elkano's Buff Bars: buffs/debuffs

Mik's Scrolling Battle Text: combat text
