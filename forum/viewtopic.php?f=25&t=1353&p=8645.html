Hi guys, I've got a Death Knight alt whom I'd like to play to her full capacity, but I have two nagging issues that I'm not quite able to overcome and was hoping someone here might have some answers.

1. According to Synti's Armory page (http://eu.wowarmory.com/character-sheet.xml?r=Lightning%27s+Blade&n=Synti), he's able to equip AND activate a Chaotic Skyflare Diamond as his meta gem. The requirements, however, are that you have more blue than red gems. Looking at the profile, that just isn't the case, nor should it be. Any reasons why this would be possible? I know that ultimately, the Cataclysm version, the Chaotic Shadowspirit Diamond, will be reverted to requiring only two blue gems, but for now, I'm still curious how he has an active ideal meta presently.

2. I'm looking to go 2H UH DPS, however, every single recommended rotation that I've found for the current build advises that Dark Transformation be activated immediately after diseases are applied. The ability is never available at that juncture. What am I doing wrong?

That meta gem shouldn't be active, probably just an armory bug. As for UH rotation, Dark Transformation isn't meant to be used immediately after you apply diseases.

"Diseases > Dark Transformation > DnD*/SS > FeS > DC..."
This is not a rotation, it is a priority list.
