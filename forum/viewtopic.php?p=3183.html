You can get extra mana from seal of wisdom. Judge when you can, it can proc from that and grant you way over 1k mana per proc.

It's ok to have higher haste. I just went for the highest ilvl items.

After a tank dies we check recount and check what happened. 90% of the time the damage was unhealable. Sometimes there are heals missing from one or both paladins, but even then it's usually bad luck. That leaves us with seeing if the paladins stand in the fire, which doesn't happen very often. You can also check if they used they cooldowns to avoid death.
