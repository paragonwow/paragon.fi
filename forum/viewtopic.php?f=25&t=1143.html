Stark wrote:In your particular playstyle what do have reforged? Crit for haste?

there are some simple rules:
if you are not hitcapped: reforge into hit (but notice: you can easily go for every socket bonus, also with blue sockets, using str / hit gems, nightmare tear is now kind of useless). Of course you want to reforge into hit beginning with expertise and ending with haste.

If you are capped reforge your hit, expertise, crit into haste as possible. If haste is already present, reforge into crit.

Items which already have crit and haste (or hit necessary to reach the cap) don't have to be reforged.

expertise can always be reforged.

Trinkets can also be reforged btw (dbw and wfs for example).

And hit rating conversion has changed as far as I know. You will need something around 246-250 hit to be capped as a non-draenei class.
