Change in the fireball glyph is actually a very minor buff.

Even though crit rating will have more value than haste in 3.3.3, it's not the same case with this glyph. Normally crit rating has more value cause it affects all your spells, haste in the other hand will be most likely capped for LB and Pyro and thus will only affect your fireball.

Since the glyph only affects fireball, normal stat values are bshit. In this case 5% more casts in X amount of time comes on top of 5% crit on a spell that allready has a very high crit percentage.

On top of this, the haste buff you get might result in getting extra fireball cast with 'pushing the limit' buff...

And to your question :>...With FFB being behind ToTW fire atm and ToTW buff only affecting fireball-spec (+ the very minor glyph buff!). It's kinda obvius that ToTW fire comes quite a bit on top of FFB in 3.3.3.
