Hit > Crit > Haste > Mastery. Now, there's controversy over 12.93% haste + some to account for latency or loads of haste to mimic T11 4-set but from what I've seen 1850 is not enough to fit CS > ASx2 > SSx5 into a tight CS cycle with the CS glyph and it's way too much Haste without the CS glyph. Since you're not using the CS glyph you should reforge all the Haste you can to Mastery down to 12.93% + some to account for your latency(13-14%) because that equates to a tight 10s CS cycle.

Before I list the said 'rotation' remember this is with my knowledge on 12.93% Haste level and assuming you will follow the 12.93$ Haste level because I am not geared enough to get to 1850(without massively reforging from Crit to Haste) and see for myself if it's a viable amount of Haste with the CS glyph to fit in a tight rotation without a lot of deadtime/delays.

To start in CA Phase: Some people find it better to HM > SrS > RF/CotW/Racials > Aimed > SSx2 > Aimedx2 > Readiness > Repeat with Aimed > SS maintaing ISS and managing focus. What I personally find that works wonders is just to HM > RF/CotW/Racials > Aimed > SSx2 > Readiness > Repeat with Aimed > SSx2 maintaing ISS and managing focus > SrS when you feel it's the right time and you can start the Standard Phase in time to refresh SrS(Make sure you get yourself to full focus before you start the Standard Phase so there isn't an issue with focus). Both work giving reliable results but you chose what works out for you.

Standard Phase: CS > ASx2 > SSx5 with MMM AI procs(on average being every other cycle) replacing a SS 3 of 4 cycles. So about 5 of 8 cycles you will have the 5th SS implemented into your cycle. The MMM AI proc can vary on where it procs into your cycle so you need to be smart and make sure you substitute it with the right shot to execute a perfect CS cycle. Use your brain and let your focus bar guide you to let you know when to do what for sometimes in the midst of a boss things happen and you need to accommodate for that quickly or it will result in a bigger dps loss.
