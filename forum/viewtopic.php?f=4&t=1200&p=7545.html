Paragon was formed by the core members of Knockout (Twisting Nether) and Sauna (Stormreaver) for Wrath of the Lich King in August 2008


I have no intention to put down Paragon in any way but just over two years is not really a long time. I know that members partly knew each others from previous guilds, but the guild known as Paragon is quite young.

My guild has been around for 12+ years throughout various MMOs and other games. Some left. Some joined. Some went inactive. But there is a core of people that was there in the beginning and that still goes strong. If the chemistry is right and you can deal with obstacles in a mature way through decisions done by leaders with a good feeling for what the guilds needs / wants, it can work.
