Shruiakand,

The best way to know what gear to use is to download the program Rawr and load your charecter into it. Save the file as anything and import it into SimulationCraft. Look at the amount of damage it does and then open your charecter again with the second set of gear. Load it into simulationcraft and see how much damage it does. Whichever does more damage is the best set. I use the combination of the two programs as a means of checking each other to make sure that I get the most benefit out of it.

I think it's really awesome that you read what I already posted before about mini burn phases and Flame Orb. Saves me some time typing :)

So... ....I just looked over your Shannox logs and I think it's incredibly halarious that for an entire minute you didn't make a single attack, which leads me to think you got hit with a crystal prison trap. I will tell you right now that no matter how much you know about your class, and no matter how well you can min/max you will still be the bottom dps if you can't dodge traps, tornadoes, fire, broodlings, brushfire, lava waves, ect.

I really have no clue what went on in that shannox kill, but something is clearly off. 97% of your damage was from Arcane Blast. I have Shard of Woe and I don't do that much % with Arcane Blast. I think you might want to look at your rotation. Read over this thread and comments to get some really good tips on stuff. http://elitistjerks.com/f75/t118084-cat ... ompendium/

If you have any other questions feel free to ask,
