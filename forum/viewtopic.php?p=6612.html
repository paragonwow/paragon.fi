Titan Panel - Replace with FuBar. Why? Because it's so much better.
SexyMap: Fine, but do you really need it? If you do not, turn it off, avoid giving it a dynamic look, a simple square'd be fine.
Carbonite: Extremely heavy, and not so useful for when you are level 80.
Atlas: Avoid the map viewer Atlas, get AtlasLoot Enchanced.
ItemPriceTooltip: Useless, I can figure that such an addon eats your RAM.
OmniCC: Good, a must-have, actually, make sure you set it to tenths of seconds.
Omen: A must-have, again, configure it properly!
Deadly Boss Mods: Good, make sure to put the bars properly!
Faceroller: Not too useful, if you got brain enough.
Gatherer: Eats your RAM as well, only turn it on when you are, well, gathering!
X-Perl: Very simple, get Shadowed Unit Frames or Pitbull Unit Frames instead, much better, and lighter.

Get Recount or Skada Damage Meter, get Grid (I'd say Grid2, but you choose!)

As for general UI improvement, check out Melee DPS UI Discussion.

I agree with most of what Nikorash said, but you should never ever use Pitbull if framerates are a concern. It's a crap ton heavier than Shadowed Unitframes for example.

Personally, I use STUF. Incredibly lightweight and extremely configurable.


You should just disable whatever you don't use while raiding. It takes only a few seconds to enable them again when you're not raiding. It's not much effort to do so, but will definitely improve your FPS while raiding when you're using "useless" stuff like SexyMap.
