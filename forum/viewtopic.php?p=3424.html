Jokke wrote:to ret palas. what is the optimal number of crit? atm i have 52% crit but only 5,1k ap. should i raise my ap and lower my crit?

There is no optimal number of crit as long as you are under the critcap(71.2%). But since str has higher value than crit you should never go for stacking crit to get it high, simply just see what items are good for your dps and what are not, if they have crit thats nice but you shouldnt intentionally aim for getting high or low crit. You can compare different items and gear combos with spreadsheets(found on EJ for example) or programs like Rawr.
