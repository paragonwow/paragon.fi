Well thank you kind sir.

It is optimal to use it with lunar eclipse, but you shouldnt really wait for that to cast your starfall. For example, your eclipse bar heading for solar in the start of a fight, just cast it without waiting for lunar. If you do however get it synced with lunar eclipse in the start with the starfall glyph it will mostly come up the next time you hit another lunar eclipse in the next cycle. We mostly use glyph of starsurge also, so depending on your luck you will get it off sync with your lunar eclipse. You shouldnt worry about it too much though, just use it when its ready if youre far away from a lunar eclipse.

Yes, one spirit is one hit rating, and they are equal in value. But technically hit is better because your treants only gain from melee hit, not spell hit which Balance of power gives. If you play resto/arena with same gear you're probably better off with the spirit though. I dont do either. But really, not a huge diffrence.
