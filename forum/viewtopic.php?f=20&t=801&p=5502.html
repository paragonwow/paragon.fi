Hello Fellow Bronzbeardien,

I had to post since i noticed you were on the same server.

I think your UI is a touch cluttered, there seems to be lots going on.
If i were you i would try to make a few tweaks to tidy it up. You dont really need to see the bag bar/character bar. These can both be hidden using bartender's options.
I would look at adjusting the Omen/Recount. I prefer to have the two on top of each other and then use the hide in combat/show in combat options so that they swap. (Thats more because i am not interested in my dps normally though)
