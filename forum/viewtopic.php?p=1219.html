Im taking most of my information from Elitist Jerks Retribution threads and I think the latest consensus in correct ret priority with 4pt10 is Judge>ds>cs>how>conse>exo>holy wrath. Yes I use haste potions, use them in sync with blood lust and trinket procs+wings. You can also pop double potions if you know exactly when the combat starts and if you are not gonna use blood lust right in the start, one right before combat and then one in combat with blood lust =).

The thing about agi gear is the agi gives you crit, but it also gives you attack power with the gear, and some times even more crit rating or haste ontop of that. The strength gear gives you strength(atk pwr) and sometimes crit and haste. On certain items the combination of stats on leather outweigh the combination of stats on plate. If leather didnt have attack power then leather wouldnt be an option, which is the direction blizzard is going in cataclysm.
