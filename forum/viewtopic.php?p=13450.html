Hit/Expertise do little for threat, that's why raid tanks (like Riggnaros with his gear) reforge away expertise and hit for Mastery and avoidance stats.

If you are just starting to tank now then you are behind the power curve of threat and it's going to take you a while to catch up. Other people are going to be in raid gear and pulling a lot of threat for your gear level.

As far as your build, you can ditch the Lichborne build and pick up something with a bit more threat (Virulence or Abomination's Might). You shouldn't need 1 point in Mind Freeze either.

jokerfmj

I have 2.53% hit and 21 expertise (which is as low as I can reforge at the moment, it'll drop once I get a new weapon), and I hold threat just fine against 26K+ DPS without tricks or MD. If you're having issues on boss fights, just keep your Dark Command and Death Grip ready so you can leap frog DPS at the start of the fight.

The only time you should have aggro issues is at the very start if someone happens to burst a few crits before you get a chance to keep ahead. Misses are going to happen but, expertise/hit don't generate enough threat for us to aim for the caps. If you're worried about missing a lot get your 8% hit but, it's really not necessary.
