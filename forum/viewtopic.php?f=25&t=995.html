There's some additional uptime on FC buff if you have it in off-hand. It's incredibly miniscule; originally I just had Razorice on my MH because I wasn't sure whether or not the off-hand hits for Razorice would do MH or OH damage.

The reason people most likely gem for haste is that it simply does more DPS overall once you're past a certain gear level.

Yes, your spec is viable. You _definitely_ don't want to end up in a situation where you don't have a DPS ability to hit on every single GCD. I'm pretty damn sure that would happen if you specced out of CotG. Frost Strike, surprisingly enough, does a lot more DPS than Horn of Winter.
