Jhazrun salutes Jiune with respect.

Unless you see yourself branching out into Holy or gaining steady access to iLevel 277 equipment, don't concern yourself with the set leggings if you already have Woven Death crafted.

The running principles behind the standard of opting for the leggings as one of the four set pieces are the absence of a non-caster, non-set option at the 277 level (rendering them a mandatory gateway item to the best-in-slot piece in established end-game raiding), and the relative ease of acquiring a 264-to-277 robe (saving a precious set token in the gearing-up phase).

For the moment being you're sitting in the middle of all of it, and feel little to no pressure for either choice.

Prayer of Mending operates by health deficits. Upon triggering it goes through every friendly target within 20 yds, pets included, for the greatest absolute number of missing hit points.
In scenarios of equal deficits (those being 0 when in full health) the spell simply picks a random target in range to leap to.

As long as both you and your healee are in combat with a hostile NPC, any true healing (not overhealing) generates an amount of threat equal to 50% of the healing done. This includes Renew and other delayed heals if, and only if, you've both been drawn into combat by the time the ticks occur.
