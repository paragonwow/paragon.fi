For the best professions for a dps warrior, your options are:

Jewelcrating/Enchanting - Pretty good choice overall

Jewelcrafting/Blacksmithing - Great choice since blacksmithing is highly flexible with the two extra sockets, could be used for things like getting expertise capped etc.

Jewelcrafting/Engineering - The best choice for max dps, It doesnt give the same benefits like two extra sockets from BS but the perks from this profession make it really great. Obviously hyperspeed accelerators for cooldown usage, but speed enchant on boots are just so nice to have for positioning etc, will most likely be able to save you from time to time from dying. In the end its also the most ''fun'' profession with various pets, mounts, and other fun gadgets available.

You can mix these professions as you please, but these are your best choice for doing dps.
