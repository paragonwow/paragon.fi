1. Your gemming and enchanting seem to be alright
2. 0/17/54 is the superior one
3. Best rotation is honestly no rotation, imo atleast. What I mean by that is that its better to have a priority system to follow instead of a set rotation. In most cases what you will end up doing is the same, using a rotation compared to a priority system, but being able to use your runes best according to each situations is far better. To give a example. Lets say you want to disease the green ooze on putricide fast, in that case a rotation will not tell you to do a pestilence, but if your just following a priority, you will be able to make that snap decision on your own. Hope it all make sense.
