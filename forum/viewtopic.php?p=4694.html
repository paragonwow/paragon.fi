Again, as I've probably said countless times in this thread (didn't check, I'm counting on it!), there are no magical numbers in stats you *should* have. However, it's *good* to have certain amounts of hit for example. Haste has no numbers at all really, it's always good to have more (and not a huge loss having less) as any haste cap isn't reachable at the current gear.

Spreadsheets calculate stuff differently, but if you have similar settings on both your friends and your own idps they should be giving similar results. I'd go with whatever Aldriana's sheet shows as better, but in the end the difference gemming a few 20ap/10haste instead of 20 haste is negligible. And as for the mongoose recommendation, I believe that Aldriana's sheet doesn't take into account the recent "stealth-nerf" to mongoose and still thinks it provides a static 2% attack speed increase. Berserking is better for anything and everything.
