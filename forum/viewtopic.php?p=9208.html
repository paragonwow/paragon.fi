Of course we'd rather have encounters that didn't favor certain classes too much.

As a one-time, easy to accomplish thing, extreme class stacking was quite funny. Raid stacking like this won't become the norm anyway, unless encounters are really flawed in the future. We're still at a point in the expansion where optimizing the raid is rather easy, because gearing only takes a short while. It wouldn't have made much sense to put in the effort to gear 11 warlocks at LK for example, and I certainly would have been extremely disappointed had that been required.

With consolidated buffs making raid composition requirements looser, we'll have to get used to the standard "normal" stacking extending to 4-5 guys though. Frankly I personally did prefer the old buff system which forced at least somewhat balanced raid setups, even though the new one opens new strategic options.
