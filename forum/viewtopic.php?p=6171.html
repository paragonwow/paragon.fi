Hello Grasshopper.

Looking at your profile, I don't really know where to begin. It would have been helpful to not equip that fishing pole. I assume you had it from a melee DPS player that you need to socket a Nightmare Tear to activate your meta. Don't. Just socket Purified Dreadstone (12sp/10spi) to where you get the best socket bonuses from to keep it active. And don't socket Spell Penetration either.

Your have almost twice as much hit as you need, assuming you raid with a Moonkin or a Shadow Priest, and even without either of those you're still well beyond the cap. I'd also suggest you to get rid of the mp5 gear. What strikes to me as the weirdest thing about your gear is that you only have 1 piece of t10 instead of 4 and you seem to have spent emblems on those incredibly bad emblem trinkets instead. Please tell me this has nothing to do with Gearscore.

Then let's switch to the subject everyone is concerned about nowadays. First of all, there are no set-in-stone boundaries for haste. Secondly, I'd guess around 95% of the time SP is more valuable than haste and you should still consider it as your primary stat. However, as you're mostly decked in 264 spirit-heavy gear or higher and your haste still lags a raid tier or two behind, you've probably fallen into the 5% category where haste potentially is a bit more valuable than SP. If you're up to it, you could stack your yellow and red sockets with Reckless Ametrines and switch back to usual socketing when you obtain more haste gear, but don't expect a drastic improvement. I can't really say I'd even recommend that though.

Here's a quick to-do list to fix some of your gear issues:

-get t10 head, chest and shoulders.
-Get Nevermelting Ice Crystal over your Maghia's (if you don't know why it's good for you, check the other recent threads)
-Get Band of the Invoker from triumph vendor until you find something better as your second ring.
-Get rid of excess hit in your gear. Remember, the DPS value for hit you don't need is 0.
-When looking for upgrades, try and fix the following item slots first: Your first trinket, neck and back.
Manni
