Change your spec to 3/7/31. The only flexibility you have is with the 2 points in entrapment, you can put them in Survival Tactics or Hunter vs. Wild depending on which one you desire most.

Gear: Change the gem in your gun to a Deadly Ember Topaz (+20agi, +20crit) and change the Glinting Demonseye (+20agi, +20hit) in you belt to a Delicate Inferno Ruby (+40agi). Change the Rigid Cogwheel to Crit. You should never gem straight hit.

After making the gem and talent changes use www.wowreforge.com to assist you in reforging your gear in the most optimal fashion.

Yes, you have too much haste. You only need 758 haste to cast three cobra shots between explosive shots. The additional haste above 758 isn't helping you to cast another cobra shot between explosives. Haste's primary function for SV is to reduce the cast time of Cobra Shot so that we can cast another one in-between Explosive Shots. These are called haste plateaus, where the first 758 haste is valued much more than the following haste up to 2265. That second haste plateau just isn't feasible for you in your gear. You'll have to gimp out too many other stats to reach it.

Please check out this link where Frostheim explains it in greater detail.

Lastly, here are the tests I did at Femaledwarf.com.

Before (no changes): 24788.36
After (suggested changes): 25601.90
