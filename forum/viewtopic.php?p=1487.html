Hello Agamand

The whole "problem" of ret dps is the FCFS system (First Come First Serve) which means we dont have a specific short term rotation but we just hit the skill which does the most DPS when 2 or more skills come off cooldown at the same time. The power of Glyph of Consecration comes into picture here. I noticed you arent using it now and you said you are swapping your exo and conse glyphs so I assume you are swapping it with exo but you should always have conse glyph on! Eventho it doesnt seem as just flat dps increase like Glyph of Judgement its power comes from giving you more "free" global cooldowns and mana. Those free global cds are important for ret especially with the 2pt10 bonus which resets your DS cd and makes your skill cooldowns clash even more. So start using Conse glyph as full time glyph. Use exo only if you have too much expertise from gear and dont have any other pieces to lower it, in these situations replace SoV glyph with exo.

You are correct about haste being a powerful stat when you get those tier10s but 10str/10crit are still better gems atleast for my gear, however you shouldnt stress too much about this because the difference between these gems is probably about 0.5-1dps.

I think the best time to use AW in your position is when your SoV is stacked to 5 in sync with Heroism ofc if possible. Your libram might not be stacked to 5 neither your herkuml to 20 but delaying AW too much isnt good either. You can try to time your trinket procs to proc 12-13 seconds after pull if you have a pull timer (BigWigs has some for bosses like saurfang,LK) so that your trinkets proc when you have your 5 stacks up, this isnt always possible ofc.

About Gormokk, this isnt the best place for the new libram and herkuml since the fight is short so you wont have fully stacked libram and trinket on even close the full time and yes, fights where libram and herkuml drop arent that good places to use them because its significant dps loss if your 5 stack libram drops and you have to build it up again and again. I suggest you to carry your Libram of Valiance with you for fights like this. And about Deathbringer, "without consecration being in rotation at all" sounds like you dont press your buttons fast enough or you are skipping globals or you are having insane luck with 2pt10 =), but you should always have time for conse also, at least I dont remember a fight where I didnt have time for it, just press your buttons faster so you dont miss those precious globals.

Im sure you can make your dps better by getting the conse glyph full time on, using proper librams to fights that need it and watch your skill usage more carefully so you dont skip any globals.
