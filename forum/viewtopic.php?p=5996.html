I'm not exactly the expert on SV at these gear levels, but let's see if I can give some useful advice.

Iwontshoot wrote:Current rotation priority is KS>Explosive>BlackArrow>SerpentSting>AimedShot>SteadyShot.


At these gear levels I'm pretty sure you want to have Aimed Shot higher than Serpent Sting in priority, possibly even higher than Black Arrow (but probably not).

Iwontshoot wrote:Been thinking about upgrading boots to Treads of the Wasteland Heroic so as to swap my bracers over to Scourge Hunter Vambraces,as well as then changing 2H to Distant Land Heroic. Is this optimal or should i really do other changes? I understand that for survival spec explosive and blackarrow are not affected by ArP. But ArP and high damage modifiers do affect my aimed + steady and autoshots.


While it is true that some of your attacks do not benefit from arpen, it's still a good stat even for SV hunter. Definitely better than haste, might be better than crit if I remember right too.

The biggest upgrade would probably be replacing that WFS with DBW - I think DV+DBW would be the best trinket combo for SV (although you might want to check the spreadsheet - I'm not exactly sure how well the Halion trinket ranks for SV).

Replacing that Bloodfall with something that does not have haste would probably be a good idea too.

Iwontshoot wrote:Also, I've looked into recount parses and notice that autoshot falls on my 3rd highest dps, is my button smashing wrong?


I think that's about correct. The abilities simply don't scale at the same rate, particularly Explosive Shot scales very badly compared to the physical abilities.

PS. The biggest upgrade for you is forcing your guild to recruit another retripaladin or shadowpriest so you can go MM and do proper dps... ;)
