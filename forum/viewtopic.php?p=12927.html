-Any advantages healing with mouseover macros?


No addon reliance for one, that is a huge one (like if you use Clique or Healbot or whatever and they fail because of a patch or update), using mouseover macros frees up your mouse to move which increases reaction times. Also it is better than targeting a player then hitting a keybound heal because that is fractions of a second slower added up over an entire fight.

-Any disadvantages?


Running out of macro space maybe, but other than that no, if you know how to make the macros it is really easy and failproof.

-What addons do you recommend for healing in general (such as raid frame addons)?


Grid, maybe powerauras, other than that its gg.

Basic Keybinding 101 - These are all keys that can be bound with minimal effort and comfort.

`/1/2/3/4/5
tab/q/e/r/t
f/g
z/x/c

Rebind c to v because nameplates should be up always anyway, you can also ad in f1-f5

That will also allow you to move with wsad. You cannot bind mouseover macros to the mouse without clique, just throwing that out there, the blizzard UI absorbs mouse clicks from any button so you would need clique. Just use your mouse buttons for something like cooldowns.
