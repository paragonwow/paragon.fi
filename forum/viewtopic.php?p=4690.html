Dragoonahn wrote:How is the new Soul Shard system? Does it make life easier, or is it more complicated with how it actually functions? Any details on this subject would be appreciated.

Also, in your opinion will Aff still be the best leveling spec?


Soul shard system is, in a word, awesome. You have no idea how much I hated the old shard system. Or actually, you might, as you play a lock as well. The most awesome thing about it is that you don't have to keep draining for shards. Summoning pets, creating healthstones etc don't need any.


The new shard mechanics:
Shard UI, displaying the 3 shards:

->




Basically, you can boost a number of spells with Soulburn. It's not hard to use, really, it's just something you'll need to try out a few times and learn. I'll write the detailed info on the boosted spells and what they do (currently) if you want it and edit this post.

You can also quickly regain shards (and some health) with Soul Harvest, which is a new baseline skill.


Another very interesting change is that the curse system got revamped - damaging curses are now Banes (of Agony and of Doom) and can overlap with Curse of Weakness and Curse of Elements for example.

The reason I mentioned that change (which is quite nice already) is that there's a new goddamn awesome talent called Bane of Havoc. It's a 21-pointer in Destruction tree. You can cast it on a target, and 15% of the damage you do is also dealt to the Baned target. You can cleave with it (imagine Twins in ToC) or you can cast it on a boss mob when AoE'ing (imagine Anub'Arak, Lich King etc).


And yes, at the moment Affliction (in my opinion, of course) is an excellent leveling spec. I'd choose it over the others.
Synti / Synjin / Vorel
