Why so red...

Anyways, getting the heroic versions of the items that provide ArP (Toskk bracers, DBW, Frostbrood ring, Aldriana gloves etc) will give you extra ArP. Reaching ArP cap is a "goal", not a requirement. You shouldn't prioritize ArP just to reach the cap - you should prioritize it when it provides the biggest dps upgrade. However, most of the time it actually will be the biggest dps upgrade, as the value of ArP increases a lot once you get closer to the cap. Best way to determine that is of course by using a spreadsheet.

I'm also sure a lot of rogues who get close to 1360 ArP use ArP food to reach (closer to) the cap.
