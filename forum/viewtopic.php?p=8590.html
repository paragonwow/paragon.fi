Dear Paragon members, especially the web crew,

Before I write my feedback I'd like to say that I am very well aware of the facts that the page is 1. not finished and 2. the race for World First Hard-mode kills is more important at the moment. Which is absolutely understandable. So I might name points which are already on your to-do list. I'll seperate the post into two parts, one regarding general errors and one about my personal opinion.

Errors / Bugs

- People who post comments are currently "not veryfied". Hope the verification system comes fast because it looks kinda weird right now
- On the individual player pages it is currently not possible to go directly to the players armory profile. You can only click on one of this specs in order to get linked. (Given the situation that the Armory is actually working)

Personal Opinion

I enjoy the new design though I think that the current set of colors matches Cataclysm but not Paragon. The old page has a very nice set of colors. It was clean, clear and straight forward. If I were to relate the colors to Paragon as a guild it felt focused and to the point. I miss this a little with the current colors. The font size seems to be a little huge right now. Again in relation to the old page, where everything felt slightly more clean and elegant due to smaller font size. I know it makes sense to use the screensize better but having a larger font does not do the job. It rather feels like greater letters cover lesser content. This goes especially for the news or for the names in the roster overview.

The missing display of the latest forum activities on the front page is something that really bothers me. It has brought my own forum activity down a lot because I do not instantly see when a thread has had a new reply and I can check what it is. The forums and the personal work a lot of Paragon members put in there to answer questions was a key element to the charm of the old page and to Paragon as a whole, at least for me. If I had to choose I would take the Latest Forum Posts over Latest Comments on the front page anytime. Seeing that various people have started to write spec and other class related questions below some members profiles in the comments tells me, that I am not the only one for whom the forums have gone slightly out of sight with the new layout.


So in short: Give the page a more Paragon look through the respective colors. Adapt the fontsizes to smaller to make it look less bulky. Lacking content will come over time, that is natural. Bring back the forums to the front page.

I gladly offer my help if possible. I enjoy giving feedback and tidy up stuff, also in detail.

/Niroth
