1 haste rating = 0.639 spell power
1 crit rating = 0.514 spell power

I calculated Reign of the Unliving is ~0.7% more DPS than Phylactery of the Nameless Lich if Lunar Eclipse is crit capped.

My questions are these:
Considering the Lunar Eclipse crit cap is around 900 crit rating (depending on your buffs and current INT), will you continue to stack crit rating over haste rating? Would you consider regemming Ashen Band of Endless Destruction with a Runed Cardinal Ruby? Have you considered using double Reigns of the Unliving instead of a crit rating trinket such as Phylactery of the Nameless Lich or Muradin's Spyglass? Have I missed something in my calculations or a short-coming of WrathCalcs?

Thanks for the response Lappé. I agree that as soon as you add latency into the equation, crit rating becomes a much more desirable stat. You also make a good point about the trinket procs at the beginning of the fight, since most boss fights are ~ 3-5 minutes now (with a few exceptions). This may change however for heroic modes if boss encounters start lasting long enough.

Concerning spec, I think 2/2 Imp MF is a must have regardless of tier gear. This leaves 1 point for genesis and 2 points to float around in either typhoon, Owlkin, brambles, or gale winds.

Concerning glyphs, it maybe wise to use the starfall glyph over insect swarm to add the dodge debuff for tanks come Heroic ICC.

One thing I still want to test is the haste level versus # of starfires during a lunar rotation. I realize haste (over the soft cap) is not a linear conversion because once you can squeeze in an extra starfire during a Lunar Eclipse proc, it becomes a much larger DPS boost.
