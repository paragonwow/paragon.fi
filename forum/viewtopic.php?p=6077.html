If you are ArP hard-capped then Aimed Shot does about the same damage as Chimera Shot, but Aimed Shot has a 12% higher chance to crit through talents. Because of the extra 12% crit, it can be better to use Aimed Shot before Chimera Shot because of Improved Steady Shot, using your Improved Steady Shot procs on Aimed Shot can potentially be a DPS increase because you gain more damage per shot. Having said that, at ArP cap with decent gear the damage difference between Chimera and Aimed shot is so little, and raid-buffed crit % is so high that you wouldn't even notice a DPS gain, even on the spreadsheet or femaledwarf.com it should only be a ~2-3 DPS difference.
