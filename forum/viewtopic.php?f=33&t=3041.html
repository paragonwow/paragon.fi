Googlezz,

After viewing your armory I have concluded that the only trinkets that will provide a dps increase are as follows:

From lowest upgrade to biggest upgrade (replacing Darkmoon Card which is the weaker trinket)

Insignia if the Corrupted Mind [384]
Variable Pulse Lightning Capacitor [397]
Insignia if the Corrupted Mind [397]
Will of Unbinding [390]
Will of Unbinding [403]
Insignia if the Corrupted Mind [410]
Will of Unbinding [416]

Anything else was either such a small increase that it wouldn't be worth acquiring or a dps loss (aka the two trinkets you listed) Getting any of these would prove to increase your damage.

If your having issues reaching the hit cap I would purchase the 397 Valor BoE boots from an alt or the AH. I can gaurantee you will use them for a while because you wont replace them until H Warmaster Blackhorn.

-Guromin
