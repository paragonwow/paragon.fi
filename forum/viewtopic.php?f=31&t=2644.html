The proc from Dragonwrath ignores the ICD of all procs and trinkets and uses its own. For example, if Arcane Blast procced MirrorICD1, Wrath of Tarecgosa (Dragonwrath proc) can proc MirrorICD2 even if MirrorICD1 is on cooldown. Similiarly, if you had a spell proc TailoringEnchant1 and put it on cooldown, Wrath of Tarecgosa can still proc it because it doesn't think it's off cooldown (it uses TailoringEnchant2's cooldown). Basically you'll have close to or double the uptime of everything that procs.
