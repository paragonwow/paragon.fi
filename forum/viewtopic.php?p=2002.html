Hello, I'm not going to be holding any hands here on gearing as there are spreadsheets around which do that for you (see my last post for links, you'll need openoffice or excel to open it). There's also a pretty decent java app that does the same thing on the elitist jerks rogue forums. Generally though going for expertise / hit after reaching the arp cap is the way to go. Arp vs AP still favors Arp heavily, as even though rupture is now the main finisher, it still isn't a huge part of your dps and most of your attacks still benefit from armor penetration.

Basically high rupture rotations might be the better way to go right now as they buffed it in the recent patch, but in case you still want to play evisc based specs (which by no means are bad) you'll want to keep snd up and only do 5 point eviscerates. The number of combos used on snd is 5 if you can pool them within 3-4 seconds of snd dropping, if you can't you just refresh it with the amount of combo points you have at the moment it's about to drop.

On the question about rupture rotation, I think you might be doing too big SNDs. Aldriana's spreadsheet shows that the optimal snd for my gear should be around 3-4 points and anything over that is basically too much as you need to refresh it before using another rupture anyway. However, you should rather refresh SND when you cap on energy even if it has time left to achieve the highest amount of rupture uptime possible. The main thing is that your snd still is big enough for you to do another one before it drops. You should look into aldriana's sheet for optimal rotations for your own gear and go on from there. From what I hear his high rupture modeling isn't perfect, but it should be accurate enough.
