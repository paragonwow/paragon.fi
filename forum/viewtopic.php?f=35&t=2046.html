I can't help but reply to this. I tested the spec myself when I saw it. One thing I found took some getting used to was the lack of surge of light. That being said, I found it to be quite effective, especially in overpowered situations. If you are simply running a 5 man to cap out valor for the week or something of that sort, the improvements to the instant cast spells alone is more than sufficient for keeping the party alive without the other talents. I was also doing this more for fun and experimentation. I found it to be different to say the least, but extra dmg coming out of a priest even a small amount, that is also very mana efficient is rather nice. This is especially nice in a situation like I said where healing wouldn't really be that intensive to begin with. 15% increased healing appears to be just as awesome in some situations for holy, as disc. Thats....a relieving thought, that perhaps at somepoint with a few tweaks perhaps in a patch, this might actually become rather viable in specific circumstances. Its worth keeping an eye on. Thanks for the chance to break up some monotony Jhaz.
