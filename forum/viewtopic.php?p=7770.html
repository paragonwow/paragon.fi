Hello,

there seems to be nothing wrong with your gear atleast, except you do have alot of int+haste gems. Pure int is really the way to go if possible, cause it gives you so much more.

If you find yourself doing low dps on fights that you have to move alot, just try thinking ahead and positioning yourself to place you _know_ you dont have to move any time soon from. And then only move the necessary amount while refreshing dots/spamming moonfire. Also, try thinking about your dots more, they are a really viable way of dealing damage now that they scale better. Try to keep them up 100%. I cant really say a whole lot more since you didnt provide more information.
