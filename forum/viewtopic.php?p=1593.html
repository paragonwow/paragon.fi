For the ret part I dont see any major flops in your gearing, only thing you should try to change is to get some hit item so you wouldnt have to gem for it, Tiny abo maybe? Tiny abo is the best trinket for ret since its procing like crazy, DW isnt one of the best trinket for ret but its not totally useless either if you had bad luck or couldnt get any better. Being that little under exp and hit cap doesnt have too much impact on your dps, but I would probably put one 10str/10hit gem on your ring so you get the hit cap and 4str, you should always use 10str/10crit on yellow slots when you gain 4 or more str from it.

I also noticed you didnt have Seal of Command on your ret spec which you should if you want to make some aoe damage, I would take points out of Stoicism to SoC and Divine Purpose since Stoicism is pretty much useless in PvE. You can do pretty sick cleaving with SoC on trash and bosses that have lots of adds that needs to be killed and Divine Purpose gives you that rng 4% miss on spells which is nice when it happens to "proc" + the very situational stunremove with freedom.
