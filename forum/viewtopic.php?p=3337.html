That's right my friends, the only trinkets that EJ says are better than it would be both versions of Deathbringer's Will (I got mine this week, yay!) and heroic Death's Choice. Apparently, the War Token even out-DPSes normal Death's Choice, but not by too much.

As it is, the 0/17/54 build shows melee being the highest portion of our damage. Many DKs also have a 340 haste boost every minute with the Engineering glove enchant, plus DBW procs.

According to EJ, Haste is so valuable that Unholy DKs may want to start switching out Inscribed Ametrines for Fierce ones (i.e., replace crit with haste).

I'd like to know what our Paragon friends think about this. I sort of groaned when I saw it because haste started out being a bottom priority stat for us in 3.3, went up in value after the SS nerf and is now being declared to be even more valuable than ArP.
