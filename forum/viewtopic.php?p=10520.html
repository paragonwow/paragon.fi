Sounds pretty normal. You can encode the videos after you have recorded them but the files that fraps puts our are going to be quite big. There is almost no compression on them after recording because even the high-end computers most likely wouldn't be able to run the game and encode the video in real time. The only ways of lowering the file size (while recording) are either lowering FPS or resolution but you can get 1 TB HDDs for like 60 euros or something if you need more space.
