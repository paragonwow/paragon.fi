Gem transmuting is quite lucrative for alchemy, you get to use alchemy-only potions and double duration flasks. In case you want resistances for some fights or learning you can also pop a resistance flask with +40 all resistance alchemy bonus.

Jewelcrafting goes nicely with alchemy since you can then not only transmute your gems but also cut them (alongside meta gems). That requires a bit more effort to get some proper recipes than alchemy though.
