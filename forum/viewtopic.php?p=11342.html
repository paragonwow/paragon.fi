I did a bunch of gold with Darkmoon Faire cards in the beginning of Wrath, but have no idea how to make tons of gold nowadays. Myself I've just raided (and wiped very little) and then done some transmutes and jc dailies every day for a few months. We've also done alt raids where we've gotten maelstrom crystals and boe epics to sell. This way I've gotten like 100k in around 4 months (after spending +60k in the beginning of Cataclysm) and geared up 2 characters after my main.

I just don't use that much money on anything.
