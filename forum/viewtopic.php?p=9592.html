1.
exo - how - tv - cs - judgement - holy wrath - conce is the priority system
hold up inq 100%.

CDs. AW and GoAK is wonderfull to cast at the same time. when AW is out cast Zealotry and try to get in so many TVs you can before GoAK is out

This macro is a marginal dps increase but you maybe have to spam it a little depending on your connection and gcd
/cast zealotry
/cast templar's verdict

Same with
/cast avenging wrath
/cast hammer of wrath
and if you got Engineering as i do. putt in Tazik's shocker in the AW macro as it benefits from dps increasing spells

a normal fight with no luck in procs should start this way
cs - judgement - holy wrath - cs - conce - cs - inq - AW - Goak - HoW then procede with the priority list.

2.
Geming, str and only str meta 51 crit and 1% spell reflect(it got 2 red gems requirement so derby it is best untill patch comes then the str+increased crit dmg becaumes the best i think)
Reforging, Hit cap - Exp cap - Crit - Haste - Mastery

il take and pull in a stat compersion so you can understand easyier how much strength overwhelms all other stats and how sucky mastery is.
