As lazeil discretely pointed out...
That trinket is BiS for every tank out there on certain fights.
Omnotron, Maloriak, Nefarian, Ascendant Council, Cho'gall and Sinestra are encounters where you will recieve alot of love from your healers if you have it.

Protip(ha ha)
Combining prismatic elixir with 225 mastery isn't bad either - If you have kings/motw + mirror + prismatic, you will resist a LOT of damage.
Not a bad trinket overall, reforging 128 mastery to 128 dodge and it's pretty good for druids =)
Stamina is nice, but well if u take double the damage then you will feed on your healers (tears) mana.
