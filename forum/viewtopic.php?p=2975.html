Hey hautiejah

Affliction warlock dps is nowadays too much relied on procs. We can do decent 11k+ dps almost in every encounter but topping the meters doesnt only require you to have high proc rate on eradication and devious minds ( Apollox had 40%+ uptime on them both) but to get those procs at the right time. For example, when BL is used getting a simultaneous proc from those two boosts your dps drastically(apollox dps peaked at ~23k at the beginning so im just going to assume that they used BL at the start and that he got these procs :) ). And ofcourse, getting these procs <25% is again a much higher dps buff than getting them lets say when the boss is at 50%. I really hope they change this in cata since I like playing affliction.

Basicly he just pumped the beasts full of corruption, nuked the boss and hoped for eradication procs -> low damage on beasts

on a side note you're missing one of the awesome lock minor glyphs which are about 2k dps each so there you go :)

I hope this helped
