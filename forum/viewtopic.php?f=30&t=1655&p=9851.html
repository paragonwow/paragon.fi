Hi Duntra,hope it can help you:

1) Generally i think that both rings itemized with haste still always better (try to farm Ring of The Boy Emperor, the archeology ring).

2)Atm (15 sec duration) i think that the best ways to use DP are: take few tics of it and not all because 15 seconds are quite a lot in most of situations; obiuvsly the other good way to use it is popping it when there's not heavy raid/tank damages (but be very carefull); i can suggest you many examples:

1) Halfus Wyrmbreaker---> pop it just a sec before Furious Roar
2) Valiona & Theralion---> pop it in the "Valiona to Theralion" interphase (when popping void zones)
3)Ascendant Council---> pop it just a sec before Terrastra and Arion going below 25% ( DP continue in the brothers's fusion break)
4)Cho'gall--->Usually i use it with Potion of Concentration just a few seconds before p2 (call it on ventrilo ;-)

1)Magmaw---> Always when him is impaled
2)Omnitron Defense System---> Always when there is the Toxitron/Arcanotron combination
3)Chimaeron---> During Offline phase if you see that aoe healings are enought to keep the raid alive for the next 2 caustic slimes
4)Maloriak--->Imho if you need it, the best time is during "End Blue to Green Phase"
5)Atramedes---> If you need it,during air phases
6)Nefarian--->Usually i use it with Potion of Concentration just a few seconds before p3 (call it on ventrilo ;-)

1)Conclave of The Four Winds---> Never used ^_^ but suppose that in every moment except during specials abilitys
2)Al'Akir---> Try to use it only during the plane collapse between p2 and p3 for 2-3 tics


Hope it help you = )
