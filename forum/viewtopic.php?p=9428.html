Blizzard originally stated that once you killed any boss in normal, you couldn't switch back to heroic. However around when people proved this false by skipping Magmaw they reverted to saying it's working as intended. As an answer to your question, as long as you've met the requirements to activate heroic mode (killed Al'akir, Cho'gall or Nefarian on normal in their respective instances) you can "skip" your way to any boss you wish to kill. As long as you've killed Nefarian on normal, you can kill every other boss on normal and go for heroic Nefarian. Skipping farmbosses doesn't really make much sense, though.
Manni
