First off, according to http://elitistjerks.com/f80/t37900-dots ... read/#Gems ; I should break socket colors using 23 spell power in all sockets (after meta is activated) with bonuses less than or equal to 5 spell power. This being said I see many people geming 12sp 10haste even if the socket bonus is equal to 5sp (especially the ashen verdict ring). Stat------------early T10 / high end T9(icc245)

haste rating-----------2.04
