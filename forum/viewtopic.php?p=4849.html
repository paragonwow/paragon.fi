Only have an old phone to take pics so quality will most likely cause severe eye bleeding.

Desk

Yes, that gigantic box on the right side of the picture is Cathode ray tube-display! It's big, heavy, ugly and does 100Hz. I use it sometimes for FPS-games, it's been stuck there as my secondary display for 4 years soon.

If you look very closely you can see a glimpse of my subwoofer under the table, I am guilty of abusing it as a footrest aswell ;(.

Desktop

Foobar2000+mIRC+windows sidebar gadgets on the right hand display. Oh and the taskbar is from software called DisplayFusion, I keep my maindisplays taskbar on auto-hide mode.

Another super useful program I use is Synergy. It lets me use my laptop on the left with my main computers keyboard & mouse via network, it works just like additional display, just need to take my cursor to my laptops display by moving it out from the left side of my main display. Even the copy paste works from computer to computer! It works amaizingly well and smoothly, so I recommend it to everyone with multiple computers on desk.
