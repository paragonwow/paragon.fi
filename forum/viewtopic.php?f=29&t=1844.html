Are you sure you entered your numbers correctly to the Simulator? The values you input need to be your completely unbuffed _caster_ form stats. I guess it's a fairly common mistake to input stats when you are in cat form. The simulator then calculates your buffed stats correctly depending what buffs you choose to include. Other than that it should work right from the "bag". Although I usually use 480 seconds as fight duration to get more accurate results, because most HC fights last around 8mins.

I'd say the dps numbers you posted should be pretty good for your current gear. Also Gear, Talents and "rotation" are ok too.

For tracking feral buffs/debuffs I use NeedToKnow and TellMeWhen. Other addons include Icehud2, Omen, OmniCC, Shadowed Unitframes, Tidy Plates - Threat Plates and Big Wigs. All in all I use a fairly light setup.

Hope this helps a bit.
