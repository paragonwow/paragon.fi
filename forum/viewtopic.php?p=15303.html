Hmm, kind of hard to help you in that situation when you are not even sure if you want the change or not. Myself I use mfclip, but it took a a couple of weeks (read: a couple of raids) to learn to play with it properly.

So I'd just advice you to force yourself get comfortable with the one you decide to use. I think there is only 3 options. Dots visible in unit frame debuffs, dots visible in some form of bars (tracks your target for instance), or dots visible on top of the unitframe. Just pick one and give yourself enough time to get familiar with it, and only then decide if it was good or not.

You can also take a look at how Manni has his dots visible if you hate bars.
