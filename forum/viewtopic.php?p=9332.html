If you want to be a good hunter, you're never "BM hunter" or "SV hunter" or "MM hunter", you're simply "hunter" and spec whatever the situation requires. At the moment, the situation is such that SV is the best dps spec by far, however if the raid setup is lacking a retri paladin bringing a BM hunter is the next best solution.

What you should do is to learn to play any spec needed, and always bring the spec needed for the current raid setup and boss. At the moment that means having SV and BM specs ready to go, with a good selection of pets for raid buffs and other specific utility purposes.
