@mikkelthejl
Ohhh dear god! I'm sorry to say, but I'm not even sure where to start. All your gems are wrong, you have the wrong meta head and you dont reforge your gear:( You miss a ton of enchants and you miss the ebonsteel belt enchant. Your specc is also totally off (especially on your iLevel) 0/3 Moonglow - Really?;)

I would start by replacing every single gem of yours.
Red socket: 40+ int (Brilliant Inferno Ruby)
Blue socket: 20 + int | 20+ Spirit (Purified Demons Eye)
Orange socket 20+ Int | 20+ haste (Reckless Ember Topaz) Your number 1 stats you should -always- try to get the most as possible of is Int, but dont go crazy on gemming all your gear with int as the gem bonus is still usually worth more. (UNLESS THE GEM BONUS IS SOMETHING LIKE 10 Crit. Then just go bananas on your Int)
Also get full enchants.

Secondly, I would reforge all of my gear (In your case) to spirit (or mastery especially if you raid 10 man! Its a great secondary stats at low iLevel gear when you dont have a lot of mana/mana regen). Later you can go for more haste, but not with poor gear. (Try to end up with 915 haste though (12.5% Assuming you have the 5% haste buff from either a Elem Shammy, Moonkin or a Spriest) as that should be your absolute minimum haste you should aim for. It gives you 1 extra tick on your rejuvenation, plus it's just horrable to play with less haste
- If you cant reach the 12.5% haste cap you should ask one of your warlocks for Dark Intent (if they for some reason don't alredy use it on you)

Then, I would change my specc.
1: Furor isnt as good as moonglow! Change 3/3 Furor with 3/3 Moonglow.
2: you really really -really- should go for Master shapeshifter. you can't just TOSS that 4% healing away!
3: You can remove some points in Nature's Bounty/Living seed (and potentialy get rid of Nature's Cure if you have other healers in your raid such as a priest to take care of that. 3 healers (10man) and 5-6 players (25m) with that ability is a waste on most encounters) To add some points in better bonuses such as mentioned earlier: Master Shapeshifter, Malfurion's Gift 2/2 (And Nature's Swiftmend since it got a major buff) but I guess thats all about prefferences (I love it though)
4: (After you changed what I mentioned earlier) I wouldn't go for Genesis rather than furor in the early stages, as you will go sooooo fast oom. 3/3 Moonglow and 2/3 Furor should keep your mana stable, then get rid of Furor if you want Genesis
5: Make sure you have the right glyphs.
Prime: Glyph of Lifebloom, Glyph of Swiftmend and Glyph of Rejuvenation.
Major: Glyph of Wild Growth, Glyph of Rebirth, Glyph of Innervate, Glyph of Healing Touch (Ditch one of the 3 last)
6: Your gear isnt fully leather. You lose 5% Intellect this way. Not worth it at all. You should aim for full leather gear.
- Also worth mentioning: Your using the wrong epic enchant for legs lol:) You will want to use [Powerful Ghostly Spellthread] for the spirit. Spirit > Stamina for Resto Druids as far as I know.

At Last, to use your mastery as good as possible (under heavy damage spikes) - always use Wild Growth, and then hot your wild growth targets with rejuvenation, listen to Hans Zimmer - Pirates of the Caribbean - Soundtrack while raiding, and don't be a Regrowth spammer! I promise you will top the meters in your guild, as they have 0 bosses killed (Which I just noticed)

Hope this will help you become a better healer;) feel free to ask chain questions as I have very little to do on my spare time.
