13 arcane shots? I can understand using it while moving if every other instant is on CD, but you're not going to be moving that much on Festergut...

You're casting 1.7*76 + 1.5*(27+23+13+4+1) = 231.2sec out of 289 - 18 = 271sec possible in the fight. That's a lot of idle time between shots, high latency or just not pressing buttons fast enough?

Additionally, your spec is horrible; 3/3 Focused Aim, even though you already have 6.77% hit from gear? No point in Go for the Throat, your pet is doing about 600dps less than mine on the same boss.

Using Rapid Fire during Bloodlust isn't the best idea either, since Bloodlust alone will cause your steady shots to be capped by the GCD, so you're not gaining full benefit from Rapid Fire.
