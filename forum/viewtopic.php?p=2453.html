Helping out like this takes roughly ten times more time than replying to some discussion thread. I'm not going to go over every single log with a magnifying glass - you should check the basic stuff for yourself (ie, Sunders/Expose are up and whatnot.. basic stuff like this are missing every once in a while, or the uptime is bad).

With that said, you're missing a ton of free RP from AMS. This is pretty bad. Another thing is that you're not using Unbreakable Armor enough. Again, I'm not at all familiar with WoL - I can't find your pet damage anywhere. Are you using your ghouls? [edit:] After the hint about how to view pet damage, it seems like you aren't.

For ERW, you pop it when you have some kind of +damage buff or procs, as long as there's not a crucial part of a fight where you need burst.

For KM, FS/Rime whatever, feel free to use the procs up asap as long as you don't delay your runes. Use the RGP (Rune Grace Period) to your advantage. Rime isn't worth it unless you're going to run into a free GCD sometime soon (not a RP-heavy fight) or there's only one target.
