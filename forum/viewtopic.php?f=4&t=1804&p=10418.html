My guilds alt 10m raid group knocked out 5/6 bwd, 2/4 bot, 1/2 TotFW in a matter of like 4 hours and 2 of the 10 people didnt know the fights very well and some didnt know how to play they're role very well in some fights.

Our setup was

Prot pally
blood dk


Holy Priest
Disc Priest
Resto Shaman


Enhance Shaman
Frost (occasional fire) mage
Fire mage
Demo Lock (occasional dest)
Fury warr

On some fights where it required 1 tank I made my blood dk go unholy.

We had 0-2 wipes on each fight. The only exception is chim which we wiped 3 times on. I cant say this is the best as the pally could be a druid but the raid wall is nice.

We did use drums though if I remember right and then used might. So we covered that buff.

As again I'm not sure this is the best setup but we had alot of luck with it and keep in mind we all pretty much knew the general idea of fights and experienced them first hand on our mains
