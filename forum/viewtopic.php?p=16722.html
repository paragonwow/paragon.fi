Up to this point Ive tried both MM and Survival specs. I shoot for about 1250 haste in either spec. It seemed as MM I had a much easier time sustaining a consistent burst through each tendon. However as survival I struggled depending on whether RF was up and how many adds I could use for explosive trap. However, I get the feeling that survival has a much higher potential depending on the techniques used to tank and kill bloods.

Here is my Armory link: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/character/f ... les/simple

Here are My personal logs: http://raidbots.com/epeenbot/us/frostmane/bowjangles/

These are the guild Logs: http://worldoflogs.com/guilds/136997/ *note 12/28-29 and 1/05*

We have only put in about 20 attempts into the fight but Damage done to Tendons Appears to be a chief concern. I so far have only seen about 6 logs of guilds that have a single hunter in 10H. but in that atmosphere the highest parse seems to be SV at something right below 6 mil (Note: Most of these logs aren't in English).

When I play Marks I came to this as being the easiest way to consistently damage the tendon:

1. Pool Focus and use 1 RF on each tendon burn as they last about 23 secs w/ Ai Dump.
2. Readiness immediately after RF to allow for readiness CD to regen.
3. Potions will prob be used on second tendon in a kill.

I see problems arising with this on the fourth lift as RF and readi will potentially both be on CD and am debating on how to overcome that.

When SV I perceive the Max Potential to be:
1. Hunters Mark and maintain a normal rotation so as to let sting to continue as the tendon is unexposed.
2. I change the norm by explosive traping instead of black arrow to allow for more Lock and Load.
3. I use RF on the first expose and the most necessary expose when it is back up around approx. 7 min mark. (potion with second RF)

Again your time and assistance is appreciated.
