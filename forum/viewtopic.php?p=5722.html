Im not synti but I guess I can respond to the disease question.

The optimal time to refresh diseases with pestilence is in the 0-3s time frame. drop the idea of refreshing them every 9s. Not only would that be quite a dps loss, but it would also be very unneccesary since its not hard at all to refresh them proberly, unless your just a really bad player in the first place :) Get a good addon to track your runes, and its a cakewalk. If you are wondering what runeaddon to get, go check out the screenshot of synti's ui, he's using a really great one for the job.
