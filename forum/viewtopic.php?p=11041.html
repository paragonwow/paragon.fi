On a more serious tone and less swear words, I really wish people could see these forum questions from our perspective.

It takes a lot of time to check through combat logs/armory pages writing the answer. It also feels really frustrating to answer very basic questions, the ones which would have answers available on various other internet communities. To us it seems like the asker is just lazy and hasn't spent any time in researching the matter himself.

Also, the point Jubeto made is completely true. We don't have rules to answer every single question we have here or be nice to everyone. We do that because we respect our fellow wow gamers and want to help them out when we have time for it. Starting to whisper various Paragon members and whining because you didn't get an answer (in 1 day) loses that respect and no one wants to deal with you anymore. After you whispered me and basically asked if I can force our locks to answer you, I think Jubeto's answer is more than you deserve.

Please, when writing to these class forums always start with the introduction post which says that you should state your matter clear and provide data like combatlogs/armory/other stuff you think is important when we go through your issue. Then our member answers it or doesn't. It's that simple. Usually well written polite questions get an answer easier than the ones that show the writer hasn't put effort into it. Also, sometimes someone else from the community might answer you and direct you to another site with information you seek.

Lastly, we don't exist here for your questions. All these answers are extra bonus, please keep that in mind. Still, I'm very glad we have so many people in here discussing the game we all love with us. Just have the right attitude when you post a question here and we'll all continue to have fun.
