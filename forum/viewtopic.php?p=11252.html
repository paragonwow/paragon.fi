Your rotation is wrong.

Do:

Bloodthirst > Raging Blow > Bloodthirst > GCD (Fill in with Colossus Smash, Slam - Only if you have the proc, or a Shout)

Use Heroic Strike when over 60 rage
Use Berserker Rage if your enrage isn't procced

Heroic Strike shouldn't be in your rotation - even more emphasis on this with the incoming patch 4.0.6
