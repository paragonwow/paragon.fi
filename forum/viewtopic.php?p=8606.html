I'm not Chinese but I'm an American living in China at the moment so I figured I'd help out with your request. I don't know if any of these players are in fact Chinese so I'll be straight up with my findings (using my handy iPhone translator). Please correct me if I'm wrong, as I'm still learning the language.

Unfortunately, neither Xe nor Jha are phonemes in Chinese. The closest might be xi or shi, and zhe or jia. Zhi has some possibilities, like "infantile" or "vagina" (fourth tone). Good to remember that Chinese is very dependent on context, and there are literally hundreds of words each sound could mean, most very normal things. I just chose out the funniest "accidental" names they could have been. Don't worry Zhi, your name also has meanings like "wisdom", "toe", and "manufacture".
