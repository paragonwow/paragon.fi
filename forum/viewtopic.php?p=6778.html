For the "use own texture" feature to work in Powerauras, you must have had the buff on you after you added it to PA or it won't show. So just cast it on yourself once and see if that helps.

If you already tried that, make sure you for example have the Resting [X] part ticked off as by default it makes your auras not work when you are resting. And be sure the other checkboxes are as they should be.
