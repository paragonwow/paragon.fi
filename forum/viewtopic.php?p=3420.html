Like I said in my earlier post...one can argue, if it's better to cast lb before the hs pyro (like I usually do)..

If you cast lb first, you get better lb uptime and less ignite munching but lose some hs procs. If you cast hs pyro first instead, you won't lose those hs procs and perhaps you get a little better t10 2pc uptime (well some of it's wasted, since you cast lb after the pyro).
