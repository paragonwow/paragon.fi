Hello, sorry for late respond (im always slacking in forums while we have progerss raids)

1) This is atm my stat prio for _RAIDS_
Agi -> hit cap -> Crit -> 1850 haste -> mastery.
After you have 1850 haste u can shoot 1 more shot in your rotation. Also excess haste ain't good, put it on mastery. Until you have 4 pt t12 u should stick with 4pt t11 :(

2) Crit is your priority rating. go all out crit.

3) Only for dumping focus, dont let your focus overflow.

4) Keep your sting up 100%, keep your ISS up 100-95%, hard cast aimeds while BL / RF ( dont stack bl and rf ), shoot chimera every time its off cd. If its possible try not to shoot lots of arcanes in row, cause you lose bit benefit of your sic 'em if they crit in row.

5) i'd go Flyid death, cause more agi and rating

6) Chimera, chimera is your best execution time ability and if you get couple extra chimeras off its lots of damage, RF is horse shit, clips steadies etc. just bad glyph. kill shot glyph reduces your termination talents effectiveness and also gives you less focuse on execution phase due 1 more global on ks.

7) Crit and mastery scales best with aoe
Mess with the best, die like the rest!

With 1348 haste rating, 3/3 pathing, full raid buffs and ISS up Steady's cast is 1.25 seconds. With the chimera glyph you can fit 2 arcanes and 5 steadies (5th one pushes chimera .25 seconds) in between chimera shots. When MMM procs simply replace the 5th steady and you'll have a completely tight rotation. Nowhere in there would 500 additional haste rating or roughly 4% haste give you enough room for another shot.

Unless the 500 haste rating gives you enough focus regen (highly unlikely) to fire 3 arcanes and 4 steadies between chimeras. Even if that were the case you would have downtime in the rotation.
