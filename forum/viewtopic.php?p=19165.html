It's super easy to do it with grid, though.

ANYTHING you can do with any raid frame addon out there, you can do with Grid, you just need an ''addon-addon'' to put it like that.

Here is a list of mine:

Grid (obviously)
GridIndicatorCornerIcons (Which might be what you are looking for)
GridIndicatorCornerPlus (More squares in corners and at sides)
GridManaBars
GridStatusDirectionArrows (arrows pointing towards people out of range)
GridStatusPriestAoE (for my Priest alt Prayer, shows who it's most efficient to cast prayer on)
GridStatusRaidDebuff + GridStatusRaidDebuff MoP (automatically adds debuffs in raids)
GridStatusRaidIcons (skull, cross etc... on players)

Grid requires a ton of work, though, but you can have it exactly like you want it.

Edit: And yea, OmniCC, ofcourse.
