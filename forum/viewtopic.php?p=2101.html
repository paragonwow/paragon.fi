To update my previous, half-awake posts:

Expertise, I miss-calculated this. My DPS has still increased, but it's not worth my attacks missing. It sucks going IT > PS > BS (MISS) > SS > BS > BS (Oh, it goes on), it will totally destroy your main rotation. It's not THAT bad, but it sucks to have to re-adjust. My point being, if you can get a piece of ARMOR (NOT your weapon (Such as 251 to 264)) to go Expertise, I'd recommened it. Also, there is food for +40 Expt. and +40 Stam. - Yes, you lose +40 Str. but it's most definetly worth it instead of your attacks missing - sometimes it happens 4x in a row.

Unholy-Frost is way better than Unholy-Blood. Yes, you lose the crit and the Bladed Armor - so what? You gain Frost Damage, Shadow Damage, Personal Haste (20%), AND 4% strength along with Mind Freeze being RP free. You also get to use extra DC's if you have to (I've found that it happens frequently). Also, instead of using Indestructible potions, you will use Potion of Speed.

To continue with Potion of Speed - Try to time it when you're going to use Gargoyle along with Heroism/Bloodlust. In most ICC fights you will find that Gargoyle will be used atleast twice, sometimes 3-4 times. I usually will pop Gargoyle initially anyways, regardless of Heroism/Bloodlust. I'll save my Potion of Speed for when Heroism/Bloodlust is used and Gargoyle is up. For the most part, I don't have a problem getting this done.
