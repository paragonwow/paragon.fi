Yea, didn't check your buff gains that much on other then Saurfang. And in that fight you took wild magic so that it actually faded before heroism or EM. But it's just a minor thing.

For pre potting I personally use only wild magic atm. Mainly because we tend to use blood lust at start of the boss most of the time and even if we don't I open with EM+engineering gloves which takes my haste so high that speed potion would be pretty much wasted as my cast time would drop below gcd.

For dps totems, fire elemental is the strongest one. For the other totems, magma totem is great when there is multiple targets and for single target fights searing totem is the best way to go.

I don't use CL that much in single target, it's good some times to sync your cast so that you get lava burst soon as it comes off cool down but other then that I don't cast it unless there is more then 1 target. If there is more then 1 target available CL should be used on every cool down since it's fairly big dps increase.
