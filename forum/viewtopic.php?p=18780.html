You need to hit an hostile target to AoE heal.
If an enemy is the prism, they take Holy damage and radiate healing to 5 nearby allies within 15 yards.
If an ally is the prism, they are healed and radiate Holy damage to 5 nearby enemies within 15 yards.

wowhead : http://www.wowhead.com/spell=114165

I like that spell because it's a nice "filter" when our main spell are on CD and you can heal a friendly target to build agro on all the hostile around him..
