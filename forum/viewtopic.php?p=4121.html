I'm not Tsukky but I'm still going to answer your question. I bet You mixed hard- and soft caps. Hard cap = 1s SBs without eradication/bl. Soft cap is the required amount of haste needed to cast 1s UAs and Haunts. You need 38,7%, 1269 haste rating from gear if you have all raid buffs and that is unreachable with haste gear sets. Sure you can get less than 1s ua casts with eradication or BL but still a big amount of our damage comes from SB, so the dps value of haste is still high.
Black magic is theoretically very very slightly better than 63 sp, but I still prefer to use sp enchant because I hate proc RNG.
