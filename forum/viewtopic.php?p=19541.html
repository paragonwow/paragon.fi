What are your thoughts on 5.2 Ele sham viability?

Thanks to the upcoming buffs and those few perks that ele has, I think it's decent enough to be brought on board for fights that are either single target or cleave orientated.

What stat priorities do you think will be the strongest?

I'll be running a mastery heavy build, but I suggest you sim your own stat weights and decide accordingly.

Finally, do you think EM and PE will be able to compete with EoE and EB?

I won't be using EM due to the issues that ele has with haste, but I'm definitely looking forward to toy around with PE.

As a side note, I think PE is highly overlooked as a talent and has numerous uses that are not included in the maths, for instance, it 1) stops your fire ele's roaming when you switch targets 2) significantly increases your burst (hello burn phases) 3) can save your life with the 20% damage reduction 4) simplifies your rotation 5) further enhances your mobility.
On top of that it has a few cool features that can be of great benefit which I won't be able to share just now ;-)
