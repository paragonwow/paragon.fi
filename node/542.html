Arena Pass: Week 4 Ladder

Submitted by xenophics on Mon, 13/06/2011 - 18:59

Another week has passed and it's again time to take a look at how things are going at Tournament realm. As the tournament has now entered locked teams state, the rankings of the teams start to matter in a completely different level. In the process, a lot of teams have completely vanished, as many were created just to test out different class combinations see and what works for each team the best. So now we have the players and the teams and we'll see who'll end up being the number one.

Sadly it seems that my and arx's bet is over as both of the teams we rooted for have vanished from first page (and I'm not interested in digging through all the pages). Just before the teams got locked last week "winter is coming" was higher than shadowlulz, so I guess I just lost the bet. Damn you arx, now I'll have to pay for our food for 1 week.

 

At the moment, most of the teams have played 50-70 games so we can expect a lot to happen when the top rated teams meet each other more in the games. At the moment the ladder looks like this:

 

The team that has kept the number one place for many weeks (man check this guys name) has finally had to surrender and make way for a new leader. "uKnoWhatITisZ" (my eyes bleed with this grammar) has taken the lead with 2531 rating.

 

The second team at the moment seems to be the good old "Rshaman take zero skill" with 2521 rating, who has been hanging in the top 10 all this time. Glad to see these guys finally made it to the top 3.

 

You can make it to the very top of the ladder with only 34 games, as "FLYIN LIONS" shows with 2506 rating. Again, good to see a team without a lock and a resto shaman in it, tho it's not like rmp is that fresh or new in any way.
