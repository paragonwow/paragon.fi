Arena Pass tournament of 2011!

Submitted by zYN on Mon, 23/05/2011 - 04:45

The thundering drums of battle echo throughout the land of Azeroth, calling upon warriors to test their mettle on the unforgiving sands of the arena. Blood will be spilled upon the earth. When the dust settles, will you be remembered, or will you fade into oblivion? Will you become the true Champions?

It's here again. The Arena Pass tournament.

The best PvP players from all around the world are invited to battle each other on the arena. Pretty much like the story in every fighting game, ever. This time, though, things are different. We will be there destroying the nerds. We are the Dural. We are the Nightmare. We are the Seth. Crushing our enemies, seeing them driven before us - to hear the lamentation of their moms.

Check more about the Arena Pass 2011 at the official site!

 

We will also be participating in the Arena Pass 2011: Community Challenge! We are also looking some of you guys to help us out in some of the challenges. More info on that coming soon!
