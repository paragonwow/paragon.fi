Nize does China - part I

Submitted by Nize on Tue, 19/04/2011 - 12:33

First entry, better late than never! I had some thoughts written up before our trip but didn't manage to finish it since I was doing everything pretty much at the last minute. Also the fact that I wasn't feeling particularly well during the morning of the day we were leaving might have had something to do with it (yeah it truly was a great idea to get drunk the night before).

So for the folks who don't know me I guess an introduction is in order. I'm Nikke 23-year-old dude living in Helsinki Finland and play a DK in Paragon with the character named Nize. I'm currently studying IT and on my free time I try to keep myself in some sort of shape by casually jogging or working out.

Probably doesn't come as a huge surprise that like the rest of us I'm also very excited about this trip. I feel very priviledged to get this opportunity and I'm confident that it's going to be an experience to remember for a long time. I've done a fair share of travelling but never had the chance to visit China earlier. Looking forward to meeting all the players from the Chinese teams aswell as our fellow Europeans from For The Horde, I'm sure it'll be a blast!
