Paragon's "first day" progress

Submitted by xenophics on Wed, 08/12/2010 - 06:39

So the first "day" of Cataclysm is finally over for Paragon after over 31 hours of more or less serious playing. As the instances will reset in 15 minutes from the moment I'm writing this news post, we rushed to the instances to get as much out of em as possible.

 

We raided around 9 hours and managed to get the following bosses down on 25-man normal mode: Conclaive of the Four Winds, Omnitron Defense System, Magmaw, Atramedes, Maloriak, Halfus Wyrmbreaker and Valiona and Theralion (7/12).
