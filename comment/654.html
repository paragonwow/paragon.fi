Merry Christmas to everybody

Submitted by xenophics on Thu, 23/12/2010 - 00:47

It's been one hell of a year for Paragon and we are happy we have been able to share it with you guys. We'll continue as we've done so far; enjoying the game and playing as good as possible. Now we are off to a short Christmas break to spend time with our families, eat great food, and take our minds off from World of Warcraft for a while (we'll of course continue doing dailies!).

 

We'd like to wish all our readers Merry Christmas and happy new year. Enjoy the holidays, have fun, and try to relax - that's what we'll do. I (xeno) would like to thank the whole guild for the time we've had together, see you in a couple of days!
