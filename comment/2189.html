The Raid Observer: Week 2, Day 2 Update and A Special Interview!

Submitted by Ladan on Thu, 08/12/2011 - 18:38

Today's update is a bit different, as I've decided my fingers are tired from so much typing and I thought you might like to listen to me giving an update. And guess what? I've also got two very special guests who agreed to be interviewed!

 

 

Heroics Continue!

So I thought I'd do today's update in audio format, but as listening to me for 20 minutes might put you all into a comatose state, I asked two members of Paragon along to check in about how things are going, what they think about the recent LFR-related events, and how they feel the race is unfolding at this very early stage. So a special thanks to Synti and xenophics who bravely agreed to talk to me! We explore the 10-man and 25-man races, the fact that the ship is not such a "loot ship" after all, and what they feel about the whole "exploit" issue.

 

Before I get to that, however, please do go and vote in my latest poll on my research blog, where I'm asking you to weigh in on the LFR exploit ban, http://www.raidingresearch.co.uk.

 

Anyway, enjoy the interview! If you like this, I'll see if I can corner more members of Paragon and other guilds for you. :)
