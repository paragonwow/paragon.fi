The new legendary by SteelSeries

Submitted by arx on Fri, 12/08/2011 - 09:32

SteelSeries unveiled their World of Warcraft MMO Gaming Mouse Legendary Edition (phew) a few days ago. Some of our guys have actually been testing the prototypes and giving feedback on the mouse and liked the feel of it.

We'll wait until we get our hands on the shipped product to comment more in-depth, but since xeno has been using the prototype for a while, I asked her for a short comment on her thoughts about the mouse:

To me, the new Legendary mouse is a much longed-for upgrade to the older version. It's a lot lighter, smaller, and more comfortable to play with. All in all I'll be happy to play with it, knowing that we were also able to give feedback during the development process.

 

 

More information about the Legendary is available at the SteelSeries website:

http://steelseries.com/products/games/world-of-warcraft/world-of-warcraft-mmo-mouse-legendary-edition
